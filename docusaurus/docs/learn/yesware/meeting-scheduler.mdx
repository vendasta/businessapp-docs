Yesware's Meeting Scheduler lets prospects book meetings directly from your calendar. Learn which plans have unlimited team meeting links, how to remove branding, working hours, and troubleshooting.

:::tip Unlimited meeting links
Premium, Enterprise, and Pro subscriptions have unlimited team meeting links. Free Forever does not.
:::

---

<div className="principle-area"><span className="principle-area__icon">📅</span>Subscription and Meeting Links</div>

### Unlimited Team Meeting Links

> **Premium, Enterprise, and Pro** subscriptions have an unlimited number of team meeting links. Free Forever does not.

### Gmail Integrations

> Gmail users **cannot** utilize both Google Meet and Microsoft Teams integrations at the same time. Choose one.

---

<div className="principle-area"><span className="principle-area__icon">🏷️</span>Removing Yesware Branding</div>

To remove Yesware branding from a user's meeting scheduler booking page:

1. Check the user's subscription
2. Open a Jira ticket
3. View the user's account
4. Confirm the change on the user's end

---

<div className="principle-area"><span className="principle-area__icon">🔗</span>Meeting Type URL and Save Button</div>

### Changing Meeting Type URL

> **Changing the meeting type URL will break the previous meeting scheduler link.** Recipients with the old link will no longer be able to book.

### Save Button Greyed Out

> If the Save meeting type button is greyed out, the most likely cause is that the user **didn't fill out the direct URL to meeting option**. Other required fields may also be missing.

---

<div className="principle-area"><span className="principle-area__icon">⏰</span>Working Hours and Calendar</div>

### Calendar Sync

> Users **can** choose which calendar they want synced with their meeting scheduler.

### Working Hours

> The meeting scheduler only allows recipients to book during a user's defined working hours. Edit these in:
- **Yesware > Meeting Scheduler > Edit settings**
- **Yesware > Meeting Scheduler > Edit button on specific meeting type**

---

<div className="principle-area"><span className="principle-area__icon">✉️</span>Confirmation Notifications</div>

> When a meeting is booked, **both the recipient and the invitee** receive confirmation notifications.
