Get started with Yesware. Learn installation, how tracking works, sidebar differences between Outlook and Gmail, and key preferences. Understand what must be true for emails to track.

:::tip Installation
Gmail: Install from Google Web Store. Outlook: One install for desktop covers web; no reinstall needed for web.
:::

---

<div className="principle-area"><span className="principle-area__icon">📧</span>Email Tracking Requirements</div>

### For Outlook Add-in

> For an email to track: (1) **Yesware Compose / New Email sidebar must be opened**, (2) **Track email Opens must be toggled to ON**. The email does not need a tracked link or attachment, or the word "Yesware."

### What Yesware Tracks

> **Individually sent emails** and **emails sent through campaigns**. Yesware does **not** track if recipients are BCC'd or CC'd.

### Attachments

> Yesware **cannot** track attachments added through the regular Outlook or Gmail attachment buttons. Use Yesware's attachment flow for tracking.

---

<div className="principle-area"><span className="principle-area__icon">📱</span>Sidebars</div>

### Count

> **Outlook: 2 sidebars. Gmail: 1 sidebar.**

### What Displays (Outlook Inbox Sidebar)

> **Recent engagement events** (Opens, Link clicks, etc.)—not all tracked emails regardless of engagement, or only open events.

---

<div className="principle-area"><span className="principle-area__icon">💻</span>Cross-Device and Installation</div>

### Gmail

> Gmail users **cannot** seamlessly use Yesware between different computers without taking additional actions. Re-authentication or setup may be needed per device.

### Outlook

> Outlook users **can** seamlessly use Yesware between different computers without additional actions. One install for desktop Outlook also works for web—no reinstall needed for web version.

### Gmail Installation

> Install Yesware for Gmail from the **Google Web Store**—not from a yesware.com download or within Gmail inbox.

---

<div className="principle-area"><span className="principle-area__icon">⚙️</span>Preferences and Integrations</div>

### Toggle Options

> Users can toggle ON or OFF: **Track Opens**, **Track Links**, **Colleague Events**. Meeting scheduler is configured separately.

### Salesforce (Outlook)

> Enable/disable via: **Yesware Inbox sidebar > Menu > Integrations > Turn ON & Link / Turn OFF & Unlink**. Not via Menu > Salesforce or sign out/sign in.
