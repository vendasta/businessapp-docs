Yesware reports help you analyze campaigns, templates, and engagement. Learn which reports have 30-day limits, which subscriptions have access, how often data updates, and what filters are available.

:::tip Data refresh
Reporting data is updated every 1–2 hours. Not daily, weekly, or instantly.
:::

---

<div className="principle-area"><span className="principle-area__icon">📅</span>Reporting Periods</div>

### 30-Day or Less Restriction

> **Recipient engagement** and **Link click engagement** reports are restricted to 30 days or less. Templates by templates and Personal tracking report are not restricted the same way.

### 365-Day Limit

> Unless a report is restricted to 30 days, the reporting period limit for all other reports is **365 days**—not 180, 90, or 2 years.

---

<div className="principle-area"><span className="principle-area__icon">📊</span>Subscription Access</div>

### Who Has Reports

> **Enterprise, Premium, and Pro** have access to Yesware reports. **Free Forever** does not.

### Enterprise/Premium Only

> **Campaigns by user** reports are present only with Enterprise or Premium. Templates by user, Personal tracking, Recipient engagement, and Link click engagement may be available on other plans.

---

<div className="principle-area"><span className="principle-area__icon">📤</span>Export and Data Refresh</div>

### Personal Tracking Report Export

> The personal tracking report **cannot** be exported in CSV format. It would not be exported as Excel either.

### Data Update Frequency

> Reporting data is updated every **1–2 hours**—not every 24 hours, weekly, 48 hours, or instantly.

---

<div className="principle-area"><span className="principle-area__icon">🔍</span>Reporting Filters</div>

Besides filtering for teams and reporting periods:

- **Include colleague emails** — Add teammate data to the view
- **Exclude own data** — Remove your own emails from the report
- **Exclude campaigns and templates without activity** — Focus on active content
