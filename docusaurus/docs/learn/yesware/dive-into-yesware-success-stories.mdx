Dive into two success stories that showcase the value of deeply embedding automation into the sales outreach process. Learn how FullContact increased pipeline and customer retention, and how OpFocus achieved a 462% increase in sales prospecting activities.

:::tip Duration
~20 minutes
:::

---

<div className="principle-area"><span className="principle-area__icon">📊</span>FullContact: Increase Pipeline and Customer Retention</div>

**Company:** FullContact — Identity resolution platform helping companies identify consumers in real-time (privacy-safe). **Stack:** Outlook, Salesforce, Yesware. **Tools:** Email Tracking, Templates, Campaigns, Salesforce Integration, Reporting & Analytics.

### Results

- **25% increase** in meetings booked
- **100% increase** in sales rep activity

### Goals

1. Reach prospects and improve customer engagement in an increasingly competitive, noisy world
2. Support sales and customer success teams throughout the sales and renewal cycle
3. Drive more meaningful conversations and accelerate deals

---

<div className="principle-area"><span className="principle-area__icon">⚡</span>Supercharging Team Activity</div>

**Problem:** FullContact's team used templates in Yesware but the process wasn't formalized or structured.

**Action:** Jake Cohen (Director of Product Marketing) worked with the team to identify needed templates—demo request responses, discovery call recaps, follow-ups for prospects who went dark. They built consistent messaging for each use case collaboratively.

**Result:** Saved several hours per week. Team activity doubled from 25 to 50+ activities per day. Customer success efficiency increased 20%. Pipeline increased 5–10% per campaign on average. Sales reps could focus on building genuine relationships while Yesware handled admin work.

---

<div className="principle-area"><span className="principle-area__icon">👁️</span>Turning Opaque Conversations Transparent</div>

**Problem:** Many salespeople hit send on a proposal and have no idea what happens next—junk folder, forwarded to boss, or ignored. They're in the dark.

**Action:** Yesware integrates into every customer and prospect conversation. FullContact's people track every message and activity. When someone opens a proposal or watches a demo recording, the salesperson receives a real-time alert.

**Result:** Knowing what prospects are reading helps salespeople get in front of objections and pre-research questions. They can be proactive and take the conversation to prospects instead of waiting. Tracking shapes both original outreach and follow-up strategy—reducing time spent waiting for a response.

---

<div className="principle-area"><span className="principle-area__icon">🚀</span>OpFocus: Increase Your Sales Prospecting Activities</div>

**Company:** OpFocus — Business consulting for growth-minded SaaS companies (strategic consulting, systems administration, custom development). **Stack:** Outlook, Salesforce, Yesware. **Tools:** Email Tracking, Templates, Campaigns, Reporting & Analytics.

### Results

- **462% increase** in sales prospecting activities
- **46% increase** in sales bookings

### Goals

1. Standardize success by analyzing templates and sharing best-performing assets
2. Stop relying on gut feel—analyze every email, link, and attachment
3. Automate: tracking, open rate, reply rate

---

<div className="principle-area"><span className="principle-area__icon">📈</span>Standardizing Success</div>

**Problem:** The sales world is always evolving. A strategy working yesterday isn't guaranteed today. OpFocus cultivated a culture of learning and improvement.

**Action:** Using Yesware, OpFocus turned dialogue optimization into a process. Reps analyzed their templates and shared the best-performing assets with colleagues.

**Result:** Sales directors compared strategies, messaging tactics, and content assets. By standardizing what worked, they scaled individual strengths across the entire team. Companies that adopt sales team collaboration grow **2.5x more** than those that don't. Content sharing correlates with account growth: 75–100% sharing ≈ 41% account growth.

---

<div className="principle-area"><span className="principle-area__icon">🪟</span>A Single Pane of Glass</div>

**Problem:** The previous tool didn't reliably sync data to CRM. Reps couldn't trust the information. Clunky campaign management slowed reps. Opaque reporting disguised underperforming campaigns.

**Action:** Yesware went live. Instead of gut feel and manual analysis, the new toolkit analyzed every email, link, and attachment.

**Result:** Reduced guesswork. Data-driven insights on a single pane of glass. Sales directors could check weekly activity breakdowns—see how reps divide time: prospecting to fill pipeline or spending time on existing deals?

---

<div className="principle-area"><span className="principle-area__icon">✅</span>Conclusion: Automation Impact</div>

Email automation is a non-negotiable tool for sales teams that want to optimize performance. Its impact affects virtually every step:

1. **Automates administrative work**
2. **Strengthens your brand**
3. **Helps you learn more about your customers**
4. **Allows for strategic messaging**
5. **Personalizes your outreach**

Design, execute, and analyze workflows and campaigns simultaneously to optimize outreach and reach the maximum number of people.
