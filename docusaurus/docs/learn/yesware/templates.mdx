Yesware templates let you send rich emails in a fraction of the time. Create, share, and personalize with merge fields. Learn how team sharing works, what integrates with merge fields, and tracking behavior.

:::tip Merge fields
Text fields and dropdown fields allow personalization. Salesforce integration works with merge fields.
:::

---

<div className="principle-area"><span className="principle-area__icon">📝</span>Merge Fields</div>

### Text Fields

> Act as placeholders or fill-in-the-blanks. You fill them in manually while crafting emails.

### Dropdown Fields

> Create a predetermined list to choose from—e.g., days of the week for quick selection.

### Integration

> **Salesforce integration** works with merge fields in templates. Meeting Scheduler and Prospector do not populate merge fields the same way.

---

<div className="principle-area"><span className="principle-area__icon">👥</span>Team Sharing</div>

When a team leader shares a template with the team, team members **cannot edit the original**. They **can duplicate and edit the copy**. They cannot edit the shared template's email content, subject line, or title directly.

---

<div className="principle-area"><span className="principle-area__icon">🔗</span>Link Tracking and Custom Fonts</div>

### Links

> Links included in templates do **not** always be tracked by default. Tracking must be enabled or configured where applicable.

### Custom Fonts

> There is **no** way to import custom fonts into the templates tool. Use available system fonts.
