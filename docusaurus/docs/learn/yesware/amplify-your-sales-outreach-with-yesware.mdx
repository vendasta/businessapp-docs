<div className="principle-area"><span className="principle-area__icon">🤖</span>Automate: Campaigns</div>

Craft personalized, multi-touch messages to a variety of prospects. Create and manage right from your inbox.

### Main Features

- **Multi-channel** — Gmail: 6 touch types (automated email, manual email, phone call, custom task, etc.)
- **Anti-spam** — Emails sent from your Outlook or Gmail server—not a 3rd-party server. Land in inbox, not spam.
- **Automated follow-up** — Auto-reminders for manual touches. Scale outreach.

### Best Practice

> On average, it takes at least **5 follow-ups** after initial contact before a customer says yes. Schedule multiple touchpoints, with a few days between each email.

<div className="principle-area"><span className="principle-area__icon">📊</span>Analyze: Reporting</div>

Yesware's reporting proves which campaigns, cadences, and follow-up counts drive the best results. **Customizable** (period, team, metrics), **Personal and team** (open, reply, connect rates for shared content), **Downloadable** (CSVs for emails and presentations).

### Engagement Reports

> Use **Engagement Reports** to view insights into your prospects' email interactions—not Email Activity or Template Reports alone.
