Yesware Admin covers subscription management, user profiles, license transfers, impersonation, and troubleshooting. Learn where to find billing info, how to remove users, and how to diagnose Salesforce and tracking issues.

:::tip Subscription info
Admin > User profile > Payment account — plan type, billing cycle, etc.
:::

---

<div className="principle-area"><span className="principle-area__icon">💳</span>Subscription and Billing</div>

### Finding Subscription Info

> **Admin > User profile > Payment account** — Plan type, billing cycle, and related info. Not: Impersonate, Edit, or User profile alone.

### Finding Unknown Subscription

> If a user doesn't remember the email for a subscription they want to cancel: (1) Search for accounts associated with the requester's name, (2) Have Billing search Zuora via the credit card that was charged. Not: search by last charge date only—there may be no other way without email.

### Seat Licenses

> Support **can** exceed the number of available seat licenses when adding users to a payment account—in certain cases. Verify with internal policies.

---

<div className="principle-area"><span className="principle-area__icon">🔄</span>License Transfer and Removing Users</div>

### Why Remove Might Fail

> A user/Support cannot remove an email from a payment account if **the email is the last remaining Leader on a Team**. Not: they're a manager, or they have templates.

### Team Ownership Transfer

> Support **can** transfer ownership of Teams between different payment accounts.

---

<div className="principle-area"><span className="principle-area__icon">👤</span>Impersonation</div>

> Changes Support makes while impersonating users **all apply** on the user's end. This includes campaigns, meeting scheduler, and other tools. All changes are correct/synced.

---

<div className="principle-area"><span className="principle-area__icon">🔧</span>Technical Troubleshooting</div>

### Delete Cached Authentication Token

> **Admin > User profile > Scroll to "More" option > Authentication Status > Choose the token to delete > Delete.** Not: Edit > Set authentication clear, or Payment account > More.

### Salesforce Integration Issues

> To check for Salesforce issues: **Admin > User profile > Scroll to "CRM settings" > CRM service Config > Show > Tasks > Failed Tasks.** Not: Impersonate, Tracking Report, or verifying login URL only.

### Tracking Disabled But Still Tracking

> If a user disabled tracking but emails still track, the cause is likely: **the account has the Forced tracking policy enabled**. Not: user still has Yesware installed, or emails contain tracked links.

---

<div className="principle-area"><span className="principle-area__icon">👥</span>Account Owner Permissions</div>

> Account owner sets permissions for individual users: **app.yesware.com > Settings > Plans & Usage > Users & teams > Click on the individual(s) > Edit > Select permissions.** Not: Team permissions > Manage members > Edit, or Teams > Edit permissions.
