Yesware tracking lets you monitor engagement—opens, link clicks, and more. Learn how the personal tracking report works, what details you can see, why opens might not record, and how forwarded emails are tracked.

:::tip Reporting
Yesware has the same reporting options for Outlook and Gmail users—no separate views.
:::

---

<div className="principle-area"><span className="principle-area__icon">📊</span>Personal Tracking Report</div>

### Functions

> View a list of all tracked emails and all associated engagement events. Test tracking functionality to verify successful tracking. Not primarily for: overall open rate percentages or quick link click rankings.

### Reporting Parity

> Yesware has **the same** reporting options for Outlook and Gmail users—no separate views.

---

<div className="principle-area"><span className="principle-area__icon">📍</span>Engagement Details</div>

### What You Can See

> If available: **geolocation** of the engagement event, **device information**. Not typically: whether the recipient has ever opened any of your emails in the past, or if the email landed in Junk/Spam.

### Location Discrepancy

> If the location listed doesn't match where you know the recipient resides, the cause is likely: **the recipient is using a device with security measures and/or a VPN**. Not: they were in a different location, used a different domain, or you forgot to enable location tracking.

---

<div className="principle-area"><span className="principle-area__icon">❌</span>Why an Open Might Not Record</div>

> The most likely cause: **the recipient has images disabled**, which prevents the Yesware tracking pixel from functioning. Not: they opened and closed within one minute, opened on phone, or you forgot a tracked link.

---

<div className="principle-area"><span className="principle-area__icon">✅</span>How Support Verifies Tracking</div>

> Support verifies tracking by: (1) **Tracking pixel is present** in test emails sent to Support, (2) **Emails are listed** on the Personal Tracking Report. Not solely: viewing user profile or user confirmation.

### To Confirm Pixel in Email

> Have the user send a test tracked email > Open the email > Click email details (three dots beside reply) > Show Original > Ctrl/Cmd+F and search for **"GIF"** > Confirm search results. Not: search for "JPG" or "tracking pixel."

---

<div className="principle-area"><span className="principle-area__icon">↗️</span>Forwarded Emails</div>

> Forwarded tracked emails will have **all engagement events listed under the primary/initial recipient**, regardless of whether the events were performed by another recipient. Yesware does not track new recipients separately for forwarded emails.
