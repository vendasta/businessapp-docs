Yesware campaigns let you craft personalized, multi-touch messages to prospects right from your inbox. Learn recipient limits, touch types for Gmail and Outlook, merge field formatting, and troubleshooting.

:::tip Touch types
Both Gmail and Outlook users have access to Automated email and Phone call. Gmail also has Manual email, LinkedIn Mail, and Custom task.
:::

---

<div className="principle-area"><span className="principle-area__icon">👥</span>Campaign Limits and Setup</div>

### Recipient Limit

> You can add up to **900 recipients** at a single time when building a campaign.

### Outlook Signatures

> Yesware does **not** automatically import Outlook email signatures into the campaign feature. You'll need to add signatures manually if desired.

### Campaign Failed to Start

> If you get "your campaign failed to start," the most likely issue is an **incorrectly formatted .csv** file. Check column headers, delimiters, and data format.

---

<div className="principle-area"><span className="principle-area__icon">✉️</span>Touch Types</div>

### Gmail and Outlook (Both)

- **Automated email** — Scheduled, automated messages
- **Phone call** — Manual touch type for calling

### Gmail Only

- Manual email, LinkedIn Mail, Custom task

### Adding Touches After Launch

> If you launch a campaign with two touches and then add two more, recipients would **not** automatically receive the new touches. Plan your touches before launching.

---

<div className="principle-area"><span className="principle-area__icon">🔗</span>Merge Fields</div>

Merge fields pull data from your prospect list. Format: `{!Header Name}` — use the exact header from your CSV. Example: For a header "First Name", use `{!First Name}`. Case-sensitive.

---

<div className="principle-area"><span className="principle-area__icon">📋</span>Campaign Dashboard and Manual Touches</div>

### Visibility

> Only the **user that launched the campaign** has visibility into engagement metrics from the campaign dashboard—not all members, and not only account owners.

### Where to Complete Manual Touches (Gmail)

> **Yesware sidebar > Campaign 'To-do List' tab** — not within the campaigns tab, compose window, or app.yesware.com.
