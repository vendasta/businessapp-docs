Social Marketing helps you stay competitive in the fast-paced digital world by engaging consumers on social media. Respond to customers easily, generate quality leads, and collaborate seamlessly with clients and co-workers—all from one dashboard.

:::tip Key capabilities
Connect accounts, draft and schedule posts, manage a content calendar, use Clickable.bio for shoppable Instagram, respond to customer posts, discover content from RSS feeds, track performance with Insights, and create content at scale with AI.
:::

---

<div className="principle-area"><span className="principle-area__icon">🔗</span>Getting Started</div>

Connect your social accounts before posting. You can draft and schedule content, explore the Overview page, and leverage the calendar to plan ahead.

### Connecting Accounts

> Social Marketing &gt; Settings &gt; Connect Accounts. Click the **+** next to the social network you want to connect, log in, and follow the prompts. Networks usually connect the account you're already logged in as—so when connecting on behalf of a customer, use an incognito or private browsing session.

### Overview Page

> The Overview page shows your connected accounts, recent posts, and quick access to My Posts, the calendar, and key features. Explore each section to manage your social presence.

### Content Calendar

> Draft and schedule posts in the calendar. Plan your content without posts going live before they're finalized. Schedule across networks and view everything in one place.

### Clickable.bio

> Clickable.bio transforms Instagram posts into a shoppable gallery of images, turning social fans into paying customers. Available free on Social Marketing Pro. Set up in Social Marketing and compose Instagram posts with links.

<div className="principle-area"><span className="principle-area__icon">📝</span>Posts and Content</div>

### Customer Posts

> Easily access the posts your customers are commenting on. Respond without leaving the dashboard. Filter by network and status to stay on top of engagement.

### Content from RSS Feeds

> Not sure what to post? Social Marketing's content feature finds interesting and relevant content from RSS feeds. Develop targeted content that keeps customers engaged and interested.

<div className="principle-area"><span className="principle-area__icon">📊</span>Insights &amp; Settings</div>

### Insights

> See how your posts are performing on all networks. Track reactions, comments, shares, and engagement. Discover how your links are performing and how often they're shared.

### Settings

> Connect different accounts, add or edit quick responses for social posts to expedite replies, and reconnect expired social connections from the Social Marketing Admin Dashboard.

---

<div className="principle-area"><span className="principle-area__icon">🤖</span>Create with AI</div>

Customize your AI tools to scale content creation in Social Marketing.

### AI Common Instructions

> Social Marketing &gt; Settings &gt; AI Settings. Add free-form text to guide the AI when generating social posts from the composer. Complete sentences work best— avoid subheadings, titles, or bullets in the box. This improves brand consistency, lowers mistakes, and boosts efficiency.

### Bulk Create Social Posts with AI

> Craft entire campaigns in minutes. Generate text and imagery for multiple posts and channels from a single AI prompt. Provide a prompt and image keyword, select networks, post quantity, length, and tone. Review and customize by account, then schedule or save as drafts.

### AI Chat Editor

> Converse with the AI webchat to refine messaging tone and improve social posts without adjusting individual settings or creating entirely new prompts.
