Social Marketing gives you the ability to easily respond to customers, generate quality leads, and collaborate with clients and co-workers. Draft and schedule posts, manage customer engagement, track performance, and create content at scale—all in one place.

:::tip Key capabilities
Connect accounts, manage Customer Posts and Content, plan with the calendar, boost Instagram with Clickable.bio, use Insights and Settings, and create with AI (Common Instructions, Bulk Create, Chat Editor).
:::

---

<div className="principle-area"><span className="principle-area__icon">🔗</span>Connect Your Social Media Accounts</div>

Social Marketing &gt; Settings &gt; Connect Accounts. From the Connect Accounts tab, click the **+** next to the social network you want to connect, log in, and follow the prompts.

### Accounts You Can Connect

> Facebook, X (Twitter), TikTok, Instagram, LinkedIn, and Google Business Profile. Pinterest is not listed among connectable accounts.

### Connecting on Behalf of a Customer

> Social networks generally try to connect the account you're already logged in as. When connecting on behalf of a customer, use an incognito or private browsing session. For platform-specific walkthroughs, visit the Vendasta Resource Centre.

---

<div className="principle-area"><span className="principle-area__icon">📝</span>Customer Posts and Content</div>

### Customer Posts

> Easily access the posts your customers are commenting on. Respond without leaving the dashboard. Filter by network and status.

### Recent Posts

> See all posts across your connected social accounts—whether they were posted by Social Marketing or not. Host and review content all in one place.

### Content (Interesting Content)

> Not sure what to post? Social Marketing's content feature finds interesting and relevant content from RSS feeds. Find and customize targeted content to keep customers engaged.

---

<div className="principle-area"><span className="principle-area__icon">📅</span>Calendar and Clickable.bio</div>

### Content Calendar

> Draft and schedule posts in the calendar. Plan content without posts going live before they're finalized. Schedule across networks and view everything in one place.

### Clickable.bio

> Transform your Instagram posts into a shoppable gallery of images—turn social fans into paying customers. Available free on Social Marketing Pro.

---

<div className="principle-area"><span className="principle-area__icon">📊</span>Insights and Settings</div>

### Insights

> See how your posts are performing on all networks. Track reactions, comments, shares, and engagement. Discover how your links are performing and how often they're shared. Use **Post Performance** to see your most popular posts across networks (e.g., during a specific month like July).

### Settings

> Connect different accounts. Add, edit, and post quick responses to social posts using pre-designed template responses to expedite the process. Reconnect expired social connections from the Social Marketing Admin Dashboard.

<div className="principle-area"><span className="principle-area__icon">🤖</span>Create with AI</div>

### AI Common Instructions

> Social Marketing &gt; Settings &gt; AI Settings. Add free-form text to guide the AI when generating social posts from the composer. Use complete sentences—avoid subheadings, titles, or bullets. This keeps brand consistency intact and boosts efficiency.

### Bulk Create Social Posts with AI

> Craft entire campaigns in minutes. Provide a prompt and image keyword. Select networks, post quantity, length, and tone. Review and customize by account. Schedule or save as drafts.

### AI Chat Editor

> Converse with the AI webchat to refine messaging tone and improve posts without adjusting individual settings or creating entirely new prompts.
