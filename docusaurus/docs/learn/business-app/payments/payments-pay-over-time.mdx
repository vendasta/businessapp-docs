Pay Over Time, powered by Wisetack, lets businesses offer flexible financing to their customers on invoices between $500 and $25,000 — while still getting paid in full upfront. It's a powerful tool for winning higher-ticket jobs and removing the "I can't afford it right now" barrier for customers.

:::tip
The business collects the full invoice amount upfront. Wisetack handles collecting installment payments from the customer. That distinction is key when explaining the feature to skeptical business owners.
:::

## <div className="principle-area"><span className="principle-area__icon">💡</span>What is Pay Over Time?</div>

> **Core concept** Pay Over Time allows businesses to offer installment-based payment to customers on invoices between $500 and $25,000. The business receives the full invoice amount upfront from Wisetack, then Wisetack collects from the customer over time.

> **Transaction fee** There is a 3.9% transaction fee for the business on Pay Over Time transactions.

> **Why it matters** Businesses can win more customers, sell higher-priced services, and stop losing sales because customers can't afford to pay a large bill all at once.

<div className="in-practice">

**In Practice:** When pitching Pay Over Time, lead with the business benefit: "You still get paid for the full job immediately. Your customer just has the option to pay in installments — and Wisetack handles all of that for you." That reframe eliminates most of the initial hesitation.

</div>

---

## <div className="principle-area"><span className="principle-area__icon">✅</span>Business Approval Process</div>

> **Application required** Businesses must apply and be approved by Wisetack before Pay Over Time becomes available on their invoices.

> **Approval criteria** Wisetack considers annual revenue ($200K or more), business history (1+ year in operation), and customer experience metrics including online reviews.

> **Once approved** Pay Over Time automatically appears on all invoices over $500. No additional setup is required per invoice.

<div className="in-practice">

**In Practice:** Frame the approval criteria positively — if a business has been around for a year and is actively collecting reviews, they are likely a strong candidate. Use the criteria as a checklist with the customer, not a barrier.

</div>

---

## <div className="principle-area"><span className="principle-area__icon">👤</span>End-Customer Experience</div>

> **Application flow** When a customer selects Pay Over Time on an invoice, they complete a short application. Wisetack reviews it using a soft credit pull (no impact to credit score, minimum FICO 540) and returns a decision 24/7.

> **Approval rate** 90% of end-customer applications are approved. Customers choose from up to 6 payment options based on the invoice amount and their eligibility — with APRs from 0% to 29.9% and terms from 3 to 60 months.

> **Job completion flow** On the final screen, customers choose either "JOB IS DONE - RELEASE PAYMENT" (invoice marked paid, funds released to the business) or "JOB ISN'T DONE - CONFIRM LATER" (reminders sent until they confirm).

<div className="in-practice">

**In Practice:** For high-ticket jobs, advise businesses to send the invoice before work begins so the customer can apply and get approved in advance. This avoids the situation where the job is already done but the customer gets declined by Wisetack.

</div>

---

## <div className="principle-area"><span className="principle-area__icon">💸</span>Payouts &amp; Refunds</div>

> **Payout timing** When a customer marks the job as complete, funds are released to the business the next business day. It then takes an additional 1–3 business days to deposit depending on the bank.

> **Payout notifications** Wisetack sends its own payout email notifications directly to the business.

> **Refunds** If a refund is needed, the business or customer contacts Wisetack support at support@wisetack.com or 1-833-927-0333. Wisetack verifies the amounts with both parties and adjusts the loan balance accordingly.

<div className="in-practice">

**In Practice:** Businesses sometimes worry about what happens if a dispute arises. Reassure them that Wisetack manages the dispute process directly — the business just needs to verify the job details with Wisetack when contacted.

</div>

---

## <div className="principle-area"><span className="principle-area__icon">⚠️</span>When It's Not a Good Fit</div>

> **Low-ticket businesses** If a business's invoices never exceed $500, Pay Over Time won't trigger — so there's no value in enabling it.

> **Revenue or tenure below threshold** Businesses with less than $200K annual revenue or less than 1 year in operation won't qualify for Wisetack approval.

> **Canadian businesses** Wisetack does not support Canadian currency, and SMS delivery to Canadian numbers is unreliable — affecting the invoice delivery flow.

> **Partner-integrated businesses** Businesses using Housecall Pro, Jobber, or Shopware should not be pitched on Payments. These partners have requested this, and honoring the request protects those relationships.

<div className="in-practice">

**In Practice:** Use the "not a good fit" criteria early in discovery — not as a rejection, but to qualify quickly and spend your time where Pay Over Time will actually land. A business that always charges under $500 is just not the right audience.

</div>
