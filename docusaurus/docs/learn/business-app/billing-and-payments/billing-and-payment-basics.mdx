<div className="principle-area"><span className="principle-area__icon">💳</span>Subscriptions &amp; Plans</div>

Vendasta's pricing is designed for businesses at every stage of growth. Your subscription gives you access to the platform, Marketplace, and tools to market, sell, bill, and fulfill.

### Starter

> Starting at $89/mo (billed at $1,068/yr). Get started with Vendasta and see how it can help you market, sell, bill, and fulfill.

### Professional

> Starting at $499/mo (billed at $5,988/yr). Build your brand with white-label software provided to your clients. Scale your team's ability to market, sell, bill, and fulfill.

### Premium

> Starting at $999/mo (billed at $11,988/yr). Powerful features for clients with multiple locations. An end-to-end commerce platform with the tools your team needs to work efficiently.

### Custom Enterprise

> Contact Vendasta to build a plan that best suits your organization's needs and get all the benefits elite partners experience every day.

### Snapshot Reports &amp; Products

> A certain number of free Snapshot Reports are included monthly in each subscription. Products in the Marketplace have unique wholesale pricing, but there is no cost to add them to your Store. You are only billed once you activate a product for a client, on a charge-per-activation basis.

<div className="principle-area"><span className="principle-area__icon">🏦</span>Set Up Billing</div>

As a new Vendasta partner, you'll be billed instantly (same day) for in-platform purchases. To activate any paid product, you need a credit card on file.

### Navigate to My Billing

> Partner Center &gt; Administration &gt; My Account &gt; My Billing. Note: there are three cards under Administration that deal with payments—only **My Billing** deals with your own payments. The other two pertain to your customer accounts.

### Add a Billing Contact

> Your billing contact receives purchase receipt emails. Receipts include a CSV attachment with a detailed breakdown of each purchase, including which of your customers the purchase was for.

### Add Your Credit Card

> Enter your credit card information in the My Billing card. Once added, you can activate products and services immediately.

<div className="principle-area"><span className="principle-area__icon">🧾</span>Manage Purchases</div>

The My Billing card gives you a full picture of your spending and makes it easy to track and download billing reports.

### View Purchases

> See a detailed list of all your purchases. Filter and organize by date, product, or account to find what you need quickly.

### Monthly Estimated Total

> View the estimated total costs for the current month at a glance, so you're never surprised by your bill.

### Download Billing Reports

> Download monthly billing reports directly from My Billing. The way you search, organize, and download your billing information is up to you.

<div className="principle-area"><span className="principle-area__icon">🔧</span>Vendasta Payments</div>

Set up Vendasta Payments (Merchant Services) to accept credit card payments from clients and send invoices directly from the platform.

### Connect Stripe

> Partner Center &gt; Administration &gt; Platform Settings &gt; Vendasta Payments. You'll set up a Stripe Connect account to begin accepting credit cards via invoices and the Shopping Cart. Stripe Connect is the payment platform integrated into Vendasta.

### Send Invoices

> Once connected, you can create and send invoices to clients—manually or automatically, on a monthly, yearly, or subscription-renewal schedule.

### Billing Automations

> To toggle on billing automations, navigate to: Partner Center &gt; Administration &gt; Default Billing Settings. Changing these settings only affects accounts created after you save—existing accounts retain their previous settings.
