A busy Inbox is a good problem to have — but only if you have the tools to manage it. Saved Replies and Snoozing help your team respond faster, stay consistent, and follow up at exactly the right time.

:::tip What you'll learn
Use Saved Replies to build a library of reusable messages with dynamic tags, and use Snooze to schedule a reminder to follow up on any conversation at the right moment.
:::

<div className="principle-area"><span className="principle-area__icon">💌</span>Saved Replies</div>

Saved Replies allow any team member to quickly find and send pre-written messages to leads and customers — saving time, ensuring accuracy, and keeping messaging consistent across the team.

### Sending a Saved Reply

> In any conversation view, tap the **➕** symbol on the left side and select **Use a Saved Reply**. A list of available saved replies appears — select one and the message text auto-populates in the message box. Edit it if needed before sending, or send as-is.

### Managing Saved Replies

> All saved replies are managed under **Settings &gt; Saved Replies**. Three replies come pre-loaded by default: **Appointment Reminder**, **Request Mobile Number**, and **Thanks for the Review**. Use the **pencil icon** to edit a reply or the **trash icon** to delete it.

### Creating New Saved Replies

> To create a new reply, select the **New** link in the upper right or the **Create a new reply** button in the lower right. Add a title and a message, then save it. The new reply immediately becomes available to all team members in conversations.

### Custom Tags (Variables)

> Saved replies support custom tags that act as variables, automatically resolving to real content when used in a conversation. Supported tags: **{{Customer First Name}}**, **{{Customer Full Name}}**, **{{Business Name}}**, **{{Business Street Address}}**, **{{Business Full Address}}**. In Settings, tags display as variable placeholders. In a live conversation, they resolve to the actual values.

<div className="principle-area"><span className="principle-area__icon">😴</span>Snoozing Conversations</div>

Snoozing lets you set a reminder to return to a conversation at a future date and time. When the snooze expires, the conversation jumps to the top of the Inbox automatically — so nothing gets forgotten.

### How to Snooze a Conversation

> Open a conversation, then tap the **menu in the upper right corner**. Select **Snooze**, then choose when to **Snooze Until**. Once snoozed, the Inbox preview confirms the snooze time.

### What Happens When It Wakes Up

> When the snooze time arrives, the conversation is bumped to the top of the Inbox. The conversation preview shows when it was originally snoozed. After a team member replies, the preview returns to normal — the snooze indicator disappears.

### Who Can Snooze

> All user roles — Members, Managers, and Admins — can snooze conversations. Anyone can see that a conversation is snoozed, but there is no record of who snoozed it. If knowing who snoozed is important, leave a note in the conversation before snoozing.

### Dismissing a Snooze

> There is no manual "unsnooze" option. The only way to dismiss a snooze is to reply to the contact — via a direct message, a payment request, or a review request. Once a reply is sent, the snooze is dismissed automatically.
