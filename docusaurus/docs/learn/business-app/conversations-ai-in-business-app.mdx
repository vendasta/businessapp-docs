<div className="principle-area"><span className="principle-area__icon">💬</span>Introduction to Conversations AI</div>

Conversations AI (previously Inbox Pro) consolidates communication into one convenient location in Business App—whether messages come from Google Business Messages, SMS, or webchat.

### One Inbox, All Channels

> All conversations are accessible from a single location in Business App, regardless of channel. Clients no longer need to check multiple platforms for incoming messages.

### Shared Team Inbox

> The shared team inbox ensures seamless communication within the team, enabling better collaboration and improved customer experience. Everyone on the team can see and respond to conversations.

### SMS Number

> US and Canada-based businesses are automatically assigned a local SMS number when Inbox Pro is activated—no manual registration required on the client side.

<div className="principle-area"><span className="principle-area__icon">🤖</span>AI Assistant</div>

The AI assistant inside Conversations AI has three primary goals that work around the clock for your clients.

### Answer Questions About the Business

> The AI assistant responds to questions using basic information from the business profile and its trained knowledge base. Clients don't have to be available for every inquiry.

### Capture Lead Contact Info

> The AI assistant collects visitor contact information (mobile or email) for ongoing conversation. Business App notifies the business of new leads via email so nothing slips through.

### Respond in 40+ Languages

> The AI assistant can reply in over 40 languages, including English, French, Spanish, and many more—so clients can serve customers in their preferred language automatically.

<div className="principle-area"><span className="principle-area__icon">🛠️</span>Getting Set Up</div>

Three ways to get Conversations AI working for you and your clients.

### Demo the AI Webchat Widget

> Any account's AI-assisted webchat is available to demo without any configuration. Navigate to Local SEO &gt; My Listing tab &gt; View My Listing to find the Web Chat Lead Capture Widget pop-up. Use it to demo Conversations AI to prospective clients on the spot.

### Add the Widget to Your Website

> Partner Center &gt; Inbox &gt; Inbox Settings. Configure the color, ensure the business profile information is correct, and add the shortcode to your website header. The widget will appear on your site, ready to capture leads and bring their information into Inbox.

### Fuel the AI with Knowledge

> Business App &gt; Settings &gt; Inbox Settings &gt; Web Chat Settings &gt; AI Assistant. Click any title to see what knowledge the AI has access to, or click 'Manage Knowledge' to add new knowledge sources. Write or paste any text information, then select it to make it accessible to the AI assistant.
