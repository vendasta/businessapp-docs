<div className="principle-area"><span className="principle-area__icon">🏪</span>What is Business App</div>

Business App serves your clients the way they want to be served—by letting them try a variety of freemium solutions. This product-led process helps you position a clear path from freemium to paying clients.

### The Client-Facing Dashboard

> Business App is a single sign-on portal that gives clients access to products, recommendations, learning tools, proof-of-performance reporting, digital marketing tools, customer communication, appointment scheduling, and more. Everything is branded as your business—there is no mention of Vendasta.

### Freemium to Premium

> Business App operates on a product-led growth strategy: show clients value first with a freemium product, then offer them a premium version. You design your own client upgrade path.

### What Business App Provides Out of the Box

> - A solution set of freemium products and services that follow the customer journey and showcase a path to upsell
> - A single sign-on app branded with your logo
> - Access to your Store and all products and services clients can enable and purchase
> - A free SMS number for communicating with customers
> - A reporting analytics tool via the Executive Report

<div className="principle-area"><span className="principle-area__icon">🔑</span>Key Features &amp; Connections</div>

Connecting Facebook and Google is the most important first step for any new client in Business App—it unlocks primary data reporting and many key features immediately.

### Google My Business &amp; Facebook Connection

> Connecting Google My Business and Facebook unlocks access to: responding to reviews, scheduling social posts, and automatically-updated reporting. It is vital these two accounts are connected to your client's Business App upon entry.

### Ecommerce Storefront

> Clients can build an ecommerce storefront directly from Business App—for free. It's an easy-to-build online store where their customers can find and purchase products or services.

### Meeting Scheduler &amp; Inbox

> Clients get access to a booking calendar (Meeting Scheduler) where their customers can book appointments. Inbox is a centralized messaging platform for managing customer communication from one place. Clients using Inbox are four times more engaged with your solutions than those who do not.

<div className="principle-area"><span className="principle-area__icon">💼</span>Value for Partners</div>

Business App positions you as the trusted expert for all your business clients' needs. Everything inside Business App is branded as your business—no mention of Vendasta.

### Your Brand, Their Experience

> Customize the look of Business App with your logo, favicon, and custom domain. Define the new client experience and set which products are automatically active upon first login.

### Product-Led Growth

> By offering clients a freemium product to try inside Business App, you demonstrate unique value first, then offer a premium version. You design your own client upgrade path.

### Automated Proof of Performance

> Send clients an Executive Report on a weekly or monthly cadence. It shows changes over time from connected data sources and tells the story of your hard work—automatically. Partners who use the Executive Report and connect a data source like Google My Business see a **51% increase in client retention** over 24 months.
