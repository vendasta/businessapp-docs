# Image and Video Requirements for Social Marketing

**Social Marketing** empowers you to post across your most valuable social media platforms. To ensure your content is optimized for both visual impact and technical performance, it's essential to follow specific image and video requirements for each platform. Below is a complete guide to the supported formats, recommended sizes, and platform-specific nuances to help you get the most from your posts.

---

## Image Requirements

Social Marketing supports **PNG, JPEG, and GIF** image formats. For best results when posting to multiple platforms, use **square images (1:1 ratio)**. Each platform, however, has specific requirements outlined below.


| Social network | Dimensions | Maximum file size | Notes |
| -------------- | ---------- | ----------------- | ----- |
| Facebook | **Recommended:** <br />1200 x 1200 px (square \| 1:1) <br />1,080 x 1,350 px (vertical \| 16:9) <br />**Minimum:** <br />600 x 315 px | 10 MB | • Up to 10 images per post <br /><br />• When uploading multiple images, Facebook arranges your images to match the first one uploaded. For best results, use consistent image sizes. |
| Instagram | **Recommended:** <br />1,080 x 1,080 px (square \| 1:1) <br />1,080 x 608 px (landscape \| 1.91:1) <br />1,080 x 1,350 px (portrait \| 4:5) <br /><br />**Minimum:** <br />150 x 150 px <br />**Aspect ratio:** 0.5 – 1.91 | 8 MB | • Up to 10 images/videos per post using the carousel feature. |
| Google Business Profile | **Recommended:** <br />720 x 720 px (square \| 1:1) <br /><br />**Minimum:** <br />250 x 250 px <br />**Maximum:** <br />5,000 x 5,000 px | 10 MB <br />*Cannot be less than 10 KB* | • 1 image per post <br /><br />• The photo should be in focus and well-lit, with no significant alterations or excessive use of filters. ## Video Requirements

Social Marketing supports **MP4** and **MOV** video formats. Some platforms also accept **WebM**. Below are the platform-specific requirements for video uploads:

| Social network | Dimensions | Bitrate | Max file size | Duration |
| -------------- | ---------- | ------- | ------------- | -------- |
| Facebook | Aspect ratio: 9:16 – 16:9 | -- | 1 GB | Up to 1 hour |
| Instagram | Aspect ratio: 0.01:1 – 10:1 <br />23–60 fps | 25 Mbps max | 300 MB | 3 seconds – 15 minutes |
| X | Max: 1920 x 1024 px <br />Aspect ratio: 1:3 – 3:1 | 25 Mbps max | 512 MB | 0.5 seconds – 2 min 20 sec |
| LinkedIn | 10–60 fps | 30 Mbps max | 1 GB | 3 seconds – 10 minutes |
| Facebook Story | 1080 x 1920 px recommended <br />(min 540 x 960 px) <br />Aspect ratio: 9:16 <br />24–60 fps | -- | -- | 3 – 90 seconds |
| Instagram Story | Aspect ratio: 0.01 – 10:1 <br />23–60 fps | 25 Mbps max | 100 MB | 3 – 60 seconds |
| TikTok | Min: 360 x 360 px <br />23–60 fps | -- | 1 GB | 3 seconds – 10 minutes |
| YouTube | Min: 240 x 240 px <br />Max: 4096 x 2304 px <br />Max 240 fps | -- | 256 GB | Up to 12 hours |

:::note Platform-specific video limits
- **Instagram** supports up to 10 videos per post (carousel). All other platforms support 1 video per post.
- **TikTok** also accepts **WebM** format in addition to MP4 and MOV.
:::

### Video Thumbnails

When uploading videos via Social Marketing, the thumbnail is automatically generated from the **first frame**.  
Custom thumbnails are currently **not supported**.  
To modify the default thumbnail, consider editing the video in tools like **CapCut** or other video editors.

## Image Optimization and Cropping

When uploading images, it's crucial to meet the platform-specific **aspect ratio** requirements. Failure to do so may result in cropping errors or distorted visuals.

### How It Works

- If an image doesn't meet the required aspect ratio, an **error message** prompts users to crop the image.
- Use the cropping tool to choose from:
  - **Freeform**
  - **Square (1:1)**
  - **Vertical (4:5)**
  - **Horizontal (1.91:1)**

This helps ensure images stay in-frame, look professional, and follow best practices for engagement.

### Google Business Profile Notice

> "On submission, Google Business will scale the image resolution slightly without a perceptible loss in quality."

By leveraging our enhanced cropping tool, you’ll maintain your image quality, maximize engagement, and showcase your brand effectively.

## Frequently Asked Questions

<details>
<summary>Why was my image cropped?</summary>

If an image does not match the platform's required aspect ratio (e.g., trying to post a wide landscape photo to Instagram's square format), the tool may force a crop to ensure the post is accepted by the network.

</details>

<details>
<summary>Can I upload HEIC files from my iPhone?</summary>

Social Marketing generally requires standard web formats like JPG or PNG. HEIC files should be converted to JPG before uploading.

</details>

<details>
<summary>What is the best video format for all platforms?</summary>

MP4 is the most widely supported video format. Using a square (1:1) aspect ratio gives you the best chance of compatibility across all major networks. Check the duration limits for each platform, as they vary significantly.

</details>
