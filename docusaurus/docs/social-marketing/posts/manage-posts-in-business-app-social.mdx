## What is Manage Posts in Business App Social?

The **Manage Posts** feature allows you to view and manage all your social and blog posts including drafts, scheduled, and published content directly within the Business App.  
This provides a centralized location to review and edit in-progress posts, monitor and reschedule upcoming scheduled content, and track performance on published posts without navigating to the Social AI product.

## Why is Manage Posts important?

This simplifies content management by allowing you to:

- Access all social and blog post statuses in one place  
- Quickly review and filter posts by account or network  
- View performance metrics on published posts  
- Save time by reducing the need to switch between products  

With this integrated view, you can make faster, data-informed decisions about your content strategy.

## What’s included with Manage Posts?

- Tabs for **Drafts**, **Scheduled**, and **Published** posts  
- Editing of draft and scheduled posts, including text and media, directly in Business App  
- AI-assisted content and image editing while you edit a post  
- Rescheduling of draft and scheduled posts by changing the date and time  
- Post-level engagement and performance data  
- Filtering by network, account, or date range  
- Web and mobile view previews  
- Draft post scheduling directly from the `Drafts` tab  
- Access to create new posts in Social AI  
- Deletion options for posts across all statuses  

## How to use Manage Posts

### Step 1: Open the Business App

1. Sign in to your Business App.  
2. From the left-hand navigation menu, go to `Social`.

![Business App View](img/manage-posts/Business-App.png)

### Step 2: Navigate between tabs

You will find three main tabs:  

- **Drafts** – Lists posts currently being created or awaiting approval.  
- **Scheduled** – Displays posts planned for future publication.  
- **Published** – Shows posts that have already been published to your social or blog accounts.

![Manage Posts Business App Social](img/manage-posts/Manage-Tabs-Socia-Business-App.png)


### Step 3: Review and manage posts

Within each tab, you can:

- View post content, status, and associated networks  
- Click `Schedule` on draft post cards in the `Drafts` tab  
- Schedule draft posts for single-location and multi-location social accounts  
- See a confirmation message after a post is scheduled  
- Access engagement metrics (likes, shares, comments, reach, etc.) for published posts  
- Click `View` to see how the post appears on its respective social network  

![Manage Posts Business App Social Published View](img/manage-posts/Manage-Tabs-Socia-Business-App-Published-View.png)


:::warning
- The `Schedule` button is only available for posts in the `Drafts` tab.
- If the selected date and time are earlier than the current time, `Schedule` is disabled and shows: `Please select a future date and time to schedule this post`.
- If the scheduled time is earlier than the current time and no social account is connected, `Schedule` is disabled and shows: `Please ensure the scheduled time is in the future and at least one social account is connected`.
:::


### Step 4: Edit a draft or scheduled post

You can edit **draft** and **scheduled** social and blog posts, including their text and media. Published posts cannot be edited.

1. Open the **Drafts** or **Scheduled** tab, select the **three-dot menu** on the post card, and click `Edit`. The social or blog post card opens.  
![Edit Workflow in Drafts and Scheduled Posts Business App Social](img/manage-posts/Edit-Workflow-in-Drafts-and-Scheduled-Posts-Business-App-Social.png)

2. **Edit text with AI:** Click into a text box, such as the social post content, blog title, or blog body, to open the **AI Assist** panel, where you can rewrite content, fix spelling and grammar, improve your writing, shorten or lengthen text, and change the tone.
![Edit Text Workflow in Drafts and Scheduled Social Posts Business App Social](img/manage-posts/Edit-Text-Workflow-in-Drafts-and-Scheduled-Social-Posts-Business-App-Social.png)
![Edit Text Workflow in Drafts and Scheduled Blog Posts Business App Social](img/manage-posts/Edit-Text-Workflow-in-Drafts-and-Scheduled-Blog-Posts-Business-App-Social.png)

3. **Edit media with AI:** Click the **pen icon** on a piece of media to open the **AI Image Assist** panel, where you can edit an image with AI, upload one from your device, or delete media you no longer want.  
![Edit Media Workflow in Drafts and Scheduled Social Posts Business App Social](img/manage-posts/Edit-Media-Workflow-in-Drafts-and-Scheduled-Social-Posts-Business-App-Social.png)
![Edit Media Workflow in Drafts and Scheduled Blog Posts Business App Social](img/manage-posts/Edit-Media-Workflow-in-Drafts-and-Scheduled-Blog-Posts-Business-App-Social.png)

4. **Add more media:** Click the **arrow** to the right of an image to open the **Add Media** screen, where you can generate a new image with AI or upload one from your device.  
![Add Media Workflow in Drafts and Scheduled Social Posts Business App Social](img/manage-posts/Add-Media-Workflow-in-Drafts-and-Scheduled-Social-Posts-Business-App-Social.png)
![Add Media Workflow in Drafts and Scheduled Blog Posts Business App Social](img/manage-posts/Add-Media-Workflow-in-Drafts-and-Scheduled-Blog-Posts-Business-App-Social.png)

5. **Reschedule:** Change the date and time on the post to reschedule when it publishes.  
![Date and Time Workflow in Drafts and Scheduled Social Posts Business App Social](img/manage-posts/Date-and-Time-Workflow-in-Drafts-and-Scheduled-Social-Posts-Business-App-Social.png)

6. **Schedule:** When you are happy with the content and media, click `Schedule` on a draft to publish it at the selected date and time.

   ![Scheduling a draft social post from the Business App](img/manage-posts/Schedule-Workflow-in-Drafts-Social-Posts-Business-App.png)

### Step 5: Apply filters

Use the available filters to refine your post list:  

- **Social Networks** – Filter by platform such as Facebook, Instagram, or LinkedIn.  
- **Connected Accounts** – Focus on posts for specific accounts.  
- **Date Range** –  
  - Drafts and Published: Filter by past date ranges.  
  - Scheduled: Filter by future publication dates.

![Manage Posts Business App Social Filter View](img/manage-posts/Manage-Tabs-Socia-Business-App-Filter-View.png)


### Step 6: Preview posts

You can preview each post in:  

- **Web View** – See how your post appears on desktop devices.  
- **Mobile View** – Review how it appears on mobile screens.

![Manage Posts Business App Social Mobile View](img/manage-posts/Manage-Tabs-Socia-Business-App-Mobile-View.png)


### Step 7: Create a new post

To create a new post:  

1. Click `Create` in the `Social` section.  
2. You will be redirected to the **Social AI** product.  
3. From there, you can compose a new social or blog post, add media, and schedule publication.

![Manage Posts Business App Social Create Post](img/manage-posts/Manage-Tabs-Socia-Business-App-Create-Post.png)


## Points to remember

:::info
You can **delete** draft, scheduled, and published posts directly from the Business App.
:::

![Manage Posts Business App Delete](./img/manage-posts/Manage-Tabs-Socia-Business-App-Delete-Post.png)


:::warning
- Only **draft** and **scheduled** posts can be edited. Published posts cannot be edited.  
- **Videos** cannot be edited with AI, but you can upload them.  
![Video Workflow in Drafts and Scheduled Social Posts Business App Social](img/manage-posts/Video-Workflow-in-Drafts-and-Scheduled-Social-Posts-Business-App-Social.png)
- **Content and media validations** apply across all social and blog networks. If you upload media that does not meet a network's requirements, an error is shown.  
- Published **Instagram** posts cannot be deleted from the Business App or Social AI.  
  To remove an Instagram post, use the native Instagram app.
:::

:::note
The AI editing tools available to you depend on your plan:

- **Premium**: full AI Assist and AI Image tools, including advanced content and image generation and editing.  
- **Pro**: standard AI Assist and AI Image tools. Upgrade to Premium for more advanced content and image generation and editing.  
- **Standard**: AI editing tools are not available; you can upload and delete media only.
:::

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

<details>
<summary>Can I edit posts from the Business App?</summary>

Yes. You can edit **draft** and **scheduled** social and blog posts, including their text and media, directly in the Business App. Open the post's three-dot menu and click `Edit`. Published posts cannot be edited.
</details>

<details>
<summary>Can I use AI to edit my posts?</summary>

Yes. When you edit a draft or scheduled post, the **AI Assist** panel can rewrite, shorten, lengthen, or change the tone of your text and fix spelling and grammar. The **AI Image Assist** panel can edit images with AI. The AI tools available depend on your plan.
</details>

<details>
<summary>Can I edit videos in my posts?</summary>

Videos cannot be edited with AI, but you can upload a video when editing a draft or scheduled post.
</details>

<details>
<summary>Can I reschedule a post?</summary>

Yes. When editing a draft or scheduled post, change the date and time to reschedule when it publishes.
</details>

<details>
<summary>Can I schedule draft posts from the Business App?</summary>

Yes. Go to `Social` > `Drafts` and click `Schedule` on the draft post card.
</details>

<details>
<summary>Can I see engagement metrics for my posts?</summary>

Yes. Published posts include performance metrics such as reach, likes, comments, and shares.
</details>

<details>
<summary>Can I create new posts in the Business App?</summary>

Yes. Clicking `Create` redirects you to the Social AI product to compose a new post.
</details>

<details>
<summary>Can I delete Instagram posts?</summary>

No. Instagram posts must be deleted using the native Instagram app.
</details>

<details>
<summary>What filters are available?</summary>

You can filter posts by social network, connected account, or date range.
</details>

<details>
<summary>Can I preview posts before publishing?</summary>

Yes. You can view posts in both web and mobile preview modes.
</details>

<details>
<summary>What’s the difference between Drafts, Scheduled, and Published tabs?</summary>

- **Drafts:** Posts still in progress.  
- **Scheduled:** Posts planned for future dates.  
- **Published:** Posts already live on social or blog accounts.
</details>

<details>
<summary>Can I manage blog posts through this feature?</summary>

Yes. The Manage Posts view includes both social and blog posts.
</details>

<details>
<summary>Does deleting a post in the Business App remove it from all platforms?</summary>

Yes. Deleted posts are removed from all connected networks, except published Instagram posts.
</details>
