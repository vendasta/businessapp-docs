Welcome to Social Marketing, your comprehensive solution for managing social media presence across multiple platforms. This section will guide you through everything you need to know to start creating, scheduling, and analyzing social media content effectively.

## Why use Social Marketing?

- Centralized platform for multi-channel publishing
- AI-powered content creation tools
- Visual content calendar and post scheduling
- Analytics and reporting for informed decisions
- Collaboration tools for teams
- Pro features to supercharge strategy

## What's included

- **[Getting Started with Social Marketing](getting-started-with-social-marketing.mdx)**: Complete beginner's guide to using Social Marketing effectively

## Get started

1. Go to `Social Marketing` in your dashboard.
2. Connect your social media accounts in `Settings` > `Connect Accounts`.
3. Create your first post using `Compose` or `Create`.
4. Schedule posts or publish immediately.
5. Track performance in `Insights` to optimize your strategy.
