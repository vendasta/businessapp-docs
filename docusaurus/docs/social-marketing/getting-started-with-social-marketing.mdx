# Getting Started with Social Marketing

Social Marketing helps you publish content across multiple social networks, schedule posts, and track performance from one dashboard. This guide walks you through connecting your accounts, creating your first post, and using the calendar and insights to grow your audience.

## Step 1: Connect your social accounts

Before you can post, Social Marketing needs access to your social profiles. Connect Facebook, Google Business Profile, and (with Pro) Instagram, LinkedIn, X (Twitter), and TikTok from one place.

### How to connect accounts

1. Go to **Social Marketing** > **Settings**.
2. Open the **Connect Accounts** tab.
3. Click the platform you want to connect (for example, **Facebook** or **Google**).
4. Sign in and grant the required permissions.
5. Repeat for each platform you want to use.

See [Account connections](./settings/account-connections.mdx) for detailed steps, platform limits, and troubleshooting.

:::info
Standard includes Facebook and Google Business Profile. Pro adds Instagram, LinkedIn, X (Twitter), and TikTok. Instagram must be linked to a Facebook Page.
:::

## Step 2: Create your first post

Use the Composer to create posts for one or multiple platforms at once. Add text, images, or videos, and customize content per account if needed.

### How to create a post

1. Go to **Social Marketing**.
2. Click **Compose** or **Create**.
3. Under **Post to**, select the accounts you want to publish to.
4. In the **Content** field, write your message. Use **Write with AI** (Pro) to generate ideas or captions.
5. Add media: upload images or videos, or use **Stock Imagery** or **Generate Image with AI** (Pro).
6. Use **Customize by Account** to adjust text for different platforms (for example, shorter for X).

See [Posting content](./posts/posting-content-to-social-marketing.mdx) and the [Posts overview](./posts/index.mdx) for platform-specific options, character limits, and media requirements.

## Step 3: Schedule or publish

After composing your post, choose to publish immediately, schedule for later, or save as a draft. The content calendar helps you plan and visualize when posts will go live.

### How to schedule or publish

1. In the Composer, find the publish options at the bottom.
2. Choose **Post Now** to publish immediately, **Schedule** to set a date and time, or **Save as Draft** to finish later.
3. For scheduled posts, open **Social Marketing** > **Calendar** to view and edit your content plan.

See the [Calendar overview](./calendar/index.mdx) for creating posts from the calendar, switching between monthly and weekly views, and sharing calendar reports.

:::tip
Standard accounts can schedule up to 20 posts. Pro supports unlimited scheduling.
:::

## Step 4: View performance in Insights

Once your posts are live, use Insights to see reach, engagement, and link performance. This helps you spot what works and improve future content.

### How to view insights

1. Go to **Social Marketing** > **Insights**.
2. Open **Post Performance** to see reach, engagement, and impressions by post.
3. Open **Link Performance** (Pro) to track clicks on shortened URLs.

See [Post performance analytics](./insights/post-performance-analytics.mdx) and [Track link performance](./insights/track-link-performance.mdx) for more detail. Content and data availability vary by platform and plan.

## Step 5: Configure AI and content settings (optional)

Pro users can configure AI to generate on-brand content and add knowledge sources (business info, promotions, website content). You can also set up RSS feeds and content inspiration.

### How to configure settings

1. Go to **Social Marketing** > **Settings**.
2. Open **AI Settings** to add business knowledge and customize how AI writes posts.
3. Open **Interesting Content** to add RSS feeds for post inspiration.

See the [Settings overview](./settings/index.mdx) and [AI settings](./settings/ai-settings.mdx) for full options.

## Frequently asked questions

<details>
<summary>Can I publish to multiple platforms at once?</summary>

Yes. The Composer lets you select multiple accounts under **Post to** and publish one post across several networks at the same time.
</details>

<details>
<summary>Can I schedule posts in advance?</summary>

Yes. Use **Schedule** in the Composer or click a date in the [Calendar](./calendar/index.mdx) to plan posts. Standard allows up to 20 scheduled posts; Pro supports unlimited scheduling.
</details>

<details>
<summary>Can I track how my content performs?</summary>

Yes. Go to **Social Marketing** > **Insights** to view reach, engagement, impressions, and (with Pro) link clicks. See [Insights overview](./insights/index.mdx) for details.
</details>

<details>
<summary>What is the difference between Standard and Pro?</summary>

Standard includes Facebook and Google Business Profile, basic scheduling (up to 20 posts), and core publishing. Pro adds Instagram, LinkedIn, X (Twitter), and TikTok, AI text and image generation, link tracking (clickable.bio), unlimited scheduling, and analytics for all connected platforms.
</details>

<details>
<summary>Can I run ads through Social Marketing?</summary>

No. Social Marketing is for organic content only. Paid ads must be run directly on each platform.
</details>
