---
title: How to Collect Payments for your meetings
sidebar_position: 6
---

Easily collect credit card payments using [Stripe](https://stripe.com) when an invitee schedules time with you using CalendarHero. Payment is fully secure and powered by Stripe. 
 

The *Collect Payments* feature is currently available to all CalendarHero Pro and Team plan members. [View Plans](https://calendarhero.com/pricing)
 

> PLEASE NOTE: Collect Payments is currently only available for 1:1 meetings accessed via your [personal scheduling link](https://support.calendarhero.com/hc/en-us/articles/14081608829079-How-to-use-a-Personal-Scheduling-Link) or [embed code](https://support.calendarhero.com/hc/en-us/articles/14079118238103-How-do-I-embed-CalendarHero-on-my-website-) AND for [Group Classes](https://support.calendarhero.com/hc/en-us/articles/14078271616023-How-do-I-Schedule-a-Group-Class-) (where multiple attendees can self book the same class or event). If you are looking for payment collection for your group meetings [drop us a line](https://calendarhero.com/get-support) at and let us know your needs!

 

**Highlights:**
 
 
- Allow customers to securely submit payments when self-scheduling a time to meet with you using your [personal scheduling link](https://support.calendarhero.com/hc/en-us/articles/14081608829079-How-to-use-a-Personal-Scheduling-Link), [self-scheduling embed](https://support.calendarhero.com/hc/en-us/articles/14079118238103-How-do-I-embed-CalendarHero-on-my-website-), or group class. 
- Specify any payment amount: Payment is automatically collected and routed to your connected Stripe account.  
- Have different offerings? Simply create multiple meeting types and customize the amount for each. 
- Currency Friendly: Collect payments in the currency of your choice - as specified in Stripe. 
- Payment Terms: Include custom payment terms that appear during the scheduling process.  
- Payment receipts are automatically sent to your invitee from Stripe. 
- Activate *Automatic Refunds* for easy refunds when a meeting is canceled by the meeting creator. 
- The meeting title will be used as the charge description (statement descriptor).
