title: Does my assistant notify me of upcoming meetings?
sidebar_position: 13
---

**Your automated assistant is there to keep you up to date on all of your meetings! **   
  
This includes automatically sending you: 
 
- A morning briefing for a quick overview of your day 
- A meeting briefing 1 hour before the meeting to ensure you are prepared 
- Notifications for pending meeting responses (e.g. declines, meeting bookings) 
- Notifications for overlapping meetings 
 

## Customizing Notifications
 

Your "Preferred" platform (chat or email) will receive notifications; including the morning briefing and upcoming meeting briefings. 
 

You can turn meeting briefings on / off and set your preferred time for the morning briefing and meeting briefings in your meeting settings: [https://app.calendarhero.com/settings/meeting](https://app.calendarhero.com/settings/meeting)
