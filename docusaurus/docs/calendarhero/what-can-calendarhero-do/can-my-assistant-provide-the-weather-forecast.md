title: Can my assistant provide the Weather Forecast?
sidebar_position: 9
---

Find out what the current or forecasted weather conditions are for your work location or another specified location.
 

** Ask your Assistant:** 

`what is the temperature in new york?` 

`forecast?` 

`show me the weather forecast for next week` 

`forecast for toronto?` 

`what is the forecast for tomorrow in new york?` 

  
**Daily Forecasts**
 

*Can I get the daily weather forecast sent to me each morning?*
 

Yes. You can set the hour you would like the forecast each day in the [morning briefing](https://app.calendarhero.com/settings/meeting/general).
 

You can see your weather settings here: [https://app.calendarhero.com/settings/user#Weather](https://app.calendarhero.com/settings/user#Weather)
