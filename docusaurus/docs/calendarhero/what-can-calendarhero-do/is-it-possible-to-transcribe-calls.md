---
title: Is it possible to transcribe calls?
sidebar_position: 10
---

CalendarHero previously released a BETA Call transcribe feature. Due to lack of use, we are no longer officially supporting this feature, but if you would find this to be valuable for your use case [please let us know.](https://calendarhero.com/get-support)
 

If you’re about to jump on a call, ask your automated assistant (via chat) any of these commands:
 

**Transcribe call with Marie Curie** 
 

**Transcribe call with 416 555 5555**. 
 

Your assistant will first call you, then call the person you are meeting with. Afterward, your assistant will email the meeting creator a transcription of the call.
