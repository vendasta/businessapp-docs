---
title: Do my invitees need to have CalendarHero for me to schedule a meeting with them?
sidebar_position: 22
---

No - CalendarHero helps manage your calendar the way a real assistant would, and your invitees do NOT need to have CalendarHero to accept meeting invites or see your availability.

Depending on how you invite your invitees, your invitees will [receive an email](/calendarhero/how-does-calendarhero-work/scheduling-page-what-do-invitees-see-when-i-invite-them-to-a-meeting) that asks them to select time slots. You can also choose to send a [private link](/calendarhero/how-does-calendarhero-work/how-do-private-invite-links-work) or initiate the scheduling via a [personal scheduling link](/calendarhero/scheduling-meetings/how-to-use-a-personal-scheduling-link). Once the meeting is scheduled all invitees will receive invitations and calendar updates.

To see what your invitees see when you schedule a meeting, see here [What do invitees see when I invite them to a meeting?](/calendarhero/how-does-calendarhero-work/scheduling-page-what-do-invitees-see-when-i-invite-them-to-a-meeting)
