---
title: Do my invitees need to have CalendarHero for me to schedule a meeting with them?
sidebar_position: 22
---

**No -** CalendarHero helps manage your calendar the way a real assistant would, and your invitees do NOT need to have CalendarHero to accept meeting invites or see your availability.
 

Depending on how you invite your invitees, your invitees will [receive an email](https://support.calendarhero.com/hc/en-us/articles/14078861497367-Scheduling-Page-What-do-invitees-see-when-I-invite-them-to-a-meeting-) that asks them to select time slots. You can also choose to send a [private link](https://support.calendarhero.com/hc/en-us/articles/14078762979863-How-do-Private-Invite-Links-work-) or initiate the scheduling via a [personal scheduling link](https://support.calendarhero.com/hc/en-us/articles/14081608829079-How-to-use-a-Personal-Scheduling-Link). Once the meeting is scheduled all invitees will receive invitations and calendar updates.
 

To see what your invitees see when you schedule a meeting, see here [What do invitees see when I invite them to a meeting?](https://support.calendarhero.com/hc/en-us/articles/14078861497367-Scheduling-Page-What-do-invitees-see-when-I-invite-them-to-a-meeting-)
