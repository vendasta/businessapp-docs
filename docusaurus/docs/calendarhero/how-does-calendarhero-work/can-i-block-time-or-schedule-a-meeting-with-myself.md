---
title: Can I block time or schedule a meeting with myself?
sidebar_position: 25
---

CalendarHero syncs in real-time with your connected calendar (e.g. Google or Microsoft) so any blocks you [add to your favorite calendar](https://support.calendarhero.com/hc/en-us/articles/14078314795543-How-do-I-add-and-customize-my-Calendars-) will be automatically considered when scheduling a meeting. For example, you can simply add a busy block to your Google calendar, and CalendarHero will know not to schedule during this time. 
 

In addition, CalendarHero users who have connected to a [chat platform](https://support.calendarhero.com/hc/en-us/articles/14078231086487-How-to-Schedule-a-Meeting-via-Chat) (like Slack) can easily block time off in their connected calendar for personal appointments, so they are not disturbed during a specific duration of time.
