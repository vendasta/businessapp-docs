Did you know that your automated meeting assistant can do "Warm Introductions" for you?

> 

 To use this feature first make sure you have added your assistant to your favourite chat platform like [Slack](/calendarhero/setting-up-calendarhero/using-calendarhero-with-slack) or [MS Teams](/calendarhero/setting-up-calendarhero/using-calendarhero-with-microsoft-teams). 

Asking for an Introduction

Ask your assistant for an introduction to someone that you want to know and it'll find your contact that knows them the best.

You will then see a list ranked in order of relationship strength, just click the button (if available) or respond with the number from the list.

Your assistant will then send an email to that contact asking them to introduce you to the person you would like to meet.  
  
Ask your assistant:

`connect me to John Smith`

`introduction to John Smith`

`introduce me to John Smith  
`
