---
title: Can I customize where I receive my notifications?
sidebar_position: 12
---

CalendarHero works where you work and allows you to specify which platform you want to receive your notifications on.
 

Your default notification platform (chat or email) will receive your CalendarHero notifications such as the [morning briefing](https://support.calendarhero.com/hc/en-us/articles/14079073213463-Does-my-assistant-notify-me-of-upcoming-meetings-) and upcoming meeting notifications. For example, if you select Slack as your default notification platform, task notifications will be sent through [Slack](https://support.calendarhero.com/hc/en-us/articles/14078092740247-Using-CalendarHero-with-Slack). 
 

**To set a "Default Notification" ****platform follow these steps:**
 
 
1. Go to your [Installed Integrations overview.](https://app.calendarhero.com/settings/accounts/list)  
2. Click the "Chat" tab 
3. Next to your preferred platform click the "..." menu and select "Send Notifications Here" 
 

** Tips:**
 
 
- Using a Plugin? Chrome / Outlook Plugin will always default to email, as the plugins can not receive notifications.  
- Don't see your Email Address? First, add your new email manually and verify it. Once this is done you will be able to select it as your notification email. How to [add another email address to your account](https://support.calendarhero.com/hc/en-us/articles/14081674977303-How-do-I-add-another-email-to-my-account-).
