CalendarHero works where you work, and offers several ways to schedule meetings ([full list here](https://support.calendarhero.com/hc/en-us/articles/14078124231703-What-platforms-does-CalendarHero-work-with-)). For those of you who spend a lot of time in email, the CalendarHero Gmail Add-On is a great way to quickly share your availability so you can meet more quickly without the back and forth.   
  
The Gmail Add-on provides quick shortcuts to the most used features and allows you to:
 

**Features:**
 
 
-  **Share Scheduling Links** - insert any of your [personal scheduling links](https://support.calendarhero.com/hc/en-us/articles/14081608829079-How-to-use-a-Personal-Scheduling-Link) into a new or replied email 
-  **Add Times to Email **- insert a preview of available meeting times into a new or replied email 
-  **Create a meeting request -** Quickly initiate a new meeting based on the sender of the highlighted email 
-  **Access people insights **- Quickly learn more about your contact before a meeting (based on the sender of the highlighted email) 
 

**Gmail Add-On: Installation Instructions**
 

NOTE: The Gmail add-on requires a CaledarHero account. You can create one at [https://calendarhero.com/start](https://calendarhero.com/start)
 
 
1. Open the CalendarHero Meeting Assistant from the [G Suite Marketplace](https://gsuite.google.com/marketplace/app/calendarhero_for_gmail/682596370674)  
2. Click Install and approve access. Once complete you should see a "successfully installed" verification.  
3. Go to your Gmail inbox. On the right-hand side, you will see a sidebar with Gmail add-ons, which will now include the CalendarHero add-on.  
4. That's it! You're ready to use the Gmail Add-on.
