Zapier ( [https://zapier.com](https://zapier.com/)) is a powerful tool used to connect all your apps, improving and automating your workflow. We’ve connected the power of Zapier to CalendarHero so you can create up to 5,000+ different flows all within your favorite chat platform. In just a few steps, you can connect to over 2,000+ apps and easily automate all of your meeting scheduling needs using CalendarHero on Zapier.

Ready to get started? 

#### [Connect to 2,000+ Apps](https://zapier.com/apps/zoomai/integrations#zap-template-list)

Actions that you can do with the Zapier app include...

- create a meeting request

- create a contact

- find contacts

- get contact insights

- find meeting-type details

- find or create a contact


You can also use Triggers to be notified when an event happens in CalendarHero and then take action on another software:

- Meeting is started

- Meeting is completed

- A new meeting request is created

- The meeting request is canceled

- The meeting request was successful and a meeting was scheduled

- The meeting request expired without a meeting being scheduled

- A new contact was added to your scheduling link
