So, you've just signed up for CalendarHero!  
  
We recommend checking out this [Quick Start Guide](https://calendarhero.com/blog/quick-guide) for new users.

Here are some additional tips: 

CalendarHero works where you work.  
We recommend getting started using the [CalendarHero web application here](https://app.calendarhero.com/meetings). You can also set up an automated meeting assistant in your preferred [chat or email platform](/calendarhero/setting-up-calendarhero/what-platforms-does-calendarhero-work-with).

CalendarHero includes powerful meeting customization settings to make automation easy.   
For speedy scheduling review and customize your [meeting preferences and types](/calendarhero/how-to/how-to-customize-general-meeting-settings)

CalendarHero is all about intelligent meeting scheduling.  
Learn how to schedule your first meeting here: [How to schedule a Meeting](/calendarhero/scheduling-meetings/how-do-i-schedule-meetings) and then get started [online here](https://app.calendarhero.com/meetings)!

CalendarHero makes it easy to manage and track your scheduling progress.  
Learn how to view and manage your upcoming and active meetings from your [Tasks page](/calendarhero/scheduling-meetings/how-to-manage-your-meetings-from-tasks). 

CalendarHero integrates with over 70+ apps - add an integration.  
This includes easy integration into your favorite [ATS](/calendarhero/what-can-calendarhero-do/how-do-i-integrate-my-ats-applicant-tracking-system), [CRM](/calendarhero/how-to/how-do-i-integrate-my-crm), [calendar provider](/calendarhero/how-to/how-do-i-add-and-customize-my-calendars), video conferencing, and more! [View the Integrations Directory to get started](https://app.calendarhero.com/settings/accounts/add).
