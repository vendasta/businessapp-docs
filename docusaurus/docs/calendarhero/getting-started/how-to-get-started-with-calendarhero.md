So, you've just signed up for CalendarHero!  
  
**We recommend checking out this [Quick Start Guide](https://calendarhero.com/blog/quick-guide) ****for new users.**
 

Here are some additional tips: 
 
 
1.  **CalendarHero works where you work.  
**We recommend getting started using the [CalendarHero web application here](https://app.calendarhero.com/meetings). You can also set up an automated meeting assistant in your preferred [chat or email platform](https://support.calendarhero.com/hc/en-us/articles/14078124231703). 
2.  **CalendarHero includes powerful meeting customization settings to make automation easy.   
**For speedy scheduling review and customize your [meeting preferences and types](https://support.calendarhero.com/hc/en-us/articles/14078268090647-How-to-customize-General-Meeting-Settings)  
3.  **CalendarHero is all about intelligent meeting scheduling.**  
Learn how to schedule your first meeting here: [How to schedule a Meeting](https://support.calendarhero.com/hc/en-us/articles/14081616167191-How-do-I-Schedule-Meetings-) and then get started [online here](https://app.calendarhero.com/meetings)! 
4.  **CalendarHero makes it easy to manage and track your scheduling progress.**  
Learn how to view and manage your upcoming and active meetings from your [Tasks page](https://support.calendarhero.com/hc/en-us/articles/14081566427159-How-to-manage-your-Meetings-from-Tasks).  
5.  **CalendarHero integrates with over 70+ apps - add an integration.**  
This includes easy integration into your favorite [ATS](https://support.calendarhero.com/hc/en-us/articles/14079115580695-How-do-I-integrate-my-ATS-Applicant-Tracking-System-), [CRM](https://support.calendarhero.com/hc/en-us/articles/14078269391767-How-do-I-integrate-my-CRM-), [calendar provider](https://support.calendarhero.com/hc/en-us/articles/14078314795543-How-do-I-add-and-customize-my-Calendars-), video conferencing, and more! [View the Integrations Directory to get started](https://app.calendarhero.com/settings/accounts/add).
