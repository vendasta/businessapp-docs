When you log in to the CalendarHero Web app you will first be taken to the Meetings Dashboard.

The Dashboard provides an overview of your:

Favorite meeting types  

  - shortcut to copy your [personal scheduling link](/calendarhero/scheduling-meetings/how-to-use-a-personal-scheduling-link)

  - shortcut to [schedule a meeting](/calendarhero/scheduling-meetings/how-do-i-schedule-meetings) using that meeting type

*Tip! *You can customize which [meeting types](/calendarhero/scheduling-meetings/create-and-customize-a-meeting-type) appear here by pinning up to 3 meeting types from your meeting type list.

Your workday  

*In the Works* is an overview of your active tasks such as active meeting requests and reminders. Click "[View Tasks](https://app.calendarhero.com/tasks/active)" to manage all of your meeting requests. 

*Today's Meetings *provide a view of your upcoming meetings. Click "View Calendar" for a full view of your [synced calendars](/calendarhero/how-to/how-do-i-add-and-customize-my-calendars).
