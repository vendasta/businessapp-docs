When you log in to the CalendarHero Web app you will first be taken to the Meetings Dashboard.
 

The Dashboard provides an overview of your:
 
 
-  **Favorite meeting types  
** 
 
  - shortcut to copy your [personal scheduling link](https://support.calendarhero.com/hc/en-us/articles/14081608829079)  
  - shortcut to [schedule a meeting](https://support.calendarhero.com/hc/en-us/articles/14081616167191) using that meeting type 
  -  *Tip! *You can customize which [meeting types](https://support.calendarhero.com/hc/en-us/articles/14081615551255) appear here by pinning up to 3 meeting types from your meeting type list. 
  
-  **Your workday  
** 
 
  -  *In the Works* is an overview of your active tasks such as active meeting requests and reminders. Click "[View Tasks](https://app.calendarhero.com/tasks/active)" to manage all of your meeting requests.  
  -  *Today's Meetings *provide a view of your upcoming meetings. Click "View Calendar" for a full view of your [synced calendars](https://support.calendarhero.com/hc/en-us/articles/14078314795543).
