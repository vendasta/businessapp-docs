title: Did Zoom.ai change to CalendarHero?
sidebar_position: 5
---

Yes! Zoom.ai is now CalendarHero. Our new name is a fresh representation of our brand and mission. We haven't changed our product. We're still focused on helping you work better through powerful, intelligent meeting scheduling automation. 
 

As part of the rebrand, you'll notice we've updated our website domains. Please rest assured your current [personal scheduling links](https://support.calendarhero.com/hc/en-us/articles/14081608829079-How-to-use-a-Personal-Scheduling-Link) still work and will automatically redirect to our new domain, so there won't be any disruption to your current scheduling process.
