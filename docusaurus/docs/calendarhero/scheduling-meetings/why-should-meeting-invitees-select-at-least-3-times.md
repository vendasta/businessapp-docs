---
title: Why should meeting invitees select at least 3 times?
sidebar_position: 2
---

**For group meetings, invitees are prompted to select multiple times to give the group the best chance of meeting at a time that works for all. **
 

CalendarHero helps meeting creators add more automation and intelligence to the meeting scheduling process - especially for group meetings. From the [meeting acceptance interface](https://support.calendarhero.com/hc/en-us/articles/14078861497367-Scheduling-Page-What-do-invitees-see-when-I-invite-them-to-a-meeting-) invitees can easily select a date and time *based on the meeting creator's availability.*
 

For group meetings, invitees can see what times others have already selected - and are prompted to select multiple times so that the group has the best chance of meeting at a time that works for all.
