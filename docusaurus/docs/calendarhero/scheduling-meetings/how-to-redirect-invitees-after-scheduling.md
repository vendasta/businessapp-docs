By default, after your invitee schedules a 1:1 event CalendarHero displays a confirmation message confirming that the meeting was booked at the selected time. CalendarHero users can select "Redirect to Custom URL" to automatically redirect invitees to an external website - such as a thank-you page. 

*Redirect to a Custom URL *is available to Professional and Team plan users: [View Plans](https://calendarhero.com/pricing)

## Adding a Redirect URL

Your Confirmation can be customized from any of your [Meeting Types](/calendarhero/scheduling-meetings/create-and-customize-a-meeting-type). If enabled it will apply to all 1:1 meeting booking styles - e.g. Personal Scheduling Links, Private Links, or Meeting invites sent via your Assistant.  

- Open "Invitee Experience" under your Meeting Type

- From "Confirmation UX" select *Redirect to custom URL*

- Set your redirect timing

  - We recommend 5 seconds so that the invitee can first read the important scheduling confirmation details that appear on the CalendarHero confirmation page. However, this can be set to 0 for an instant redirect.

- Enter your custom URL - e.g. `www.mymarketingwebsite.com`

  - Note: For security reasons "https://" links are required
