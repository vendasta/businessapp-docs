---
title: Why are my Meeting Types inactive?
sidebar_position: 32
---

Basic plan users are limited to 3 out-of-the-box Meeting Types: Default Meetings, Calls, and Coffee, and cannot create new custom meeting types.
 

If you previously created a custom meeting type (for example while on the 14-day Pro plan trial) then you will need to upgrade to the [Pro or Team plan](https://calendarhero.com/pricing) in order to apply these meeting types to your meetings. 
 

Meeting types are a powerful feature of CalendarHero because they let you easily pre-configure your meeting settings, and then reuse these settings for faster meeting scheduling. 
 

[Learn more about Meeting Types](https://faq.calendarhero.com/article/65-what-different-types-of-meetings-can-my-assistant-understand)
