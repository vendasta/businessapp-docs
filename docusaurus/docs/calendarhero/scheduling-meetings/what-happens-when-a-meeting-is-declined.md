title: What happens when a meeting is declined?
sidebar_position: 21
---

Sometimes an invitee needs to decline a meeting request. CalendarHero will always keep the Meeting Creator in the loop on the latest status. Here's what to expect:
 

**Declining a Meeting **
 

An invitee can easily decline a meeting by clicking the "Decline" button at the bottom of the meeting acceptance page. Once clicked invitees will also be prompted to include an (optional) reason why they declined.
