Once you create a [meeting scheduling request](https://support.calendarhero.com/hc/en-us/articles/14081616167191-How-do-I-Schedule-Meetings-) you can manage and track your scheduling progress from the Tasks page: [https://app.calendarhero.com/tasks/active](https://app.calendarhero.com/tasks/active)  
  
**Tasks include 3 tabs indicating the status:**
 
 
-  **Active ** 
 
  - Pending meetings that have not yet been scheduled and are awaiting a reply from your invitees. 
  
-  **Upcoming ** 
 
  - Meetings that have been successfully scheduled, but have not yet taken place.  
  
-  **Completed ** 
 
  - Meetings that have been successfully scheduled, but have already taken place.  
  - Pending or Upcoming meetings that have been canceled 
  
 

## Filter Tasks**  
** 
 

To easily keep track of your upcoming meetings and requests, you can filter all of your tasks by [meeting type](https://support.calendarhero.com/hc/en-us/articles/14081615551255-Create-and-customize-a-Meeting-Type). The filter includes a tally so you can also easily view how many meetings you've booked per meeting type.   
  
By default, all associated meeting types are listed under each task tab. To filter the results simply click the filter at the top of the tasks page and select the meeting type name. To view all meeting types at once select 'View All.'
