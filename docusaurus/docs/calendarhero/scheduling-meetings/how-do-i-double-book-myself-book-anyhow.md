title: How do I double book myself (Book Anyhow)?
sidebar_position: 17
---

While CalendarHero takes care of finding the best time to meet, there are times when you already know what time you want to schedule your meeting. While booking a specific time CalendarHero will alert you if you already have a conflict. A conflict could be due to an existing meeting, a calendar block, or due to your daily downtime. If needed you can decide to ignore that conflict and "Book Anyhow".
 

> Want to have more control over your meeting priority? Learn how to use the Meeting Type "Priority" setting to [prioritize external meetings over internal meetings.](https://support.calendarhero.com/hc/en-us/articles/14081567399959-How-can-I-prioritize-external-meetings-)

##    
Book Anyhow (Web Scheduler)
 

If you use the [web scheduler](https://support.calendarhero.com/hc/en-us/articles/14081616167191-How-do-I-Schedule-Meetings-) to schedule a meeting (or group class) **at a specific time**, but you already have a meeting at that time you will be presented with the known conflicts and the option to "Book Anyhow." When the alert appears simply click "Next" to agree to double book at the selected time slot.
 

The Book Anyhow option is only available for meeting or group class requests made for a *specific time*. It will not appear when selecting "Ask my invitees to suggest a date and time".
