---
title: How do I create teams/groups for meetings?
sidebar_position: 12
---

Need an easy way to book meetings with a pre-defined group of contacts - such as the Sales or HR team? CalendarHero makes it easy to create multiple "teams" and reuse them for faster scheduling!   
  
CalendarHero imports your contacts so that it knows who you ask to schedule meetings with and can reach out on your behalf to invite those contacts to your meetings. Teams can be created using any of your [existing contacts](https://support.calendarhero.com/hc/en-us/articles/14079074835223-Can-my-assistant-add-edit-contacts-). Add any contact (including non-CalendarHero users) to a custom Team' including internal colleagues and external clients. You can manage your list of contacts from [https://app.calendarhero.com/contacts/list](https://app.calendarhero.com/contacts/list). 
 

  
**Setting up a Team **
 

Create a new Team:
 
 
- Login to the web app and go to Meeting Scheduling Meeting Teams/ Groups: [https://app.calendarhero.com/settings/meeting#groups](https://app.calendarhero.com/settings/meeting#groups)  
- Click "Add" and give your team a name 
- Add the team contact's name (keep typing the full name until you find the matching contact). The contact must be an existing contact but does NOT have to be a member of your organization or a CalendarHero user. [Learn more about Contacts](https://support.calendarhero.com/hc/en-us/articles/14079074835223-Can-my-assistant-add-edit-contacts-)  
- Save your team - and that's it 
- Once a team/group is created you can refer to them when you schedule a meeting. Learn how by scrolling down to the next section.  
 

Import existing Teams:
 
 
-  *Read-only* teams are teams that have been automatically imported from your corporate directory (via a connected 3rd party provider such as Google Cloud)
