---
title: How do I customize the branding on the invitee experience?
sidebar_position: 18
---

Looking to customize your invitee scheduling experience? Pro and Team plan users can quickly and easily customize the invitee experience with their own logo and/or profile icon.   
  
When a custom logo is added it will appear:
 
 
- On the left banner of the [meeting acceptance page](https://support.calendarhero.com/hc/en-us/articles/14078861497367-Scheduling-Page-What-do-invitees-see-when-I-invite-them-to-a-meeting-)  
- On the top of the [Meeting Directory page](https://support.calendarhero.com/hc/en-us/articles/14077996672535-How-do-I-create-a-Meeting-Directory-)  
 

When a custom profile icon is added it will appear on the:
 
 
- On the left banner of the meeting acceptance page 
- On the top of the Meeting Directory page (replacing the meeting default icon) 
 

Pro and Team plan users can also select to hide the small "powered by CalendarHero" footer at the bottom of these pages.
