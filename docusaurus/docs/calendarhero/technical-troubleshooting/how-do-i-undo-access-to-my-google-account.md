---
title: How do I undo access to my Google account?
sidebar_position: 10
---

To remove your Google account access, please go to your account list here: [https://app.calendarhero.com/settings/accounts/list](https://app.calendarhero.com/settings/accounts/list) and click the red "X" button next to your Google account to remove. You will then be prompted with instructions.

Concerned about your Privacy? Rest assured we take it very seriously - [learn more about our Enterprise-grade security](https://calendarhero.com/security)

Have a Google error warning? If you have an error we suggest removing and re-adding as it will automatically re-sync your account.
