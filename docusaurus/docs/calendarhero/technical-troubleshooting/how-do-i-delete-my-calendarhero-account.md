---
title: How do I delete my CalendarHero account?
sidebar_position: 5
---

We're sad to see you go. Is there anything we can do to convince you to stay?
 

If you would like to let us know why you are deleting your account, we always appreciate your feedback. Please send an email to [hello@calendarhero.com](mailto:hello@calendarhero.com). 
 

To delete your account at any time, log in [here](https://app.calendarhero.com/login). Navigate to **My Account > My profile Settings > Click the "Delete Your CalendarHero Account"** button and follow the instructions provided.
