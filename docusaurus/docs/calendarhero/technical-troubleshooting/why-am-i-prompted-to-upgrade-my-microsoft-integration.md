---
title: Why am I prompted to upgrade my Microsoft Integration?
sidebar_position: 4
---

CalendarHero has made changes to improve the syncing speeds of your Microsoft integrations. As a result, as of March 11th, 2021, users with existing Microsoft integrations (such as Office365 and Outlook) will see a message prompting them to upgrade their related integrations. 
 

While your Microsoft integrations will continue to work using existing connections, ** we highly recommend all users upgrade their Microsoft integrations as soon as possible. ** Upgrading is quick and easy, and will improve the syncing speed of your information; so any updates such as new calendar events can sync faster than ever!
