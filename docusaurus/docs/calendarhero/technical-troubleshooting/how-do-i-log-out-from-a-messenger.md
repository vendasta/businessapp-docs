title: How do I log out from a messenger?
sidebar_position: 7
---

**Did you add a chat platform you need to remove? **
 

  
You can go directly to your settings by clicking here: [https://app.calendarhero.com/settings/accounts/list#messenger](https://app.calendarhero.com/settings/accounts/list#messenger). Then click the "Remove" button. The next time you send a message to your assistant on the messenger that you removed, it should restart the experience from the beginning.
