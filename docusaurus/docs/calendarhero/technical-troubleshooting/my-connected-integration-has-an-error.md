CalendarHero integrates with over 60+ 3rd party providers. If one of your 3rd party integrations encounters an error, or if you remove access permissions for that integration an error message will appear. Learn more below on how to manage errors.   
  
If you have been recently prompted to upgrade your Microsoft providers [please view this article for more information](https://faq.calendarhero.com/hc/en-us/articles/4430401649307).
 

**Error Notifications: **
 

If your account has an error CalendarHero will be as proactive as possible to alert you to the error. Error alerts will appear on the [Dashboard](https://faq.calendarhero.com/hc/en-us/articles/4430389689371), on your [morning briefings](https://faq.calendarhero.com/hc/en-us/articles/4430394727195), and on your [My Integrations page](https://faq.calendarhero.com/article/173-where-can-i-view-my-connected-accounts) (associated with that provider on the Integration card).
 

  
For most errors, you may also receive an error message notification (by email [or your preferred notification platform](https://faq.calendarhero.com/hc/en-us/articles/4430395089435)) as soon as the error occurs. This is to help you proactively identify when a problem occurs that requires action. Ignoring the error may cause a core service such as scheduling to fail.
