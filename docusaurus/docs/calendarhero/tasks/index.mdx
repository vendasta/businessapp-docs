The [Tasks page](https://app.calendarhero.com/tasks/active) is where you manage all of your meetings after you've created a scheduling request. Every meeting you create appears here, organized by status.

## Task tabs

**Active** — pending meetings that have not yet been scheduled. These are waiting for invitees to respond.

**Upcoming** — meetings that have been successfully scheduled but haven't happened yet.

**Completed** — meetings that have already taken place, plus any pending or upcoming meetings that were canceled.

Use the filter at the top of any tab to view meetings by meeting type. A count next to each type shows how many you have.

## Active tasks

Active tasks give you a full picture of each pending request — creation date, duration, location, which invitees have responded, and which haven't. From an active task you can:

- **Cancel** — notify all invitees that the meeting is canceled
- **Reschedule request** — update the time frame or duration and re-invite invitees, or book a specific time directly
- **Send reminder** — manually resend the scheduling invite to invitees who haven't responded yet
- **Find Times** — have CalendarHero suggest the best time based on current invitee responses, even before everyone has replied. Click **… → Find Times**, then select a time and click **Book Now**.
- **Add / Remove invitees** — add or remove attendees after the request has been sent

Active reminders (created via chat) also appear on this tab. Cancel a reminder here to stop it from firing.

## Upcoming tasks

Upcoming tasks list your confirmed meetings. From an upcoming task you can:

- **Cancel** — remove the meeting from all calendars and notify attendees
- **Reschedule** — move the meeting to a new time (see [Rescheduling](#rescheduling) below)
- **Add / Remove invitees** — adjust the attendee list after the meeting is booked

## Rescheduling

CalendarHero handles all the details when you reschedule — updating calendar invites and notifying attendees automatically.

**From the web:** click **…** on any Active or Upcoming task card, then select **Reschedule** (or **Reschedule Request** for active tasks). You have two options:

- **Ask invitees for a new time** — use this when you don't know the exact new time. CalendarHero cancels the original meeting and sends a new scheduling invite asking invitees to select a time within the updated date range.
- **Reschedule to a specific time** — use this when you already know the new time. The meeting moves immediately and all attendees receive an automatic update. CalendarHero will flag a conflict if you choose a time you're already booked.

**From chat:** say *"reschedule the meeting"* or *"postpone the meeting."* CalendarHero will ask which meeting, then what to change. Specify a new exact time (e.g. *"August 8 at 10 am"*) to book it directly, or a range (e.g. *"next week"*) to re-invite invitees. Room bookings are automatically updated if you use the room booking integration.
