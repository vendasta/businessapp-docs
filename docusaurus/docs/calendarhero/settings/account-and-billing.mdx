## Account profile

Update your name, work location, and other personal details at [My Profile Settings](https://app.calendarhero.com/user/profile).

## Email addresses

You can add additional email addresses to your account — useful if you use aliases or want CalendarHero notifications delivered to a specific inbox.

**To add an email:**
1. Go to [My Profile Settings → Email](https://app.calendarhero.com/settings/user#Email)
2. Enter the new address in the **Additional Email** field and click **Add Email**
3. Check your inbox for a verification code, enter it, and click **Validate Code**

Once added, you can set the new email as your preferred notification address. If it's a Google or Microsoft email, you can also use it to log in.

**To remove an email:**
Click the red **X** next to the address in your [email settings](https://app.calendarhero.com/settings/user#Email). If an email doesn't have an X, it was added automatically by an integration — remove the associated integration to remove the email.

To remove your original login email, [contact support](https://calendarhero.com/get-support).

## Plans and billing

View your current plan and upgrade options at [Settings → Billing](https://app.calendarhero.com/settings/billing).

The **Billing Admin** is the team plan administrator responsible for managing the organization's subscription and billing information (processed via Stripe).

## Cancelling your free trial

When your trial ends, your account automatically downgrades to the free Basic plan — no action needed. If you prefer to delete your account entirely, go to [My Profile Settings](https://app.calendarhero.com/user/profile) and click **Delete Your CalendarHero Account**.

## Cancelling a Team plan

To cancel a Team plan, an admin must remove all members one by one from the Users section under the organization-wide settings. Select **…** next to each member's name and choose **Remove from team** — their account downgrades to Basic. Remove the admin last, as they lose org-wide access once removed.

The subscription is cancelled once all users and admins have been removed. Note: there is no direct downgrade path from Team to Professional. If a user wants a Professional plan after leaving the team, they can upgrade after being removed.

## Automatic refunds

If you collect payments through CalendarHero, you can enable **Automatic Refunds** per meeting type. When enabled, a refund is automatically issued when the meeting creator cancels the meeting by clicking **Cancel** on the Task card.

Refunds are **not** automatically issued when an invitee declines — the meeting creator must click **Cancel** on the Task card to trigger the refund. All refunds are processed through your connected Stripe account. CalendarHero notifies the meeting creator when a refund is issued.

## AppSumo

**Redeeming codes:** Go to [calendarhero.com/appsumo](https://calendarhero.com/appsumo) to redeem your code. A single code gives you the Professional plan (1 user). Each additional code adds one more user to a Team plan (e.g. 2 codes = 3 users, 3 codes = 4 users). There is no limit on total codes, but a maximum of 10 can be purchased at once.

**Upgrading from Professional to Team:** Go to [Settings → Billing](https://app.calendarhero.com/settings/billing) and redeem an additional code. You must be registered with a work email address (not @gmail.com) to create a Team plan.

**Changing your AppSumo email:** The email used to register your AppSumo account cannot be changed — it's tied to your codes by design. If you need to change it, delete your account (which releases your codes) and re-register with the new email at [calendarhero.com/appsumo](https://calendarhero.com/appsumo).

## Deleting your account

Go to [My Profile Settings](https://app.calendarhero.com/user/profile) and click **Delete Your CalendarHero Account**. When your account is deleted, all stored data — including third-party authorizations — is permanently erased.

## Frequently asked questions

<details>
<summary>Can I export my contacts from CalendarHero?</summary>

Yes. You can export contacts via the [CalendarHero API](https://api.calendarhero.com/documentation) or through a [Zapier](https://zapier.com/apps/zoomai/integrations) integration.

</details>

<details>
<summary>Can I add my Facebook contacts?</summary>

No. Facebook contacts are not supported because CalendarHero cannot access their email addresses, which are required for scheduling.

</details>

<details>
<summary>Can I hide invitee email addresses in the meeting invitation?</summary>

No. CalendarHero always shows all invitee email addresses in the meeting invitation — there is no option to hide them.

</details>

<details>
<summary>Can I disable the email sent when a meeting is cancelled or an invitee is removed?</summary>

No. These automated emails cannot be turned off. When a meeting is cancelled or an invitee is removed, CalendarHero always sends them an email notification.

</details>
