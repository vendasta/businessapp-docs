Your CalendarHero meeting assistant is 100% AI-powered — it works around the clock without any human involvement. These settings let you customize how it presents itself and what it knows.

## Personalize your assistant

Your assistant sends meeting invites on your behalf, so its name and title appear in all outgoing emails. Customize these at [Settings → Assistant](https://app.calendarhero.com/settings/assistant).

- **Assistant's name** — choose any name you like (e.g. "Aria", "Alex"). This is what invitees see in automated emails.
- **Job title** — choose between "Assistant" or "Automated Assistant". We recommend "Automated Assistant" so invitees aren't confused about whether they're interacting with a person.
- **Custom email footer** — add a footer that appears in all meeting invite emails sent by your assistant.

Team admins can set these values org-wide from [Admin Settings](https://app.calendarhero.com/org/settings), and optionally lock them so individual users can't override.

## Learned preferences

When a setting shows **Learned**, it means CalendarHero used your data to infer a preference — such as your typical meeting duration or location. Learned values are often incorrect. Leave **Learned** unchecked and enter the correct value manually to ensure CalendarHero uses the right setting.

## People insights

People Insights (also called "Who Is") lets you quickly look up public information about anyone you're about to meet with. It returns available public data including:

- LinkedIn, X, Facebook, and Medium profiles
- Website and recent blog posts
- Current organization and recent news
- Your meeting history with that person
- CRM or ATS data (if connected)

You can also view contact information from the [Contacts page](https://app.calendarhero.com/contacts/list) in the web app.

## Weather

Your assistant can provide current weather and forecasts for your work location or any other location. To include a daily weather update in your morning briefing, set the forecast time in [General Meeting Settings](https://app.calendarhero.com/settings/meeting/general). 

Update your temperature unit preference (°F or °C) at [My Account → My Profile Settings → Weather](https://app.calendarhero.com/settings/user#Weather).

## Frequently asked questions

<details>
<summary>Is the assistant controlled by a human at any point?</summary>

No. The assistant is fully automated and AI-powered. It operates 24/7, including nights and weekends, without any human involvement.

</details>

<details>
<summary>Can I stop the assistant from sending messages on my behalf?</summary>

The assistant's outgoing invite message includes a fixed component that cannot be removed. The second part — meeting instructions or context — is optional and can be customized or left blank per meeting type.

</details>

<details>
<summary>Where does People Insights data come from?</summary>

Information comes from publicly available external sources — not from CalendarHero or your private accounts. CRM and ATS data is sourced from your connected integrations (HubSpot, Salesforce, Greenhouse, and others).

</details>
