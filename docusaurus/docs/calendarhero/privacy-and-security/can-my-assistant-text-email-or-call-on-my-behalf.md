---
title: Can my assistant text, email, or call on my behalf?
sidebar_position: 1
---

Your CalendarHero Meeting Assistant will only send emails to your invitees in order to schedule a meeting on your behalf. For example, when you create a new meeting request your meeting assistant can automatically send an email invite to all invitees with a link. You can choose to be [Cc'd on these emails](https://faq.calendarhero.com/hc/en-us/articles/4430401967643) and also [customize the email invite sent](https://faq.calendarhero.com/hc/en-us/articles/4430391175451). 
 

However, if you choose to share a [Private Meeting Link](https://faq.calendarhero.com/hc/en-us/articles/4430390584987) or a [Personal Scheduling link](https://faq.calendarhero.com/hc/en-us/articles/4430395807643) then no email invite is sent from your assistant. You can control how you share the meeting request invite.
 

Follow-up email notifications will be sent to the invitee to remind them about the meeting.
