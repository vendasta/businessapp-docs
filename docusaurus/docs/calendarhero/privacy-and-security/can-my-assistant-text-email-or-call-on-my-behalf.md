---
title: Can my assistant text, email, or call on my behalf?
sidebar_position: 1
---

Your CalendarHero Meeting Assistant will only send emails to your invitees in order to schedule a meeting on your behalf. For example, when you create a new meeting request your meeting assistant can automatically send an email invite to all invitees with a link. You can choose to be Cc'd on these emails and also customize the email invite sent. 

However, if you choose to share a Private Meeting Link or a Personal Scheduling link then no email invite is sent from your assistant. You can control how you share the meeting request invite.

Follow-up email notifications will be sent to the invitee to remind them about the meeting.
