#### Big News: CalendarHero has been acquired!
 

####  [Read the full CEO announcement here.](https://calendarhero.com/blog/calendarhero-is-joining-vendasta) 
 

### FAQ
 

#### **Is the CalendarHero product going away? **
 

 

Nope! CalendarHero users can continue to use the product as they always have. CalendarHero will continue to add new features and improve the product, with the support of Vendasta.
 

 
  

 

 

#### **What’s changing?**
 

You can expect CalendarHero’s ability to integrate with Vendasta to grow over time. We see great value in offering our meeting scheduler to Vendasta’s channel partners and customers.
   

   
 

 

#### **As a CalendarHero user, is there anything I need to do? **
 

No! Keep scheduling and being awesome. 
   

   
 

 

#### **Is my pricing changing?**
 

There are no plans to change our existing pricing. CalendarHero regularly assesses pricing and packaging to ensure we are competitive in the market. 
   

   
 

 

#### **What is Vendasta anyhow?**
 

Vendasta provides an end-to-end e-commerce platform to experts who deliver digital products and services to local businesses worldwide. Vendasta is trusted by 60,000+ channel partners serving more than 5.5 million small and medium-sized businesses. Vendasta aims to make local economies around the world better! [Learn more](https://www.vendasta.com/)
