---
title: Can CalendarHero automatically send out the daily schedule the night before the upcoming workday?
sidebar_position: 2
---

No, CalendarHero does not have the functionality to automatically send out the daily schedule the night before the upcoming workday. The earliest notification option available is for 5 AM on the same workday.

You can find more information about this feature in the following article: [CalendarHero Daily Schedule Notification](/calendarhero/what-can-calendarhero-do/does-my-assistant-notify-me-of-upcoming-meetings)
