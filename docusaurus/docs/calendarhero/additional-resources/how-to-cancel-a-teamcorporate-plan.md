---
title: How to cancel a Team/Corporate Plan
sidebar_position: 4
---

To cancel your Corporate/Team plan, a user set as an administrator for your organization will navigate to the **Users** section under the Organization-wide options of their dashboard. To remove a team member, the user must select the **three dots** adjacent to the member's name and opt for the **Remove from team** option. Once this action is executed, the member's account will be downgraded to a Basic subscription.
