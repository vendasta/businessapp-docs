---
title: Can I Export Contacts From CalendarHero?
sidebar_position: 8
---

CalendarHero users who wish to export contacts from the platform can do so via our [API](https://support.calendarhero.com/hc/en-us/articles/14078091567639-Is-there-a-CalendarHero-API-) or [Zapier](https://support.calendarhero.com/hc/en-us/articles/14078867285143-Using-Add-Ons-including-Zapier-) integration!
