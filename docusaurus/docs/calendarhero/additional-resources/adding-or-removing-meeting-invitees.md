title: Adding or removing meeting invitees
sidebar_position: 9
---

Sometimes plans change, and you realize that you should have invited someone else to your meeting. Not to worry! You can easily **add or remove meeting attendees** either after you've sent the meeting request OR even after the meeting is already scheduled.   
  
Looking for more info on adding invitees to the initial meeting request? [Learn how to schedule a meeting.](https://support.calendarhero.com/hc/en-us/articles/14081616167191-How-do-I-Schedule-Meetings-)
 

#### Task Card: Add/Remove Invitees
 

To get started go to your [Active or Upcoming Task card](https://support.calendarhero.com/hc/en-us/articles/14081566427159-How-to-manage-your-Meetings-from-Tasks) and select "Add / Remove Invitees" from the "..." menu.
