title: Scheduling error on an Upcoming Task card
sidebar_position: 10
---

If you see a *Scheduling Error *on your [Upcoming Task card](/calendarhero/scheduling-meetings/how-to-manage-your-meetings-from-tasks) then this means a scheduled meeting has failed to add to your [booking calendar](/calendarhero/how-to/how-do-i-add-and-customize-my-calendars)due to a 3rd party calendar integration error. You should have also received an email notifying you of the error.  
  
Steps to take:

1. Remove your failed calendar integration from "My Integrations" ([https://app.calendarhero.com/settings/accounts/list](https://app.calendarhero.com/settings/accounts/list))

2. Re-add your calendar integration from the "Integration Directory" ([https://app.calendarhero.com/settings/accounts/add#calendar](https://app.calendarhero.com/settings/accounts/add#calendar)). Re-adding should resolve the error. 

3. Return to your Task Card (after re-adding your integration) and click "Add to Calendar". This will add the meeting to your booking calendar. 

If you continue to experience issues please [contact Support](https://calendarhero.com/get-support).
