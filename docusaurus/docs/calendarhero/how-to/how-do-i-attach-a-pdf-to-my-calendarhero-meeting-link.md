---
title: How do I attach a PDF to my CalendarHero meeting link?
sidebar_position: 1
---

There is no way to directly attach a PDF to a meeting link, however, users can provide a link to a webpage or online form in the "invitee Experience" section of a meeting type.

To do this navigate to the Invitee Experience section of the meeting's settings > Add a question > Select "Consent Form" from the dropdown > In the information box hyperlink the webpage or space where the client can complete the form > Save. To make this form more visible you can set this question as required.
