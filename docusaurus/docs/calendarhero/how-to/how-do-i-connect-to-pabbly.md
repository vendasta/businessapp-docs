The team over at [Pabbly](https://www.pabbly.com/) has added a CalendarHero integration. Triggers include *New Contact Added,* *New Meeting Requests,* and more.
 

Check it out and set up your integration here: [https://www.pabbly.com/connect/integrations/zoom/calendarhero/](https://www.pabbly.com/connect/integrations/zoom/calendarhero/)
