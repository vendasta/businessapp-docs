---
title: How do I manage and sync my People/Contacts?
sidebar_position: 22
---

CalendarHero imports your contacts so that it knows who you ask to schedule meetings with and can reach out on your behalf to invite those contacts to your meetings.  

You can manage your list of contacts from [https://app.calendarhero.com/contacts/list.](https://app.calendarhero.com/contacts/list)This list includes all contacts imported from your connected accounts or those manually added. You can edit the contact information if needed. 

CalendarHero automatically syncs contacts from your calendar when you set up your account, and regularly syncs them to keep things up to date. Import additional contacts by adding other 3rd party accounts such as your favorite ATS or CRM. Add new integrations here: [https://app.calendarhero.com/settings/accounts/add#contacts](https://app.calendarhero.com/settings/accounts/add#contacts)  
  
Learn more about People Insights - People Insights is a quick and easy way to find information about people you're about to meet with.
