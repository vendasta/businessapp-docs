title: How do I customize my Availability Window?
sidebar_position: 26
---

CalendarHero makes it easy for you to choose when you want to be available for each [Meeting Type](https://support.calendarhero.com/hc/en-us/articles/14081615551255-Create-and-customize-a-Meeting-Type). The **Availability Window** is a powerful feature that gives you control over the days and times each meeting type can be scheduled. Customize your calendar schedule for the way you want to work!
 

**Availability Window includes the following options:**
 
 
-  **Time** - Is there a time of day you prefer for these types of meetings to happen? Simply set the hours, or add time ranges with different hours and invitees won't be able to select time outside this range. Don't make this too narrow if you are meeting with external clients, as it will limit the time offered to them. 
-  **Day** - Are there specific days of the week you prefer for these types of meetings to happen? Simply select the days you want to offer availability and omit those you don't. 
-  **Customize Times *per* Day** - Set different availability per day *plus *set different ranges for different days of the week. For example create a meeting type that offers availability on Mondays, Tuesdays, and Wednesdays from 9 am - 5 pm, on Thursdays from 9 am - 12 pm *and* 2 - 7 pm, and Fridays as "No availability". 
 

**Use Case Examples:**
 
 
- create dedicated days for client training sessions - e.g. only Mondays and Wednesday 
- set a wide availability window for sales demos from 8:00 am - 7:00 pm to accommodate new leads, but then limit internal team meetings to only occur between 10:00 am - 4:00 pm for more work-life balance 
- make sure you get time for a well-deserved lunch break by customizing time ranges that block off lunchtime  
- allow screening interviews to take place 7 days a week, to ensure flexibility during peak hiring  
- block of Fridays as "No Meeting Days" so you have dedicated time to catch up on other work 
 

**Plan Access:**
 
 
- While all plans have access to* Availability Window*, only users on the Pro and Team plans can add multiple time ranges per day - [Compare Plans](https://calendarhero.com/pricing)  
 

**Meeting Type: Availability Window**
 

For all your Meeting Types you can customize your *Availability Window *to control when invitees can book associated meetings with you. Since CalendarHero always syncs with your [real-time calendar availability](https://support.calendarhero.com/hc/en-us/articles/14078314795543-How-do-I-add-and-customize-my-Calendars-) any *Availability *settings are always layered on top of your real-time free availability. 
 

Customize your *Availability Window* from the "*Availability* " tab. To edit your regular availability for that meeting type simply click on any day in the week. A pop-up window will appear allowing you to customize the time ranges for that day. 
 
 
- To omit an entire day toggle "Available" to off. Days marked as "Not available" will NOT be included in the availability offered to your invitees.  
- To add new time ranges click "+Add a time range".  
- To create a block simply omit that time using time ranges. For example, to set a 12-1 pm lunch block during a typical 9-5 pm day set your range to 9:00 am - 12:00 pm AND 1:00 pm - 5:00 pm.
