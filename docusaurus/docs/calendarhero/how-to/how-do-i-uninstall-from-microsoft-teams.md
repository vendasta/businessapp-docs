---
title: How do I uninstall from Microsoft Teams?
sidebar_position: 8
---

To uninstall your CalendarHero Meeting Assistant from Microsoft Teams please follow the flow below:

- Select "..." from the left navigation bar in MS Teams

- Select the App you want to install

- Click "Uninstall" 

If you are uninstalling because you are having issues - [please contact us](https://calendarhero.com/get-support) and we would love to help!
