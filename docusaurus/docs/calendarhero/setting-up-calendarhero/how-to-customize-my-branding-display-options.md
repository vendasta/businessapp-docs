CalendarHero makes it easy to customize your invitee experience from *My Profile >Branding & Display.  
*Update these settings by going to: https://app.calendarhero.com/settings/user#Branding

Display options currently include:

- Customizing your availability calendar display - [Learn More](/calendarhero/scheduling-meetings/customizing-the-calendar-display-for-my-invitees)

- Adding a custom logo to the invitee experience - [Learn More](/calendarhero/scheduling-meetings/how-do-i-customize-the-branding-on-the-invitee-experience)

- Hide the footer on the invitee experience - [Learn More](/calendarhero/scheduling-meetings/how-do-i-customize-the-branding-on-the-invitee-experience)
