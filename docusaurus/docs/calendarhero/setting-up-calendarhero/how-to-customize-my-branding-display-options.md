sidebar_position: 17

CalendarHero makes it easy to customize your invitee experience from *My Profile >* *Branding & Display.  
*Update these settings by going to: https://app.calendarhero.com/settings/user#Branding
 

**Display options currently include:**
 
 
- Customizing your availability calendar display - [Learn More](https://support.calendarhero.com/hc/en-us/articles/14079321141655-Customizing-the-calendar-display-for-my-invitees)  
- Adding a custom logo to the invitee experience - [Learn More](https://support.calendarhero.com/hc/en-us/articles/14081564941975-How-do-I-customize-the-branding-on-the-invitee-experience-)  
- Hide the footer on the invitee experience - [Learn More](https://support.calendarhero.com/hc/en-us/articles/14081564941975-How-do-I-customize-the-branding-on-the-invitee-experience-)
