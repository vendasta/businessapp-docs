---
title: How do I customize email footers?
sidebar_position: 16
---

CalendarHero [sends emails to invitees](/calendarhero/scheduling-meetings/what-emailsreminders-are-sent-to-my-invitees)(on behalf of the meeting creator) to invite them to your meeting. In addition, we also send reminders on your behalf, if a meeting request is still waiting for a response. 

To personalize the emails sent to your invitees you can customize the assistant email footer from [your Profile Settings](https://app.calendarhero.com/settings/user). Simply select the "My Assistant" tab and enter your custom email copy.

When you add a custom email footer it will be *added* to the current default email footer. Therefore, the custom email footer is useful for adding additional context to your email templates.   
  
Team plan admins can also customize the footer and [meeting request email](/calendarhero/how-does-calendarhero-work/can-i-customize-my-meeting-request-invites)for the entire organization from ([Admin Settings](https://app.calendarhero.com/org/settings) Email). If a footer is customized by an admin, this customization will override any user-level customization.
