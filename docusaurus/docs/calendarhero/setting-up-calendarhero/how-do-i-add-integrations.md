Add New Integrations

Whether you want to add Slack, Hubspot, Zoom.us, or Mailchimp - CalendarHero makes it easy with our Integration Directory. Most integrations take less than five minutes to add and require only a click or two to get set up!

- Individual users (on any plan) can add new integrations by logging into their account and heading to the [Integration Directory](https://app.calendarhero.com/settings/accounts/add) Select a category or use the Search bar to find your favorite apps.

- Admins (on the Team plan) can add integrations on behalf of members of their organization from the [Team Plan: Integration Directory](https://app.calendarhero.com/org/accounts/add) This makes it easy to add 3rd party providers for all members in your Team plan. 

- Admins (on the Team plan) can restrict which integrations can be added for their organization from the [Team Admin Settings (Integrations tab)](https://app.calendarhero.com/org/settings). Scroll down for more info.

- Looking to build a custom integration or workflow? CalendarHero also offers an[API](/calendarhero/setting-up-calendarhero/is-there-a-calendarhero-api) and [Extensions](/calendarhero/what-can-calendarhero-do/adding-extensions-and-custom-applications) via Zapier, MS Flow, and Workato.

[Managing Installed Integrations](/calendarhero/setting-up-calendarhero/how-do-i-view-my-installed-integrations) - Want to view and manage the integrations you have already added? Head to your "[My Integrations](https://app.calendarhero.com/settings/accounts/list)" to view your installed Integrations, messengers, and plugins.

Integration Legend

Each Integration includes a legend representing the type of integration that it supports. For example:


[Calendar integrations](/calendarhero/how-does-calendarhero-work/how-to-set-a-default-calendar) are a core integration required by CalendarHero. Your "Default" calendar will be used for scheduling your meetings (e.g. booked meetings will appear in this calendar). Other (non-default) connected calendars will be used to check your availability only.


 [Contact integrations](/calendarhero/how-to/how-do-i-manage-and-sync-my-peoplecontacts) import your contacts so you can easily schedule meetings with them


[Group integrations](/calendarhero/scheduling-meetings/how-do-i-create-teamsgroups-for-meetings) import your groups of users/contacts so that you can schedule meetings with a group

  


 [Video integrations](/calendarhero/what-can-calendarhero-do/how-do-i-connect-my-video-conference-software) can automatically add a video conference to your meetings when scheduling in CalendarHero

  


[Room Booking integrations](/calendarhero/how-does-calendarhero-work/how-does-room-booking-work) are used to reserve a conference room when you schedule a meeting

  


 Plugins and extensions integrations allow you to use CalendarHero inside your inbox, browser, or Zapier

  


[Payment collection integrations](/calendarhero/what-can-calendarhero-do/how-to-collect-payments-for-your-meetings) allow you to collect payment when a user schedules a meeting with you.

 


[CRM integrations](/calendarhero/how-to/how-do-i-integrate-my-crm) allow searching CRM information, pushing meeting information to relevant CRM objects, and add in CRM information to meeting briefings

 


[ATS integrations](/calendarhero/setting-up-calendarhero/lever-ats-api-key)allow you to synchronize candidates and push meeting information into your ATS

 


[Search integrations](/calendarhero/how-does-calendarhero-work/how-does-unified-search-work) can be searched for many types of objects (files, events, deals, ...) using Unified Search

 


File integrations are used in Unified Search and Document Generation

 


[FAQ / Knowledge Base integrations](/calendarhero/how-does-calendarhero-work/what-is-knowledge-base-faq-and-how-does-it-work) allow you to search the knowledge base in Unified Search and in chat

 

Team Admins: Restricting Integrations

By default Team plan members can add any integration available in their individual [Integration Directory](https://app.calendarhero.com/settings/accounts/add). If your organization would prefer to restrict access then the Team Admin can hide specific integrations.

To restrict access:

1. Go to the Team Admin - Settings Integrations tab ([https://app.calendarhero.com/org/settings](https://app.calendarhero.com/org/settings))

2. To disable specific integrations select "Some" and then deselect the integration from the list. 

3. Click "Save" to apply the changes.

Important:

- Restricting an integration will hide that integration from appearing in the members' integration directory only. Other Team Admins can still [enable corporate-wide integrations](https://app.calendarhero.com/org/accounts/list). 

- If an integration was already added (before it was restricted) then it will still appear in a member's [Installed Integration list](https://app.calendarhero.com/settings/accounts/list), however, the member will no longer see it in the [Integration Directory](https://app.calendarhero.com/settings/accounts/add).

- If there are no integrations available for team members, the category will show the message: "Integrations in this category have been disabled for your organization by your Team Plan Administrator."
