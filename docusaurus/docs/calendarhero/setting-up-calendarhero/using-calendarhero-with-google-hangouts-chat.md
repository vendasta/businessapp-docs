Not sure which Google chat platform you are using? 

Back in 2015, Google announced it would be discontinuing Google Talk (also known as “Gchat”) and instructed users to migrate to the Google Hangouts app on the Chrome browser. As part of this transition, Google stopped supporting bots on Gtalk. As of 2019 Google is once again transitioning users from classic Hangouts to their latest chat platform - Hangouts Chat.

*We recommend using CalendarHero with Hangouts Chat. As required by Google - you will need a Gsuite account to use Hangouts Chat. *

 


Setting up CalendarHero on Google Hangouts Chat

1. If you haven't already - register for your CalendarHero account here: [Get Started with CalendarHero](https://calendarhero.com/start)

2. Next, open Google Hangouts Chat (either in the desktop version or in your browser via [https://chat.google.com/](https://chat.google.com/)). If you are not signed into Google, sign in.  *Note: You need a G Suite account to access Hangouts Chat.*

3. Click on the Hangouts Search box and select "Find a Bot."

4. Search the bots directory for "CalendarHero" (formerly "ZoomAI"). When it appears select it.

5. From the CalendarHero listing click "Message." This will add the CalendarHero Assistant to your list of Hangouts Bots. It will appear as "CalendarHero under your Bots list." 

  1. If you have already registered with CalendarHero ([from our web app](https://calendarhero.com/start)) then your assistant should say hi to you in chat.

  2. If you haven't registered follow the instructions provided by your assistant in chat. 

  3. Not heard from your assistant? Say hi to get started.

6. Whenever you want to use your CalendarHero assistant in Hangouts chat simply click on the CalendarHero and chat with your assistant! It's that easy. [Learn more about scheduling meetings in chat.](/calendarhero/scheduling-meetings/how-do-i-schedule-meetings)





Removing CalendarHero from Google Hangouts Chat

1. If you need to remove CalendarHero from Hangouts Chat simply mouse over the CalendarHero  (formerly "ZoomAI") listing under "Bots," click the "..." menu and select "Remove."
