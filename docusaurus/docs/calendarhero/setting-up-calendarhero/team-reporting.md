title: Team Reporting
sidebar_position: 9
---

Team plan administrators can easily view and download report data from their Admin Reports dashboard - [https://app.calendarhero.com/org/reports](https://app.calendarhero.com/org/reports).
 

Reports can be generated by month, week, or day. Once you selected your time period click "View" to view the results in the dashboard, or "Download" to download a CSV.
