Pre-populate your Invitee information on the Scheduling Page with Name/Email 

Want to pass information from a lead form to pre-fill your prospect's CalendarHero scheduling page?

When you share your CalendarHero scheduling link with an invitee or post the link on your website, you can have CalendarHero automatically display information that's specific to your invitee - such as their name and email. This saves your invitee from once again filling in this information.  

You can customize your scheduling link to pre-populate the invitee's name and email by adding these values to the URL, for example:

```
https://calendarhero.to/customname?email=jane@recruiterhawk.comi&name=Jane Skywalker
	
```

You may also include a form on your website that will take invitees to the CalendarHero scheduling page with their name and email pre-populated, for example:

```
*    
Name:  
     
  
E-mail:  
   
  
   
  

*

```
