---
title: How do I add CalendarHero to Webex Messaging?
sidebar_position: 14
---

CalendarHero supports Webex Messaging as a chat provider. Simply chat with your A.I. meeting assistant in natural language, just like you would a real human assistant.  

 

** Get started with Webex Messaging:**
 
 
1. Invite calendarhero@webex.bot into your Webex Messaging account 
2. Say 'hello!' If our bot was added in a group room, say '@calendarhero hello' 
3. Follow your CalendarHero assistant's instructions to sign in and start delegating scheduling tasks 
    

Or visit the CalendarHero listing in the Webex App Hub - to add your assistant from there:   
[https://apphub.webex.com/teams/applications/zoom-ai-meeting-assistant-zoom-ai-inc](https://apphub.webex.com/teams/applications/zoom-ai-meeting-assistant-zoom-ai-inc)
