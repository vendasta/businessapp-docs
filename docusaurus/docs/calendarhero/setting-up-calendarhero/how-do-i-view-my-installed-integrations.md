CalendarHero integrates with over 60+ applications, including your favorite calendar, contacts, chat platforms, video conferencing, CRM, and ATS platforms! [View the Integration Overview](https://calendarhero.com/integrations)

The "Installed Integrations" page ( [https://app.calendarhero.com/settings/accounts](https://app.calendarhero.com/settings/accounts)) details all 3rd party integrations you currently have connected to CalendarHero. Here you can view a list of each integration and details on the current status.

Integration Legend

Each Integration will include a legend representing the type of integration that it supports. For example:


[Calendar integrations](/calendarhero/how-to/how-do-i-add-and-customize-my-calendars) are a core integration required by CalendarHero. Your "Default" calendar will be used for scheduling your meetings (e.g. booked meetings will appear in this calendar). Other (non-default) connected calendars will be used to check your availability only.


[Contact integrations](/calendarhero/how-to/how-do-i-manage-and-sync-my-peoplecontacts)import your contacts so you can easily schedule meetings with them

 


[Group integrations](/calendarhero/scheduling-meetings/can-i-schedule-a-group-meeting-with-multiple-attendees) import your groups of users/contacts so that you can schedule meetings with a group

  


[Video integrations](/calendarhero/what-can-calendarhero-do/how-do-i-connect-my-video-conference-software) can automatically add a video conference to your meetings when scheduling in CalendarHero


[Room Booking integrations](/calendarhero/how-does-calendarhero-work/how-does-room-booking-work) are used to reserve a conference room when you schedule a meeting.

  


Plugins and extensions integrations allow you to use CalendarHero inside your inbox, browser, or Zapier.

  


[Payment collection integrations](/calendarhero/what-can-calendarhero-do/how-to-collect-payments-for-your-meetings) allow you to collect payment when a user schedules a meeting with you.  

[CRM integrations](/calendarhero/how-to/how-do-i-integrate-my-crm) allow searching CRM information, pushing meeting information to relevant CRM objects, and adding CRM information to meeting briefings.


[ATS integrations](/calendarhero/what-can-calendarhero-do/how-do-i-integrate-my-ats-applicant-tracking-system) allow you to synchronize candidates and push meeting information into your ATS.

 


[Search integrations](/calendarhero/how-does-calendarhero-work/how-does-unified-search-work) can be searched for many types of objects (files, events, deals, ...) using Unified Search.


File integrations are used in Unified Search and Document Generation.


[FAQ/Knowledge Base integrations](/calendarhero/how-does-calendarhero-work/what-is-knowledge-base-faq-and-how-does-it-work) allow you to search the knowledge base in Unified Search and in chat.

 

Add New Integrations

Whether you want to add Slack, Hubspot, Zoom.us, or Mailchimp - CalendarHero makes it easy with our Integration Directory. Most integrations take less than 5 minutes to add and require only a click or two to get set up!

- Individual users (on any plan) can add new integrations by logging into their account and heading to the [Integration Directory](https://app.calendarhero.com/settings/accounts/add) Select a category or use the Search bar to find your favorite apps.

- Admins (on the Team plan) can add integrations for their organization from the [Team Plan: Integration Directory](https://app.calendarhero.com/org/accounts/add) This makes it easy to add 3rd party providers for all members in your Team plan! 

- Looking to build a custom integration or workflow? CalendarHero also offers an[API](/calendarhero/setting-up-calendarhero/is-there-a-calendarhero-api) and [Extensions](/calendarhero/setting-up-calendarhero/how-do-i-add-integrations) via Zapier, MS Flow, and Workato.
