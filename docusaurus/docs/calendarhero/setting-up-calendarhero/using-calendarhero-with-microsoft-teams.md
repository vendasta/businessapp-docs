Setting up CalendarHero on *MSTeams*

To add CalendarHero to MS Team [visit the CalendarHero Getting Started page](https://calendarhero.com/microsoft-teams/) and simply click "install" to quickly start the App installation process. You can also [go directly here to launch the installer](https://teams.microsoft.com/dl/launcher/launcher.html?url=%2f_%23%2fl%2fapp%2fcac7469b-37cc-44f5-bf08-ff6654d35819&type=app&deeplinkId=8a944408-b61f-4b88-9f93-318386aae4d8) and follow the instructions.

Alternatively to install directly from inside MS Teams simply locate CalendarHero via the Teams Apps directory (found at the bottom left corner of the Teams screen). Once in the App Store directory, you'll be able to search for CalendarHero. Once you locate the app click on the card and then you will see the related app information and an "install" button. Click Card  Click the Install button.

Once installed your Meeting Assistant will be available under the "..." menu. Click on CalendarHero (Meeting Assistant) and say hi to your new assistant!

Troubleshooting:

- MS Teams owners can restrict who can add apps to a team. Contact your IT Admin if you suspect they have disabled your ability to add Apps to your organization's MS Teams.

What are the CalendarHero "tabs" on MS Teams?

Microsoft users can access key CalendarHero settings inside Microsoft Teams by clicking on the integrated CalendarHeroTeams tabs. Users may be prompted to first sign into CalendarHero with their linked Microsoft account. 

These tabs provide a view of the CalendarHero web app so that users can more quickly access core CalendarHero settings inside MSTeams - specifically  *Dashboard*, *Settings*, *Tasks,* and *Skills Help*. For full CalendarHero functionality, we recommend opening [app.calendarhero.com](https://app.calendarhero.com/) in your browser. 

Please note that due to integration limitations, these MS Teams tabs (*Dashboard*, *Settings*, *Tasks,* and *Skills Help*) are only accessible to CalendarHero users who log in with Microsoft. If you cannot access these tabs (e.g. you use CalendarHero with Google) don't worry! You can continue to use the CalendarHero Automated Assistant via chat in MSTeams (to make meeting requests in natural language), and simply access the additional CalendarHero functionality in any web browser using the full CalendarHero Web App ([app.calendarhero.com](https://app.calendarhero.com/))
