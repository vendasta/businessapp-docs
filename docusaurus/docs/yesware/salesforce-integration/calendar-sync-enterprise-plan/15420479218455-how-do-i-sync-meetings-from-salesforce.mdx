# How do I sync meetings from Salesforce into Google Calendar?

To synchronize events *from* Salesforce *to* Google Calendar, your Salesforce administrator must install a Salesforce package, which our Support Team can supply you. Please use the "Submit a request" option in the top-right corner of this page to request the package from our Support Team.
