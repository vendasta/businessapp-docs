# What does Yesware tracking track?

Yesware tracks who opened your email, when it was opened, what type of device they used to read it, and in some cases, where they opened it.
**To learn more: **[Email Tracking 101 for Outlook (O365)](/docs/tracking/getting-started-with-tracking/send-tracked-emails-and-links-on-outlook) 
Yesware can also track links clicked in emails if you enable [link tracking](https://support.yesware.com/hc/en-us/articles/15420627399831) and we also offer [attachment](https://support.yesware.com/hc/en-us/articles/15420528296471-How-do-I-add-attachments-to-Templates-and-or-Campaigns-) tracking.
