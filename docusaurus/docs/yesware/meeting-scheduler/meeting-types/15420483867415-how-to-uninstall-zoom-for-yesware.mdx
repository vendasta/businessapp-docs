# How to Uninstall Zoom for Yesware

**To uninstall the Yesware || Zoom integration:**
- Log into your *Zoom* account and navigate to the Zoom App Marketplace
- Click Manage &gt; Installed Apps or search for the Yesware app
- Click the Yesware app
- Click Uninstall
