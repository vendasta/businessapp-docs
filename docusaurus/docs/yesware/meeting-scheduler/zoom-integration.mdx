# Zoom Integration

Yesware's Zoom integration automatically inserts Zoom meeting details when someone books a time through Meeting Scheduler. Available on **Premium and Enterprise plans** for both Gmail and Outlook, and works with free or paid Zoom accounts.

## Connecting Your Zoom Account

1. Go to your [Meeting Scheduler Settings](https://app.yesware.com/meeting_scheduler)
2. Click **Edit Details** on your My Calendar Link
3. Under **Web Conferencing**, find the Zoom option:
   - **Gmail**: Click **Connect Zoom Account**
   - **Outlook**: Click **Link Account** under Zoom
4. Complete the connection in the popup window — it closes automatically when successful

## Adding Zoom to a Meeting Type

1. Open an existing meeting type or create a new one
2. Go to **Meeting Location**
3. Click **Virtual Meeting** and select **Zoom**
4. A Zoom link will be auto-generated whenever that meeting type is booked

## Adding Zoom to Custom Availability Links

When creating a Custom Availability link, click the **Zoom** button in the **Location** section. The Zoom link is automatically included when the recipient books.

## What the Recipient Sees

Once a booking is confirmed, the Zoom meeting details appear in the calendar invite sent to all attendees.

:::note
If your Zoom account is set to use a Personal Meeting ID (PMI), Yesware will use it. Otherwise, a unique Zoom ID is generated per meeting (recommended for security). Password-protected meetings are only supported if your Zoom admin has enabled passwords at the org level. Zoom settings cannot be managed within Yesware on a per-meeting basis.
:::

## Disconnecting Zoom

1. Log in to your Zoom account and go to the [Zoom App Marketplace](https://marketplace.zoom.us/)
2. Click **Manage > Installed Apps** or search for Yesware
3. Click the Yesware app and select **Uninstall**

## Frequently Asked Questions

<details>
<summary>Zoom links are missing from my scheduled meetings — how do I fix this?</summary>

Re-establish the connection by following these steps:

1. Go to **My Calendar Link Settings > Web Conferencing > Zoom** and click **Unlink Zoom Account**
2. For each meeting type, go to **Meeting Location** and remove the Zoom link
3. Go back to **My Calendar Link Settings** and reconnect your Zoom account
4. Re-select Zoom as the location for each meeting type
5. Book a test meeting to confirm Zoom links are now generating correctly

If you don't see an **Unlink** option, use the **Link** button instead to re-establish the connection.

</details>
