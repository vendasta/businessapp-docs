# Yesware Email Limit

Yesware does not impose any restrictions on the number of daily emails you can send. However, the **limit on the number of emails** is determined by your email provider (either **Google** or **Microsoft**).
Please note, We do have a **monthly limit** on the number of contacts you can add to email campaigns created through Yesware. These limits are based on your subscription plan.
For a complete list of features and limits by plan, please refer to our **pricing table**.
