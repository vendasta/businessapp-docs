# Update name in Meeting Scheduler

If your name has changed and you’d like to update it in Meeting Scheduler, follow these steps:
- Go to your **Meeting Scheduler settings**. 
- Click **Edit Details**.  
- Update your **Display Name**.
- If your name appears in your calendar link URL, you may also update that. 
- Click **Save Details** at the bottom of the screen.
Your changes will be reflected on your scheduling page immediately.
