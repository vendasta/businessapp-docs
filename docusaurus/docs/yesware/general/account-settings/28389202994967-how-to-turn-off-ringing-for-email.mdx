# How to turn off ringing for email opens

Yesware doesn’t have a built-in ringing notification for email opens, but you can manage your system’s notification settings to disable the sound. Follow these steps for a distraction-free experience:
- **Open Gmail**.
- **Click on Settings** (top right) and select **See all settings**.
- Scroll to **"Desktop notifications"**.
- Toggle the option to either **New mail notifications on** or **Important mail notifications on**.
- Next to **"Mail notification sounds"**, choose a sound or select **None** to turn off the sound.
- Scroll down and click **Save Changes**.
This will disable email notification sounds for a quieter workflow
