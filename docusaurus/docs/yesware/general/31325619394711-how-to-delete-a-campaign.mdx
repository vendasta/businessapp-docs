# How to delete a campaign

To delete a Yesware campaign, first go to the **Campaigns** **&gt; select the specific campaign &gt; click the Delete button in the bottom right corner. **A confirmation window will appear and then click Delete Campaign to permanently remove it.
Remember that this action is irreversible and all associated data will be lost.
