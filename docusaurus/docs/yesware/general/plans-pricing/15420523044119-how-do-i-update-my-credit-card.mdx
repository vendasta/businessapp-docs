# How do I update my credit card information?

### How Gmail and Outlook user can update their credit card info with Yesware
Head to theÂ **[Payment Method page](https://app.yesware.com/account/payment_method)** of our app site. Enter your card information and select "save."
You can also find this page by going to **Settings &gt; Payment Methods &gt; Update card &gt; Save.**
Please note the Payment Methods page will only be accessible to the Account Managers of your Yesware account, and Yesware accepts payment via credit card but does not accept payment via PayPal.
