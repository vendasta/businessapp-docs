# Automatically add video conferencing link when scheduling a meeting

To set up automatic video conferencing links when scheduling meetings, follow these steps:
**Open the Meeting Type Settings**:
- Click the **Settings** icon next to the desired meeting type.
**Edit the Meeting Location**:
- Select **Edit** under the meeting type settings.
- Go to the **Meeting Location** section.
**Enable Virtual Meetings**:
Choose **Virtual Meeting** and select your preferred platform:
- **Zoom**
- **Microsoft Teams**
- **Google Meet**
**Save &amp; Connect**:
- Once the platform is connected, a meeting link will be automatically generated and included upon booking.
