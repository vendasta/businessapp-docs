# Understanding Account Roles in Yesware

Yesware offers Various account roles with specific permissions:
**Account Manager**
The Account Manager serves as the primary administrator. They have full access to manage the account, including:
- Creating and managing teams
- Promoting users to administrative roles
- Sharing templates across the team
- Adding new users to the account
**Billing Manager**
The Billing Manager is responsible for account-related communications, including:
- Receiving order receipts and account change notifications
- Acting as the main contact for subscription renewals
These roles help streamline account management and ensure appropriate access and communication within Yesware.
