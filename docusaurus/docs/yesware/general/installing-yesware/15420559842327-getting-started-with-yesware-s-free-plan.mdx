# Getting Started with Yesware's Free Plan

Yesware offers a Free Tier of the product in order to provide value for unpaid users who would benefit from a limited set of Yesware tools. 
### What is offered on the Free Plan? 
- Email tracking details for emails sent less than 24 hours ago (limit 40 events)
- 10 global Campaigns recipients/month (Campaign stats - not incl.)
- Unlimited Templates (Template stats - not incl.) 
- Unlimited Meeting Scheduler link insertions 
- Unlimited Response Insights 
- Unlimited Reminders and Send Later on Gmail 
**To provide the best Free experience, you will also retain all of the data and information you added to Yesware when trialling or subscribing (i.e. Teams, Templates, Campaigns, tracking data). **
### How do I get added to the Free Plan? 
The Free Plan becomes available as soon as your free trial ends (provided that you do not begin a paid subscription). You must have the Gmail extension or Outlook add-in loaded to access the Free Plan. Users who were on Trial before or subscribed to Yesware in the past can also access Freemium by [installing the Yesware extension or add-in in their inbox](https://www.yesware.com/install/). 
### Removing Yesware
We hope you enjoy the Free Plan and all of the tools it provides at no cost, however, if you would like to remove Yesware from your inbox, you can do so by following these instructions: 
**For Chrome:**1) Open a new tab and enter ***chrome://extensions ***into the address bar.2) Find Yesware on the list and click 'Remove.'3) Refresh Gmail.
**For Outlook: **
- Click 'Get Add-ins' &gt; My add-ins &gt; Yesware - click the 3 dots and select 'Remove'
- Close Outlook
When you remove the extension or add-in, Yesware stops collecting your email or tracking information. For further data erasure information, you can contact Yesware support at [support@yesware.com](mailto:support@yesware.com).
