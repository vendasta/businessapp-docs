# Does Yesware offer a mobile app?

**Yesware does not offer a mobile app for GMail or Outlook.**
If you have a question about this business decision or Yesware’s current product offering, please submit a ticket to support.yesware.com (you’ll see a “submit a request” button in the upper right corner).
