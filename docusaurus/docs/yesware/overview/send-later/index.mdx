# Send Later

Send Later lets you schedule emails to be sent at a future date and time. Available on **Pro, Premium, and Enterprise** plans for Yesware on Gmail.

## Scheduling an Email

1. Open a new compose window and draft your email
2. Click the blue **Later** button (paper airplane icon) at the bottom of the compose window
3. Choose your date and time and click **Confirm**
4. Click **Send on \{date\}** when ready

The email is stored in your **Drafts** folder until the scheduled time — Yesware sends it automatically with no further action required.

## Editing or Cancelling a Scheduled Email

1. Go to your **Drafts** folder and open the scheduled message
2. Click the arrow next to **Send on \{date\}**
3. Choose a new date and time, or click **Unschedule** to cancel

## Viewing Scheduled Emails

Scheduled emails appear in your **Drafts** folder and in the **Scheduled** tab of your Yesware Activity Feed in Gmail.
