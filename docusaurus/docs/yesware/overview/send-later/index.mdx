# Send Later

Send Later lets you schedule emails to go out at a future date and time, including in the recipient's time zone. It's useful for drafting messages right after a meeting while details are fresh, then sending when the recipient is most likely to engage.

Available on **Pro, Premium, and Enterprise** plans.

## Schedule an Email

1. Open a compose window and draft your email.
2. Click the blue **Later** button (paper airplane icon) at the bottom of the compose window.
3. Choose your date and time, then click **Confirm**.
4. Click **Send on \{date\}** when ready.

The email moves to your **Drafts** folder until the scheduled time — Yesware sends it automatically with no further action required.

## Edit or Cancel a Scheduled Email

1. Go to your **Drafts** folder and open the scheduled message.
2. Click the arrow next to **Send on \{date\}**.
3. Choose a new date and time, or click **Unschedule** to cancel.

## View Scheduled Emails

Scheduled emails appear in your **Drafts** folder and in the **Scheduled** tab of your Yesware Activity Feed.
