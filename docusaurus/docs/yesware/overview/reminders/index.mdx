# Reminders

Reminders let you set a follow-up alert on any sent or incoming email so nothing slips through the cracks. You can view upcoming Reminders in the **Scheduled** tab of the Activity Feed at the top of Gmail.

## Setting a Reminder

When composing or sending an email, set a Reminder directly from the compose window. You can also set one retroactively on a sent email or on an incoming message you're not ready to respond to:

1. Open the email thread
2. Click the **Remind** button at the top of the thread
3. Set your date, time, and parameters
4. Click **Confirm**

## Frequently Asked Questions

<details>
<summary>How do I edit an existing Reminder?</summary>

1. Open the email with the Reminder
2. Click the bell icon (Reminder dropdown)
3. Click **Clear Reminder**
4. Set a new date and time in the popup and save

</details>

<details>
<summary>Can I change my default Reminder time?</summary>

Yes. On the Reminder setup page, hover over your preferred time and click the **star icon** to save it as your default.

</details>

<details>
<summary>Where do I view my upcoming Reminders?</summary>

In the **Scheduled** tab of the Activity Feed at the top of Gmail.

</details>
