# Changing Your Email Address

To transfer your Yesware license to a new email address, contact [support@yesware.com](mailto:support@yesware.com) and request a license transfer from your old address to your new one. Let the support team know if you'd like your Personal Template folder migrated as well.

Note: email address is Yesware's unique identifier. Historical tracking data, Campaigns, and Reminders cannot be transferred to the new address.
