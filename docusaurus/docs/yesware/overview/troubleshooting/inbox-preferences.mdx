# Inbox Preferences

You can customize several Gmail inbox features directly from Yesware Preferences. To access these settings, click the **Yesware** dropdown in the top left of Gmail, select **Preferences**, then click **Inbox**.

## Add to Campaign Button

The **Add to Campaign** button appears next to recipient email addresses in your Gmail threads. To show or hide it:

1. Go to **Yesware > Preferences > Inbox**
2. Toggle **Add a Recipient to a Campaign from an Email** on or off
3. Click **Save & Reload**

## Response Insights Widget

The Response Insights widget appears in your email threads. To show or hide it:

1. Go to **Yesware > Preferences > Inbox**
2. Toggle **Response Insights — Composer Widget** on or off
3. Click **Save & Reload**

## Restoring Response Insights in the Sidebar

On the Enterprise plan, the Yesware sidebar shows Contacts, Salesforce, Campaigns, and LinkedIn. If Response Insights has been replaced by LinkedIn Sales Navigator, you can restore it by disabling the LinkedIn integration:

1. Go to **Yesware > Preferences > Integrations**
2. Scroll to **LinkedIn Sales Navigator** and toggle it **Off**
3. Click **Save & Reload**
