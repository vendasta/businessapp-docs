# Outlook Troubleshooting

If Yesware isn't working as expected in Outlook, start by checking the [Yesware status page](https://status.yesware.com/). Subscribe to updates so you're notified of any ongoing incidents.

If there's no status to explain the issue, contact the Yesware Support Team at [support@yesware.com](mailto:support@yesware.com).

## Yesware Icons Are Grayed Out

Grayed-out icons typically mean the add-in is inactive or disabled.

### In the Outlook toolbar (inbox)

1. **Single-click** an email in your inbox — the Yesware add-in only activates when a message is selected
2. Make sure your **Reading Pane** is set to **Right** or **Bottom** (not Off)
3. Once the Yesware sidebar appears, click the **Pin icon** to keep it active

### In the compose window

If buttons like **Send with Yesware**, **Add Template**, **Meeting Scheduler**, or **Open Yesware** are unavailable:

**Step 1 — Remove conflicting add-ins**

Save your email as a Draft, then close and reopen it. Check for and disable conflicting add-ins such as:
- GlobalMeet, ToDoist, Boomerang, EmailTracker
- Antivirus add-ins (McAfee, Norton, Spyware tools)

**Step 2 — Re-authenticate**

1. Click **Yesware** in your Outlook toolbar
2. In the Sidebar, go to the **Menu** tab and select **Sign Out**
3. Follow the prompts to reactivate your Yesware account

**Step 3 — Reinstall the add-in**

1. Click **Get Add-ins** in the top-right corner of Outlook
2. Go to **My Add-ins**
3. Find Yesware, click the **three dots**, and select **Remove**
4. Search for Yesware and re-add it
