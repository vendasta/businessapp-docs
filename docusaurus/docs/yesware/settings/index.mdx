## Managing Users

### Adding Users

You must be a Yesware admin or Account Manager to add users.

1. In Gmail, open the Yesware dropdown and select **My Account**
2. Navigate to **Users** in the left-hand menu
3. Click **Add Users** in the upper right

Adding a new email address requires an additional license. Yesware will confirm any extra charges before adding the license. Users must be on a verified domain â€” by default, the domains associated with Account Managers on your account. To add a new verified domain, contact [support@yesware.com](mailto:support@yesware.com).

To edit a colleague's name, remove the user and re-add them with the correct name, or contact [support@yesware.com](mailto:support@yesware.com) for assistance.

### Removing Users

1. In Gmail, open the Yesware dropdown and select **My Account > Settings**
2. Navigate to **Users** in the left-hand menu
3. Select the user(s) you want to remove and click **Remove**

### Updating Seats at Renewal

1. Go to your [User Administration page](https://app.yesware.com/account/manage)
2. Click **Update Seats at Renewal** and adjust as needed

This ensures you're only charged for the seats you intend to keep.

## Re-authenticating Your Account

If your Yesware session expires, features like Campaigns, Send Later, Reminders, and Salesforce Sync will stop working until you sign back in.

Common reasons for needing to re-authenticate:
- You changed your password
- Your authorization token expired
- You changed your email address

**To re-authenticate in Gmail:** Sign out of Gmail and sign back in, or visit [app.yesware.com](https://app.yesware.com) and click **Sign in with Google**.

:::note
Any failed Campaigns, Send Later messages, or Reminders that occurred while you were unauthenticated cannot be restarted.
:::

## Email Preferences

To manage which Yesware emails you receive, visit the [Preference Center](https://www.yesware.com/preference-center). Enter your email address, check the boxes for the communications you want, and click **Save Preferences**.

## Frequently Asked Questions

<details>
<summary>How do I delete a trial account?</summary>

Trial account deletion must be handled by Yesware Support â€” this cannot be done on your own. To remove Yesware from your inbox in the meantime:

**Chrome:** Go to `chrome://extensions`, find Yesware, click **Remove**, then refresh Gmail.

**Outlook:** Click **Get Add-ins > My add-ins**, find Yesware, click the three dots, select **Remove**, then close and reopen Outlook.

<details>
<summary>How do I turn off notification sounds for email opens?</summary>

Yesware doesn't have a built-in notification sound, but you can manage sounds through Gmail's settings:

1. In Gmail, click **Settings > See all settings**
2. Scroll to **Desktop notifications**
3. Next to **Mail notification sounds**, select **None**
4. Click **Save Changes**

</details>

<details>
<summary>How do I control GeoIP location sharing?</summary>

GeoIP data is collected by a third-party service. You can manage this through your operating system:

**Windows:** Go to **Settings > Privacy > Location** and toggle location on or off for your device.

**Mac:** Go to **Apple menu > System Settings > Privacy & Security > Location Services** and disable it for specific apps or entirely.

</details>

User provisioning and de-provisioning must be managed manually within Yesware.
