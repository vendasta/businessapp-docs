# BCC to CRM

BCC to CRM lets you automatically send a copy of your outgoing emails to your CRM by BCCing a unique email address. If your CRM provides a BCC or dropbox email address, Yesware can route your outbound messages there automatically.

This feature is available on **Pro and Premium plans for Gmail** users. Enterprise users with Salesforce Sync enabled do not need BCC to CRM — emails are synced automatically via the Salesforce integration.

## Setting Up BCC to CRM in Gmail

1. Click the **Yesware** dropdown in the left-hand corner of Gmail
2. Select **Preferences**
3. Under **Integrations**, scroll to **BCC to CRM** and toggle it on
4. Enter your CRM's unique BCC email address
5. Click **Save & Reload**
6. When composing an email, confirm the **CRM** button is checked at the bottom of the compose window

## Finding Your BCC Address by CRM

Each CRM stores its BCC address in a different place. Here's where to find it in popular tools:

| CRM | Where to find the BCC address |
|---|---|
| **Salesforce** | Your name (top right) > My Settings > Email > My Email to Salesforce |
| **Pipedrive** | Settings > [Email integration settings](https://app.pipedrive.com/settings#email-sync) |
| **SugarCRM** | Admin > Email Archiving |
| **Capsule** | My Preferences > Mail drop box |
| **Zoho** | See [Zoho's BCC Dropbox guide](https://www.zoho.com/crm/help/email/bcc-dropbox.html) |

:::note
Yesware does not support BCC to CRM with Microsoft Dynamics, as it does not support the BCC email feature required for this integration.
:::

## Frequently Asked Questions

<details>
<summary>Do I need BCC to CRM if I'm on the Enterprise plan?</summary>

No. Enterprise users with Salesforce Sync enabled have their emails automatically synced to Salesforce. You do not need to set up a BCC address.

</details>

<details>
<summary>Is BCC to CRM available for Outlook?</summary>

No. BCC to CRM is only available for Yesware on Gmail.

</details>

<details>
<summary>My CRM isn't on the list — can I still use BCC to CRM?</summary>

Yes, as long as your CRM provides a unique BCC or dropbox email address, Yesware can route emails there. Contact your CRM's support team to find your BCC address, then enter it in **Yesware > Preferences > Integrations > BCC to CRM**.

</details>
