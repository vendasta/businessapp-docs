# Plans & Pricing

Yesware offers three plans: **Pro**, **Premium**, and **Enterprise**. To view current pricing and a full feature comparison, visit the [Plans and Pricing page](http://www.yesware.com/plans-and-pricing). All prices are per license per user.

## Upgrading Your Plan

1. Go to [app.yesware.com/account/plan](http://app.yesware.com/account/plan)
2. Click the **Upgrade** button in the upper right corner, next to your email address
3. Select your new plan and complete the checkout

## Cancelling Your Plan

1. Go to [app.yesware.com/account/plan](http://app.yesware.com/account/plan)
2. Scroll to the bottom of the page and click **Cancel Renewal**

Cancelling stops future charges. Your current plan features remain active until the end of your billing period. Since Yesware is subscription-based and billed in advance, no refunds are issued for unused time remaining in your term.

## Updating Your Payment Method

Only Account Managers can update payment information.

1. Go to the [Payment Method page](https://app.yesware.com/account/payment_method)
2. Enter your new card information and click **Save**

You can also find this page via **Settings > Payment Methods > Update Card > Save**.

Yesware accepts payment by credit card. PayPal is not accepted.

## Frequently Asked Questions

<details>
<summary>What happens to my data if I cancel?</summary>

Your account moves to the Free Plan at the end of your billing period. Your templates, campaigns, teams, and tracking history are preserved. See the Free Plan section in [Installing Yesware](../../overview/installing-yesware/) for what's included.

</details>

<details>
<summary>Can I get a refund if I cancel mid-term?</summary>

No. Yesware is subscription-based and billed in advance. No refunds are provided for the unused remainder of a billing term.

</details>
