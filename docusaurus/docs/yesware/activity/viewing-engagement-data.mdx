# Viewing Engagement Data

## Why Doesn't My Message Have Tracking Data?

If you're not seeing tracking data for a sent email, here are the most common reasons:

**Tracking wasn't enabled when you sent**
- Gmail: the **Track** checkbox wasn't checked in the compose window
- Outlook: the **Yesware Sidebar wasn't open** when you sent, or tracking was turned off in settings

**The email was sent in plain-text mode**
Yesware's tracking pixel requires HTML format. Plain-text emails disable tracking entirely.

**The recipient has images disabled**
Yesware tracking is image-based. If the recipient blocks images, opens won't register — but link clicks, presentation views, and replies may still be tracked.

**The email hasn't been opened yet**

**Colleague Events are turned off**
Internal sends may be excluded from your tracking data by default. Toggle this in your Preferences.

**Your Yesware session expired (Gmail only)**
If your session expired, the tracking pixel may not have been inserted. Log out and back in, then resend.

## Frequently Asked Questions

<details>
<summary>Can I search the Activity Feed?</summary>

The Activity Feed does not currently have a search option. To find activity for a specific email:

- Use Gmail's or Outlook's search bar to locate the thread in your Sent folder
- Open the email and click the **Tracking** eyeball icon (Gmail) or select the **Message** tab in the Yesware Sidebar (Outlook) to view its tracking history

You can also search the [Tracking Report](https://app.yesware.com/reports/tracking) directly.

</details>

<details>
<summary>Can I tell which specific recipient opened an email with multiple people on it?</summary>

No. When an email is sent to multiple recipients, Yesware cannot identify exactly who opened it. You'll see a general notification like "John Smith or 5 others opened." To track opens accurately, send to one recipient at a time.

</details>

<details>
<summary>Will Yesware count a bounce message as a reply?</summary>

No. If Yesware detects a bounce, it is not counted as a reply. The bounce is recorded in your [Tracking Report](https://app.yesware.com/reports/tracking) — click the subject line in the report for detailed information. On the Enterprise plan with Salesforce Sync enabled, bounce notices are also synced to Salesforce.

</details>
