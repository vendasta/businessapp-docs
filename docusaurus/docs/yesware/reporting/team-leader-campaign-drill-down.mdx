# Team Leader Campaign Drill-Down

If you are designated as a **Team Leader** of a Team, you can click directly into any campaign owned by one of your direct reports from the [Campaigns by User Report](./what-data-is-shown-on-the-campaigns.mdx) to see that campaign's full email touch content and recipient list. This gives you visibility into a direct report's messaging and engagement without needing them to share it with you manually.

## Who can see this

The click-through into a campaign is only available to Team Leaders, and only for campaigns owned by their direct reports. If you are reviewing your own campaigns, or viewing a peer's or a skip-level team member's campaigns, this option is not available.

This feature relies on the existing Teams feature, which is only available on the Premium and Enterprise plans.

Your access depends on how your Team is set up. See [Teams](../teams/index.mdx) for how Team Leader roles and direct reports are assigned.

## How to view a campaign's details

1. Go to the Yesware AppSite and select **Reports** in the left navigation panel.
2. Under the **Campaigns** section, click **Campaigns by User**.
3. Select a direct report from the user list to open their campaign summary.

<div style={{marginBottom: '24px'}}>
<img src={require('./img/team-leader-campaign-drill-down-user-campaigns.png').default} alt="A direct report's campaign list showing Total Recipients, Open %, Click %, Connect %, and Meetings Booked columns" style={{width: '100%', border: '1px solid #e0e0e0', borderRadius: '8px', boxShadow: '0 2px 8px rgba(0,0,0,0.08)'}} />
</div>

4. Click a campaign name to drill into it. This option is only visible to Team Leaders.
5. Review the **Touches** tab for each email touch's content, timing between touches, and per-touch engagement metrics (reply rate, open rate, click rate, delivered, skipped).

<div style={{marginBottom: '24px'}}>
<img src={require('./img/team-leader-campaign-drill-down-touches-tab.png').default} alt="Campaign drill-down Touches tab showing touch sequence cards with expanded email content and per-touch metrics" style={{width: '100%', border: '1px solid #e0e0e0', borderRadius: '8px', boxShadow: '0 2px 8px rgba(0,0,0,0.08)'}} />
</div>

6. Switch to the **Recipients** tab to see everyone enrolled in the campaign, along with their current touch, campaign status, opens, last open date, replies, last reply date, and meetings booked.

<img src={require('./img/team-leader-campaign-drill-down-recipients-tab.png').default} alt="Campaign drill-down Recipients tab showing the recipient table with current touch, status, and engagement columns" style={{width: '100%', border: '1px solid #e0e0e0', borderRadius: '8px', boxShadow: '0 2px 8px rgba(0,0,0,0.08)'}} />

## Frequently asked questions

<details>
<summary>Who can use the campaign drill-down feature?</summary>

This feature relies on the existing Teams feature, which is only available on the Premium and Enterprise plans. Only users designated as a Team Leader with direct reports can click into a direct report's campaign. Peers and users without direct reports do not see the click-through option in the Campaigns by User report.
</details>

<details>
<summary>Can I see campaigns for direct reports I do not directly manage?</summary>

No. Access is scoped to your direct reports only. Skip-level or broader team members' campaigns are not accessible through this view.
</details>

<details>
<summary>I manage someone, but I can't see their campaigns. What's wrong?</summary>

Your Team may not be set up correctly. Verify and update the Team and manager assignments in [Teams](../teams/index.mdx).
</details>

<details>
<summary>Can a direct report tell when I've viewed their campaign?</summary>

No. There is no notification sent to the direct report when you review their campaign.
</details>

<details>
<summary>Can I edit or delete a direct report's campaign from this view?</summary>

No. Access through this view is read-only. You can view a campaign's touches and recipients, but you cannot modify the campaign.
</details>
