# My Dashboard

The [My Dashboard](https://app.yesware.com/reports/dashboard/#/dashboard) view in Yesware's reporting shows a snapshot of your personal activity and highlights insights about your recipients and content performance for the last 7 days.

The dashboard gives you a centralized place to quickly view key data and answer questions like:

- What was my email activity the last 7 days?
- How is my overall templates performance? Is it improving?
- How many recipients are actually engaging with my emails?
- Who has engaged the most?
- What content is generating the most interest?

## Metrics included

- **Emails Tracked** — Total tracked emails sent in the last 7 days
- **Templates Reply Rate** — Percent of tracked templates that received a reply in the last 7 days
- **Recipients on Engaged Emails** — Total unique recipients on engaged emails in the last 7 days
- **Total Link Clicks** — Total number of link clicks in the last 7 days

## What Recipients Are Engaging?

This card shows the recipient who performed each action the most in the last 7 days. Note: this only shows engagement from emails sent to a single recipient.

- **Most Opens**
- **Most Clicks**
- **Most Presentation Views**

## What Content is Succeeding?

This card shows which content had the most engagement in the last 7 days:

- **Most Opens** — Subject line of the most opened email
- **Most Clicks** — Link that was most clicked
- **Most Presentation Views** — Name of the most viewed presentation

Data trends compare the last 7 days against the previous 7 days. The last 7 days includes 6 full days plus the current day up to the time you view the report.
