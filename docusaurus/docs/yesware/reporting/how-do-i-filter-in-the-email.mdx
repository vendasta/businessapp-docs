# Filtering Reports

Both the Email Activity and Templates Reports support filtering and sorting to help you zero in on the data that matters. Note: the Email Activity Report includes both tracked and untracked messages; the Templates Reports include only templates sent in tracked messages.

## Email Activity Report

In the [Email Activity Report](https://app.yesware.com/reports/activity/#/activity), filter by:

- **Reporting period** — Choose a date range
- **Filter users by** — Narrow to a specific team
- **Sort by** — Choose a metric to sort on

**Advanced filters:**

- **Exclude My Data** — Remove your own stats from the table and team averages
- **Exclude Users Without Activity** — Hide team members with no emails sent in the selected period
- **Include Colleague Emails** — Include emails sent to people at your own domain (excluded by default)

## Templates Reports (By User and By Template)

In the [Templates Reports](https://app.yesware.com/reports/templates/#/templates/by_user), filter by reporting period, team, and metric. Advanced filters include:

- **Exclude My Data** — Remove your own template stats
- **Exclude Users Without Activity** *(By User only)* — Hide team members with no template activity
- **Exclude Templates Without Activity** *(By Template only)* — Hide templates that haven't been used
- **Exclude Personal Templates Usage** — Show only shared Team Templates
- **Include Colleague Emails** — Include templates sent to colleagues at your own domain (excluded by default)
