# Link Click Engagement Report

The [Link Click Engagement Report](https://app.yesware.com/reports/link_click/#/link_click) shows how your recipients are engaging with tracked hyperlinks in your emails. It displays the total number of clicks each link received within your selected time period, and the number of tracked emails that contained each link.

Note: emails that had no engagement in the selected period are omitted from this report.

Filter and sort by time period, link, number of emails, and total link clicks. Click **View Recipients** in the right-hand column to see exactly who clicked a given link and how many times.

Click **Download Report** to export the data to a CSV.
