# Recipient Engagement Report

The [Recipient Engagement Report](https://app.yesware.com/reports/recipient/#/recipient) shows how each individual recipient is engaging with your tracked emails, so you can base your follow-up on who is engaging and who isn't.

The report is broken down recipient-by-recipient and shows the total number of tracked emails that were engaged with (opened, link clicked, or attachment viewed) by each recipient within the selected time period. Emails without engagement in the selected period are omitted.

You can sort by recipient, number of engaged emails, opens, link clicks, attachment views, and date of most recent engagement. Choose from a variety of date ranges to identify your most and least engaged recipients.

Click **Download Report** to export the data to a CSV.
