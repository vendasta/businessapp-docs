# Why do emails that have "Bounced" also show as “Opened” or “Clicked”?

This happens when the recipient's email inbox scans the message prior to bouncing it. Consequently, it is recorded as both bounced and opened. A notification will be sent regarding the undelivered email that includes additional details as to why the message bounced.
