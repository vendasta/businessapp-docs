# Campaigns Report

The [Campaigns by User Report](https://app.yesware.com/reports/campaigns/#/campaigns/by_user) provides an overview of campaign usage and performance for you and your team over the last 13 months. Use the search function to find teammates by name or email, and the date filter to view specific time frames. Click on any user's row to drill into their individual campaign analysis.

## Metrics included

- **Total Recipients** — Total recipients across all campaigns who received at least one email touch or phone call during the reporting period
- **Open Percentage Rate** — Fraction of those recipients who opened an email touch at least once
- **Click Percentage Rate** — Fraction of those recipients who clicked a tracked link at least once in an email touch where link tracking was enabled
- **Connect Percentage Rate** — Fraction of those recipients who replied at least once, were marked as "Connected" on a phone call task, or booked a meeting through Meeting Scheduler
- **Meetings Booked** — Total first meetings booked through Meeting Scheduler across all the user's campaigns

Click **Download Report** to export the data to a CSV.
