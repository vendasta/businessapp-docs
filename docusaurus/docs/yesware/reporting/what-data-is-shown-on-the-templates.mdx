# Templates Report

The [Templates Report](https://app.yesware.com/reports/templates/#/templates/by_template) shows the usage and performance of your templates, so sales leaders can see which templates are performing well and team members can follow what's working. You can [filter and sort](how-do-i-filter-in-the-email) the data by reporting period for more targeted analysis.

## Templates by Template

Breaks down performance by each individual template:

- **Template** — Name of each unique template
- **Users** — Total number of users who sent tracked emails using that template
- **Open Percentage Rate** — Of all tracked emails using that template, the percent that received at least one open
- **Reply Percentage Rate** — Of all tracked emails using that template, the percent that received at least one reply

## CSV export

Download up to 365 days of template activity for you and your team. The CSV includes:

- **Templates Used** — Number of unique templates inserted into tracked emails
- **Number of Emails Tracked with a Template** — Total tracked emails that included at least one template
- **Open Percentage Rate** — Of tracked emails with a template, the percent that received at least one open
- **Reply Percentage Rate** — Of tracked emails with a template, the percent that received at least one reply

Click **Download Report** to export.
