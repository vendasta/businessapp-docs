# CSV Titles That Export from Campaigns

To export recipient data from a campaign:
Go to the specific campaign.
Click the **Recipients** tab.
Check the box for **Personal Details**.
Click **Export**.
Currently, only the following field titles will export:
Email
FirstName
LastName
Name
Title
Phone
Company
**Note:** These field titles must be spelled exactly as shown above, including capitalization. For example, “FirstName” will export, but “firstname” or “Firstname” will not.
