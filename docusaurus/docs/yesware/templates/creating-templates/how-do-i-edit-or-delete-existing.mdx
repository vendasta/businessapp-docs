# Edit or Delete Templates

Any user can edit and delete personal templates. Team Leaders can also edit and delete team templates and folders.

## Edit a template

1. Select the **Templates** menu at the top of Gmail.
2. Find and click the template you want to update.
3. Click **Edit** at the bottom of the window.
4. Make your changes and click **Save Template**.

> After editing, click the refresh button in the Templates modal in your compose window to see the updated template right away.

## Delete a template

1. Select the **Templates** menu and click the template.
2. Click **More**, then **Delete Template**.
