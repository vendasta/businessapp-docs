You can create templates in Gmail or Outlook — from a compose window, an existing email thread, or directly in the Yesware template library. Templates support text formatting, images, HTML, tracked links, and attachments.

## Create a template in Gmail

You can create a template four ways:

**From the Templates library**
1. Click the **Templates** menu at the top of Gmail.
2. Click **New Template** in the upper right.
3. Write your message and set the name, subject, and style options.
4. Click **Save Template**.

**From the compose window**
1. Write your email.
2. Click the **Templates** button at the bottom of the compose window.
3. Select **Save as Template**.

**From a sent or received email**
1. Open the email you want to reuse.
2. Click **Save as Template** at the top of the email.
3. Remove any recipient-specific content, then save.

See [Create in Gmail](creating-yesware-templates-in-gmail.mdx) for the full walkthrough.

## Create a template in Outlook

1. In the Outlook ribbon, click **Open Templates**.
2. Click **New Template** in the upper right.
3. Write your message and set the name, subject, and style options.
4. Click **Save Template**.

See [Create in Outlook](creating-yesware-templates-in-outlook.mdx) for the full walkthrough including how to insert and use templates.

## Merge fields

Merge fields let you personalize templates for each recipient. Yesware supports three types:

| Type | What it does |
|---|---|
| **Text Field** | A free-form blank you fill in at send time |
| **Dropdown Field** | A pick-list with up to 5 options you define |
| **Salesforce Field** *(Enterprise only)* | Auto-fills from the matching Contact or Lead record |

See [Merge Fields](how-to-use-placeholders-merge-fields-in.mdx) for step-by-step setup.

## Common questions

<details>
<summary>How do I save an email I've already sent as a template?</summary>

Open the sent email, click **Save as Template** at the top of the email, remove any recipient-specific content, and save. You can also do this from the compose window — write your email, click the **Templates** button at the bottom, then select **Save as Template**.

</details>

<details>
<summary>How do I edit or delete a template?</summary>

**Edit:** Click the **Templates** menu, find the template, click it, then click **Edit** at the bottom. Make your changes and click **Save Template**. After editing, click the refresh button in the Templates modal to see the updated version right away.

**Delete:** Click the **Templates** menu, click the template, then click **More** → **Delete Template**.

Team Leaders can also edit and delete team templates and folders.

</details>
