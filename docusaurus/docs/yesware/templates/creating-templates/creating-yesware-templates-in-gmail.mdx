# Creating Templates in Gmail

Templates let you save reusable email content so you can send rich, consistent messages faster.

## How to create a template

You can create a template in four ways:

**From the Templates library**

1. Click the **Templates** menu at the top of Gmail.
2. Click **New Template** in the upper right.
3. Type your message, then set the template name, subject, and style options.
4. Click **Save Template**.

**From the compose window**

1. Compose your email.
2. Click the **Templates** button at the bottom of the compose window.
3. Select **Save as Template**.

**From a sent or received email thread**

1. Open the email you want to reuse.
2. Click **Save as Template** at the top of the email.
3. Remove any recipient-specific content, then save.

**From the Yesware App**

Visit [app.yesware.com/template_library/manage](https://app.yesware.com/template_library/manage), click **New Template**, and save.

## Merge fields

Use merge fields to personalize templates for each recipient. See [Merge Fields](./how-to-use-placeholders-merge-fields-in) for details on Text Fields, Dropdown Fields, and Salesforce Fields.

## Team template sharing

On Premium and Enterprise plans, you can share templates with your team. After you create the template, click **Share with team**.
