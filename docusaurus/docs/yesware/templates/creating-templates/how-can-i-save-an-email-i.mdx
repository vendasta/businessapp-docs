# Save a Sent Email as a Template

## From a sent or received email

1. Open the email you want to reuse.
2. Click **Save as Template** at the top of the email.
3. Remove any recipient-specific content.
4. Save the template for future use.

## From the compose window

1. Write your email in the compose window.
2. Click the **Templates** button at the bottom.
3. Select **Save as Template**.

A copy is saved to your template library where you can edit it before saving.
