# Templates

Templates let you save reusable email content so you can send rich, consistent messages in a fraction of the time. On Premium and Enterprise plans, templates can be shared across your entire team.

## What's in this section

- **[Creating Templates](./creating-templates)** — Create templates in Gmail or Outlook, use merge fields, and edit or delete existing templates
- **[Sharing Templates](./sharing-templates)** — Share templates with your team, manage folders, and control permissions
- **[Using Templates](./using-templates)** — Insert templates, add attachments and links, use HTML and images, and troubleshoot

## Frequently asked questions

<details>
<summary>Can I add a video to a template?</summary>

Not directly. As a workaround, take a screenshot of the video, insert it as an image in the template, then hyperlink the image to the video URL. This gives recipients a clickable thumbnail.
</details>

<details>
<summary>Can I use dynamic Salesforce fields in templates?</summary>

Dynamic Salesforce fields in templates are available on the Enterprise plan only. See [How do dynamic templates work with Salesforce?](../salesforce-integration/salesforce-sync-enterprise-plan/how-do-dynamic-templates-work-with-salesforce.mdx) for details.
</details>

<details>
<summary>Can I export my templates?</summary>

No. Template export is not available. The best alternative is to manually copy and paste the template content from Yesware.
</details>
