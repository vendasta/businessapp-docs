# Move Templates Between Folders

## Move a template in Gmail

1. Select the **Templates** menu at the top of Gmail.
2. Find and click the template you want to move.
3. Click **More** at the bottom of the window, then select **Move Template**.
4. Choose the destination folder and click **Move Template**.

> Moving a template to a team folder requires Team Leader permissions.

## Move a template in the Yesware App

1. Go to [app.yesware.com/template_library/manage](https://app.yesware.com/template_library/manage).
2. Find the template, click **More**, then **Move Template**.
3. Select the destination folder and click **Move Template**.

> After making changes in the Yesware App, refresh your email to see updates in the extension.

## Reorder templates

Navigate to any folder and click **Custom Sort**. Drag and drop templates into your preferred order.
