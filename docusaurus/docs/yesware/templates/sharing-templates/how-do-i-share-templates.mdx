# Share Templates

Sharing is available to Team Leaders on Premium and Enterprise plans.

## Share an existing template

1. Navigate to the template.
2. Click **Share with Team** at the bottom of the page.
3. Select the team and folder.
4. Choose:
   - **Share** — moves the template from Personal to the team folder. Team members can view, edit, and use it.
   - **Duplicate & Share** — keeps your personal copy and creates a shared copy for the team.

Alternatively, use **More** → **Move Template** to switch the template from Personal to a Team folder. Note that moving removes the template from your Personal folder.

## Create a template and share it immediately

1. Click **New Template**.
2. In the **Save in Folder** section, click **Edit** → **Team**.
3. Select the team and folder.
4. Save. The template is shared with team members — no personal copy is created.

> After sharing, team members must refresh Gmail to access the new template.

## Reorder templates

Navigate to any folder and click **Custom Sort** to drag and drop templates into your preferred order.
