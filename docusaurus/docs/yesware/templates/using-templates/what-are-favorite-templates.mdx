# Favorite Templates

Favorite Templates let you quickly access your most-used templates from a single place. Click the **star** next to any template name to favorite it.

## Gmail

Favorites appear under the **Favorites** tab in the Templates modal when composing an email.

## Outlook

Favorites appear in the left-hand menu under **My Favorites**.
