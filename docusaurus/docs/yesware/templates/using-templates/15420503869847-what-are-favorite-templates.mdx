# What are favorite Templates?

Favorite Templates allow you to quickly access your most frequently used Templates, saving them in a single, easy-to-access place. You can favorite a Template by clicking on the star next to the Template name.
### Gmail Users
Favorite Templates will be stored under the "Favorites" tab at the top of the small Template modal in a Gmail compose window.
### Outlook Users
Favorite Templates will be stored in the left hand menu under "My Favorites."
