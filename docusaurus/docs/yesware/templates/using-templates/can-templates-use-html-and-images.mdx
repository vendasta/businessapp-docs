# HTML and Images in Templates

Templates support HTML formatting and image insertion in both Gmail and Outlook.

## Gmail

**Using the compose window:**

1. Open a compose window and design your message with formatting and images.
2. Click the **Templates** button at the bottom.
3. Select **Save as Template**.

**Direct HTML input:**

1. Click **Templates** → **New Template**.
2. Click the **< >** HTML mode button in the formatting toolbar.
3. Paste or write your HTML.

**Insert an image:**

1. While editing a template, click the **insert/edit image** icon.
2. Browse your computer, drag and drop, or enter an image URL.
3. Click **Okay** and save the template.

## Outlook

1. Click **Open Templates** in the Outlook ribbon.
2. Click **New Template** in the upper right.
3. To write HTML, click the **< >** button in the formatting toolbar and enter your code.

**Insert an image:**

1. Click the **insert/edit image** icon.
2. Browse your device, drag and drop, or add an image URL.
3. Click **Okay** and save the template.
