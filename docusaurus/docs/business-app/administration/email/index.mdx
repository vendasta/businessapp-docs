# Email overview

Email in Business App covers **domain setup and deliverability**, **email history**, and **notification preferences**. Use the topics below to configure sending, review sent messages, or manage what you're notified about.

## In this section

| Topic | What it covers |
|--------|-----------------|
| [**Email Configuration**](./email-configuration) | Verify your domain (SPF, DKIM, DMARC), set send-from name and address, improve deliverability, and troubleshoot spam issues. |
| [**Email history**](./email-history) | View, filter, and export outbound and inbound emails; check delivery status and troubleshoot. |
| [**Email Subscription Preferences**](./email-subscription-preferences) | How contacts manage which email categories they receive — marketing, product updates, and website communications. |
| [**Notification Settings**](../notification_settings.mdx) | Choose instant vs daily digest and turn notification types on or off by product (reviews, inbox, Executive Report, etc.). |

## Where to find it in the app

- **Settings → Email Configuration** — Domain verification and send-from
- **Settings → Conversations → Email history** — Email log and export
- **Email unsubscribe link** — Contacts manage their own subscription preferences from the unsubscribe link in emails
- **Settings → Notification Settings** — What you get notified about and how often
