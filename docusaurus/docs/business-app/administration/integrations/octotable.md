Business App integrates with OctoTable so you can connect your account without entering separate API credentials. You only need your OctoTable Property ID to complete the connection.

**Requirements:** An OctoTable account with RixAPI enabled.

## Before you connect

Enable API access in your OctoTable account before starting the connection in Business App:

1. Log in to your OctoTable account.
2. Search for **RixAPI**.
3. Enable **RixAPI** for your account.
4. Locate your **Property ID**. You'll need it to complete the connection in Business App.

## Set up the OctoTable integration

1. In Business App, go to **Administration** → **Integrations** → **Browse**.
2. Find the **OctoTable** card and click it.
3. On the marketing page, click **Connect**.
4. Enter your **Property ID** on the pre-connect form.
5. Click **Add Connection** to complete setup.

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

<details>
<summary>Do I need a Client ID and Secret to connect OctoTable?</summary>

No. Business App uses a shared, pre-configured connection for all OctoTable accounts. You only need to provide your Property ID.

</details>

<details>
<summary>What is RixAPI?</summary>

RixAPI is the name OctoTable uses for the API you must enable in your OctoTable account before connecting to Business App.

</details>

<details>
<summary>Where do I find my Property ID?</summary>

Your Property ID is available in your OctoTable account after you enable RixAPI. Check your OctoTable account settings to locate it.

</details>

<details>
<summary>Can each location connect its own OctoTable account?</summary>

Yes. Each location connects using its own Property ID.

</details>
