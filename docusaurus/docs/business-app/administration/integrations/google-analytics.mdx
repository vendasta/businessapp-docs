Business App can integrate with a Google Analytics 4 property to surface analytics in reporting.

## Connect Google Analytics 4

1. In Business App, go to **Administration → Integrations → Browse**.
2. Click the **Google Analytics (GA4)** card.
3. On the marketing page, click **Add Connection**.
4. Sign in to your Google account and authorize access.

## FAQ

<details>
<summary>Does this support Universal Analytics (UA)?</summary>

No. Only GA4 properties are supported.

</details>

<details>
<summary>What permissions are required?</summary>

Your Google account must have access to the GA4 property you are linking.

</details>

<details>
<summary>Where will I see the data?</summary>

Relevant metrics appear in Website Pro and Business App reporting.

</details>
