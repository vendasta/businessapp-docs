# Billing settings

The **Billing settings** section in Business App lets you keep your payment information current.

:::info Note
For security reasons, payment methods cannot be managed if your service provider is currently logged in to your account on your behalf. To manage payment methods, please log in directly to your account.
:::

## Frequently Asked Questions

<details>
<summary>Where can I add my credit card details?</summary>

You can add your credit card details in **Business App ? Administration ? Billing Settings ? Add Payment Method.**

</details>

<details>
<summary>What payment options can I use?</summary>

You can use the following payment methods:

- Credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover)
- ACH transfers
- Canadian Pre-Authorized Debits (PADs)

</details>
