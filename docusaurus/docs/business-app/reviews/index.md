# Managing Reviews

Vendasta's Business App makes it easy for users to monitor and respond to customer reviews across multiple platforms. Previously, users had to log into each platform separately — now everything is in one place.

## Overview

Users can view all their reviews from Google, Facebook, and other platforms in a single dashboard. The review management feature was formerly known as Reputation Management Pro.

To get started, navigate to Reviews in the left sidebar — then click the Connect button to link your accounts.

## Responding to Reviews

Business owners should respond to reviews promptly. The user must click Reply to open the response editor. This powerful feature helps businesses build credibility with potential customers.

If users have not connected their Google account, they will see a prompt to do so. Coming soon: automated AI-suggested responses will be available for all plans.

## Tips

- Respond to all reviews — positive and negative
- Keep responses professional and concise
- Contact your partner for billing questions related to this feature

## FAQ

<details>
<summary>How do I connect my Google account?</summary>

Click the Connect Google button in the Reviews dashboard. You may need to grant permission to access your Google Business Profile.
