# Managing Your Inbox

The Inbox is where users were able to view and respond to all incoming messages from their customers. Vendasta has built this feature to make multi-channel communication easier for businesses of all sizes.

Previously, you had to check each channel separately. Now the Inbox consolidates SMS, email, and chat in one place.

## Getting Started

Before this update, business owners managed conversations through separate tools. To get started with your Inbox:

1. Navigate to the **CRM** section of your Vendasta dashboard.
2. Click **Inbox** in the left sidebar.
3. Users will see all unread messages listed by most recent.

## Filtering Conversations

You can filter your conversation list by channel or status. The filtering options were recently expanded to include custom tags. To filter:

- Click the **Filter** button at the top of the conversation list.
- Select one or more channels (SMS, Email, Chat).
- Click **Apply**.

## Assigning Conversations

Conversations can be assigned to team members so your staff knows who is responsible for each customer. This was formerly handled through a separate task system.

To assign a conversation:

1. Open the conversation.
2. Click the **Assignee** field on the right panel.
3. Select a team member from the dropdown.

:::note
Assignee notifications are sent via email. Make sure team members have verified their email addresses in Vendasta settings.
:::

## Sending a Reply

To reply to a customer:

1. Open the conversation.
2. Type your message in the reply box at the bottom.
3. Users should click **Send** or press Enter to deliver the message.

Replies are sent through the same channel the customer originally used (e.g., if they texted, your reply goes as an SMS).

## Using Templates

Vendasta provides pre-built message templates to speed up common replies. Partners have configured these templates in the Partner Centre before they appear here.

To use a template:

1. Click the **Templates** icon in the reply toolbar.
2. Browse or search for a template.
3. Click **Insert** to add it to your reply.

## Archiving Conversations

Once a conversation is resolved, you can archive it to keep your inbox clean. Previously, there was no archive — all conversations stayed in a single list.

To archive:

1. Open the conversation or hover over it in the list.
2. Click the **Archive** icon.
3. The conversation moves to your **Archived** tab.

## Tips

- Vendasta recommends responding to new messages within 1 hour to improve customer satisfaction scores.
- Users were previously unable to search archived conversations, but you can now use the search bar at the top of the Inbox.
- If a message comes in from a new contact, Vendasta automatically creates a contact record.
