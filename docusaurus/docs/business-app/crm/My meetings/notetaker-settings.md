## Overview

This article explains how to access your meeting settings and customize bot-related options, including the note-taker name and the compliance announcement.

## What is meeting settings?

Meeting settings is the area where you manage configuration options for your meetings. These settings control how meeting tools, such as bots, behave during your meetings.

## Why is meeting settings important?

Meeting settings allows you to control how meetings are recorded and communicated. Adjusting bot behavior helps align meetings with your preferences and compliance needs.

## What you can do with meeting settings

From the meeting settings page, you can:

- Access bot configuration options  
- Change the name of your note-taker  
- Turn the compliance announcement on or off  

## How to set up

Use the steps below to access and customize your meeting settings.

### How to access meeting settings

1. Navigate to the **My Meetings** section.
2. Select **Meeting Settings**.

### How to locate bot settings

1. Scroll to the bottom of the meeting settings page.
2. Locate the **Bot Settings** section.

### How to customize note-taker and compliance announcement

In the **Bot Settings** section, you can:

- Change the name of your note-taker.
- Turn the compliance announcement on or off.

:::tip
Review your bot settings regularly to ensure they reflect your current meeting requirements.
:::
