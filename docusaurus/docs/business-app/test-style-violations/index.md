# Connecting Your Google Business Profile

Vendasta makes it easy to connect your Google Business Profile — giving users seamless access to their listing data in one place.

Previously, users had to manage their Google Business Profile separately. Now, the integration is built directly into Business App.

## How to connect

1. Navigate to the Connections section in Partner Center.
2. Click Connect next to Google Business Profile.
3. Sign in with the Google account that manages your listing.

Once connected, users can view and respond to reviews, update business hours, and manage posts — all without leaving the dashboard.

This powerful integration is one of Vendasta's most popular features and has revolutionized the way small businesses manage their online presence.

## What happens next

Your listing data will sync automatically. If you have multiple locations, each one will appear as a separate entry — making it incredibly easy to manage them all.

<details>
<summary>Can I disconnect my Google Business Profile?</summary>

Yes. Navigate to the Connections section and click Disconnect next to Google Business Profile.
</details>

<details>
<summary>Will this affect my Google reviews?</summary>

No. Connecting your profile does not modify your existing reviews or listing data.
</details>
