# Home Overview

## What You'll See

- **Key Metrics**: Performance data organized into Impressions, Engagement, and Leads
- **Recent Activity**: Live feed of messages, calls, and customer interactions  
- **Business Profile**: Quick access to your location info and profile management
- **Products & Services**: Your available offerings and store access
- **Get Started Guide**: Helpful setup assistance if you're new

## Invite Team Members

You can invite additional team members to help manage your Business App account.

### How to Invite a Team Member

1. Log into **Business App**.  
2. From the **Home page**, select **Invite Team Member** in the top right corner.  
3. Enter the first name, last name, and email address of the team member you want to invite.  
4. Click **Send Invite**.

The invited team member will receive an email with instructions to join and access your Business App account.
