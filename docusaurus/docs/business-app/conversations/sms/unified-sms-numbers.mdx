Review requests and Conversations AI messages are sent from the same SMS number. Customer replies to review requests appear directly in Conversations AI, keeping all interactions in one place.

## How it works

Your Conversations AI SMS number is used to send review requests. This gives you several advantages:

1. **Single A2P registration:** You only need to register one number for both review requests and Conversations AI.
2. **Unified communication:** Review events are logged in the Conversations AI conversation, so all interactions with a customer stay in one place.
3. **Visible replies:** Customer replies to review requests appear in Conversations AI, so you can respond directly.

## Credits and usage

Conversations AI messages are unlimited. Review Management Premium uses credits per request.
