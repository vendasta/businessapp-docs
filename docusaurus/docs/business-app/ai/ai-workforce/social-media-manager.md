## What is the Social Media Manager?

The Social Media Manager is an AI Employee in `AI` > `AI Workforce` that helps you generate recurring social content plans. It uses **Social Calendar Generation** to create scheduled content on an automatic cadence.

When you open the trigger settings, the schedule is pre-configured so you can start generating weekly plans without manual setup.

## Why is the Social Media Manager important?

- Save setup time with pre-filled scheduling values
- Keep content planning consistent with a weekly cadence
- Start with a recommended AI model for faster setup
- Run generation automatically with schedule-based triggers

## What is included by default?

The Social Media Manager opens with these defaults:

- **Feature name**: `Social Calendar Generation`
- **AI model**: `Gemini Flash Latest` (recommended)
- **Schedule type**: `On a Schedule`
- **Frequency**: `Weekly`
- **Day of week**: `Monday`
- **Time**: `9:00 AM`
- **Timezone**: your business timezone in Business App
- **Start date**: today
- **End date**: `No end date`

## Configure Social Calendar Generation

1. Go to `AI` > `AI Workforce`.
2. Open your `Social Media Manager` AI Employee.
3. In the generation area, confirm the feature is listed as `Social Calendar Generation`.
4. Verify the model is set to `Gemini Flash Latest`.
5. Open the trigger schedule settings and review:
   - `On a Schedule`
   - `Weekly`
   - `Monday`
   - `9:00 AM`
   - Your business timezone
   - Start date set to today
   - `No end date`
6. Click `Save` if you make any adjustments.

## Platform availability

Social Calendar Generation is available in:

- Web
- Mobile

## Frequently Asked Questions

<details>
<summary>Can I change the weekly defaults?</summary>

Yes. You can update the schedule frequency, day, time, timezone, start date, and end date in the trigger settings.
</details>

<details>
<summary>Which timezone does the schedule use?</summary>

The schedule uses your business timezone configured in Business App.
</details>

<details>
<summary>Does the schedule end automatically?</summary>

No. The default uses `No end date`, so it keeps running until you change it.
</details>

<details>
<summary>Can I use Social Calendar Generation on mobile?</summary>

Yes. Social Calendar Generation is available on web and mobile.
</details>
