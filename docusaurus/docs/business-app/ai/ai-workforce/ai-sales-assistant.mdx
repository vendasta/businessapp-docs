## What is the AI Sales Assistant?

The **AI Sales Assistant** is an AI-powered sales companion that automatically captures every sales conversation, updates CRM records, and lets you interact with your CRM data through natural language. It works in the background to keep your CRM accurate without manual data entry, so your team can focus on selling.

The AI Sales Assistant is part of CRM AI and is available with a CRM AI Standard or Pro subscription. See [CRM AI editions](#crm-ai-editions) for details.

## What you can do with the AI Sales Assistant

### Auto-update CRM records

The AI Sales Assistant extracts outcomes, key details, and action items directly from sales activities and updates your CRM records automatically. This reduces manual data entry and ensures every interaction is logged accurately.

- Automatically extract meeting outcomes and update contact, company, and opportunity records
- Keep CRM data current without reps entering information manually
- Improve CRM hygiene by ensuring every interaction is captured

### Chat with your CRM data

Use a natural-language chat interface to get answers from your CRM instead of manually searching through records.

- Ask questions about contacts, companies, opportunities, and activity history
- Surface insights and trends across your pipeline
- Get quick answers without navigating through multiple CRM views

### Record and transcribe meetings

The AI Sales Assistant automatically records, transcribes, and logs meetings in your CRM.

- **Auto-join meetings** when integrated with Google Calendar
- **Record and transcribe** Google Meet and Microsoft Teams meetings
- **Log meeting summaries, transcripts, and video recordings** directly into your CRM
- **Automatically create CRM contacts** if no matching record exists
- **Join ad-hoc or client-hosted meetings** by manually inviting the notetaker
- **Access all past recorded meetings** in the CRM activity feed

## How to set up

### Core requirements

- Calendar integration
- Google Workspace connection
- CRM access

### Step 1: Calendar integration

There are two ways to connect your calendar:

**As an administrator**

1. Go to **Administration** > **Platform Integrations**.
2. Connect Google Workspace.

**As a salesperson**

1. Go to **CRM** > **My Meetings**.
2. Reconnect the **Book Me Now** integrations.

### Step 2: Optional customization

- The default AI employee settings work well without changes.
- Optionally, add your knowledge base for tailored meeting notes.

### Step 3: Adding to meetings not hosted by you

- Works with Google Meet and Microsoft Teams.
- Use the Atlas bar workflow to invite the notetaker.

## Finding recorded meetings

- Go to the **CRM activity feed** and select **Recorded Meetings** to see all sessions in one place.
- Internal meetings appear in the feed but are not linked to external contacts unless they are added to the CRM.

## FAQs

<details>
<summary>Does the AI Sales Assistant depend on Book Me Now?</summary>

No. Book Me Now is only used to read your calendar. The AI Sales Assistant works with any booking tool.
</details>

<details>
<summary>Can it join meetings not on my calendar?</summary>

By default, no. You can manually invite it to ad-hoc meetings.
</details>

<details>
<summary>Does it work for client-hosted meetings?</summary>

Yes, but you must manually add it.
</details>

<details>
<summary>Are internal meetings recorded?</summary>

Yes, but they are not linked to external contacts.
</details>

<details>
<summary>Who manages AI Sales Assistant settings?</summary>

Only administrators can configure it.
</details>

<details>
<summary>How do I rename my notetaker?</summary>

From the AI employee settings page. Changes apply account-wide and can be made once per hour.
</details>

<details>
<summary>Can I turn off compliance audio and chat?</summary>

Yes, from the AI employee settings page. Changes apply account-wide and can be made once per hour.
</details>

<details>
<summary>What is the difference between CRM AI Standard and Pro?</summary>

Standard includes the AI Sales Assistant with auto CRM updates, chat with CRM data, and custom objects. Pro adds conversation intelligence (meeting recording with AI summaries and action items), sales coaching with conversation scoring, and sales methodology frameworks. See the [editions table](#crm-ai-editions) for a full comparison.
</details>
