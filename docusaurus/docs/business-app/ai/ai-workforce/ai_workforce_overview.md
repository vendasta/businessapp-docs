# AI Workforce Overview

The AI Workforce in Business App allows you to leverage advanced AI agents to automate tasks and improve efficiency. These agents can handle various business processes, providing consistent and scalable support.

## Custom AI Employees

You can now create and manage **Custom AI Employees** tailored to your specific business needs. These specialized AI agents are designed to:
- Perform unique workflows specific to your industry.
- Integrate with your existing data and tools for more personalized interactions.
- Provide 24/7 coverage for specialized tasks like lead qualification, customer support, or data entry.

By utilizing Custom AI Employees, businesses can scale their operations without significantly increasing overhead, ensuring that every customer interaction or internal process is handled with precision and speed.
