# AI Workforce Overview

The AI Workforce in Business App allows businesses to leverage specialized AI agents to automate tasks, engage with customers, and streamline operations.

## What is the AI Workforce?

The AI Workforce consists of digital employees designed to perform specific roles within your business ecosystem. From handling customer inquiries to managing social media presence, these AI agents work alongside your human team to increase productivity.

## Key Benefits

- **24/7 Availability:** AI employees work around the clock.
- **Scalability:** Easily handle increased volume without adding headcount.
- **Consistency:** Ensure every interaction follows brand guidelines.

## Custom AI Employees

**New in Business App!** You can now create **Custom AI Employees** tailored to your specific business needs. 

Custom AI Employees allow you to:
- Define a unique persona and role for the agent.
- Provide custom knowledge bases for the AI to reference.
- Set specific instructions on how the agent should behave and interact with users.

To create a custom AI employee, navigate to the **AI Workforce** tab and select **Create Custom Employee**. Follow the prompts to define their skills, knowledge, and personality.

## Getting Started

To explore the AI Workforce, navigate to the AI section in your Business App dashboard.
