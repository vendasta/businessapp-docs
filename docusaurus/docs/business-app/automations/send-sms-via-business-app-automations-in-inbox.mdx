You can send SMS messages from automations so they flow through Conversations in Business App.

### How to use SMS via automations in Conversations

**Step 1** – Go to **Business App** → **Automations**.

**Step 2** – Open an automation or create a new one.

**Step 3** – Set the trigger to **When a Contact is created**.

**Step 4** – Add the SMS step.

![SMS automation step](./img/business-app-automations/send-sms-via-business-app.png)

SMS messages from this step are sent through your Conversations interface.
