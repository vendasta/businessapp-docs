# Automation templates in Business App

## What are automation templates?

Automation templates let you create automations from pre-built templates or from scratch. Templates help you save time and streamline workflows by providing ready-made triggers and actions.

## How do templates work?

**Step 1** — Go to **Business App** > **Automations**.

**Step 2** — Click **Create Automation**. You’ll see all available templates, or you can create a blank automation.
