## What financial data is included?

After you connect your QuickBooks Online account in Business App, your Executive Report can include financial data to help you track business performance.

### Financial metrics in the report

The Executive Report can show:

- **Income data** – Revenue and earnings
- **Expense data** – Business spending and costs
- **Other financial metrics** – Additional performance indicators from QuickBooks

![QuickBooks data in Executive Report](./img/quickbooks-executive-report.jpg)

## Invoice notifications

As well as the financial data in the report, you can get notifications about your QuickBooks Online invoices:

- **In-app notifications** – Alerts in Business App when invoices are paid
- **Email notifications** – Emails when QuickBooks Online invoices are paid in full

That way you can stay on top of cash flow and payments without constantly opening QuickBooks.

## How to connect QuickBooks Online

To see financial data in your Executive Report:

1. Go to **Business App** > **Administration** > **Connections**
2. Find **QuickBooks Online** in **Browse Integrations**
3. Complete the steps to link your QuickBooks Online account
4. Once connected, financial data will start appearing in your Executive Report

## Benefits of the QuickBooks integration

- **Reporting in one place** – See marketing and financial performance together
- **Automatic updates** – Financial data syncs to the Executive Report
- **Invoice alerts** – Notifications when invoices are paid
- **Share when needed** – Use the report to show financial progress to others
- **Full picture** – See how marketing ties into your financial results

Together, the Executive Report and QuickBooks give you a single view of business performance so you can make informed decisions about growth and where to invest.
