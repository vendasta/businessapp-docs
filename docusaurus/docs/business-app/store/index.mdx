# Store

The **Store** tab in Business App lets you browse and purchase products and packages, or express interest, directly from the app. When you open the Store, you'll see all the products and packages available to you.

## How to access the Store

Log in to Business App and click **Store**.

<img src={require('./img/business-app-store/store-tab.jpg').default} alt="Store tab in Business App" style={{width: '100%', border: '1px solid #e0e0e0', borderRadius: '8px', boxShadow: '0 2px 8px rgba(0,0,0,0.08)'}} />
</div>

## How to use the Store

Click any package to open its information page. There you can review marketing materials, product details, pricing, and more.

<div style={{marginBottom: '24px'}}>
<img src={require('./img/business-app-store/package-info.jpg').default} alt="Package information page" style={{width: '100%', border: '1px solid #e0e0e0', borderRadius: '8px', boxShadow: '0 2px 8px rgba(0,0,0,0.08)'}} />
</div>

1. **Package information** — Package name, tagline, and solution type.
2. **Pricing** — Price for the package and the purchase button (see below).
3. **Package description** — Overview of the package.

   <div style={{marginBottom: '24px'}}>
   <img src={require('./img/business-app-store/package-description.jpg').default} alt="Package description section" style={{width: '100%', border: '1px solid #e0e0e0', borderRadius: '8px', boxShadow: '0 2px 8px rgba(0,0,0,0.08)'}} />
   </div>

4. **Product selection** — Choose a product to view more information.
5. **Product description** — What the product does and how it helps.
6. **Gallery** — Media showing how the product works.

   <div style={{marginBottom: '24px'}}>
   <img src={require('./img/business-app-store/product-details.jpg').default} alt="Product details section" style={{width: '100%', border: '1px solid #e0e0e0', borderRadius: '8px', boxShadow: '0 2px 8px rgba(0,0,0,0.08)'}} />
   </div>

7. **Key selling points** — How the product can help you.
8. **FAQs** — Answers to common questions before you purchase.
9. **Files** — Case studies, walkthroughs, brochures, and similar materials.

### Taking action on a package

After browsing a package and reviewing its details, you'll see one of the following options depending on how the package is configured:

- **Add to Cart** — Add the package to your shopping cart and complete the purchase yourself.
- **Contact Sales** — Send an email to express interest and connect with a sales representative.
- **External link** — Visit an external website for more information or to sign up directly.

### Using the cart

You can access your cart at any time by clicking the <MdShoppingCart size="1em" style={{ verticalAlign: 'text-bottom' }} aria-label="Shopping cart icon" /> cart icon in the top-right corner of Business App. A dropdown shows the items in your cart, the price for each item, and the subtotal.

<div style={{marginBottom: '24px'}}>
<img src={require('./img/business-app-store/cart-dropdown.png').default} alt="Cart dropdown" style={{width: '100%', border: '1px solid #e0e0e0', borderRadius: '8px', boxShadow: '0 2px 8px rgba(0,0,0,0.08)'}} />
</div>

Click **View Cart** to open the full shopping cart page, where you can review your cart contents, adjust quantities, remove items, and proceed to checkout.

</div>
