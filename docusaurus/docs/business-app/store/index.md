# Store

The **Store** tab in Business App lets you browse and purchase products and packages, or express interest, directly from the app. When you open the Store, you’ll see all the products and packages available to you.

## How to access the Store

Log in to Business App and click **Store**.

![Store tab in Business App](./img/business-app-store/store-tab.jpg)

## How to use the Store

Click any package to open its information page. There you can see marketing materials, product details, pricing, and more.

![Package information page](./img/business-app-store/package-info.jpg)

1. **Package information** — Package name, tagline, and solution type.
2. **Pricing** — Price for the package and the purchase button (see below).
3. **Package description** — Overview of the package.

   ![Package description section](./img/business-app-store/package-description.jpg)

4. **Product selection** — Choose a product to view more information.
5. **Product description** — What the product does and how it helps.
6. **Gallery** — Media showing how the product works.

   ![Product details section](./img/business-app-store/product-details.jpg)

7. **Key selling points** — How the product can help you.
8. **FAQs** — Answers to common questions before you purchase.
9. **Files** — Case studies, walkthroughs, brochures, and similar materials.

When you’re ready to move forward, you can **Contact Sales** or **Add to the Cart**, depending on how the package is set up. A window will open so you can either email to express interest or complete the purchase yourself.

![Purchase/contact sales window](./img/business-app-store/purchase-window.jpg)

## Frequently Asked Questions

<details>
<summary>What happens when I click "Upgrade now"?</summary>

Clicking "Upgrade now" takes you through the standard Store purchase flow where you can review package details, pricing, and complete your purchase.
