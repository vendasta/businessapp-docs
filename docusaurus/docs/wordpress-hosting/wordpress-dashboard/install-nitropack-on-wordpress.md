Here is the step-by-step guide on how to install NitroPack on WordPress:

1.  Log in to your **WordPress admin** dashboard and go to **Plugins > Add New**.

    ![Add new plugin NitroPack](./img/29661864746903-25d14fbe3a.png)
2.  Search “**NitroPack by NitroPack LLC”** then click **Install Now** button.  
    
    ![NitroPack Review: Features, Pricing & More (2025)](./img/29661864746903-11f167c649.jpg)
    
3.  Go to **Plugins > Installed Plugins.**
4.  Click **Activate** link on NitroPack.
5.  Your NitroPack installation is complete!

### How to connect NitroPack to your WordPress

After successfully installing NitroPack, you need to connect it to your WordPress. Follow these steps:

1.  Create an account at [NitroPack](https://nitropack.io/) if you don’t have one yet.
2.  Log in to your account and go to the **“Connect Your Website”** menu on the left.
3.  Open your WordPress admin dashboard and go to **Plugins > Installed Plugins > NitroPack > Settings.**
4.  Click **“Connect to NitroPack”**.
5.  Now, you have connected your WordPress to NitroPack!

### NitroPack setup guide

After your WordPress is connected with NitroPack, you can start configuring which features to use in the NitroPack plugin. Follow these steps:

Go to the settings tab of NitroPack so you can see the **NitroPack dashboard**.

### NitroPack dashboard

There are 4 tabs in the NitroPack dashboard:

**Optimized Pages**

The tab on the top left shows **Optimized Pages**, where you can see how many pages have been optimized by NitroPack and which pages they are.

**Plan**

The tab on the top right shows your NitroPack **Plan** details.

**Settings**

The tab on the bottom left is **Settings**, where you can enable features like cache warmup and HTML compression. You can also disconnect the NitroPack plugin from your WordPress site.

**Configuration**

The tab on the bottom right is **Configuration**, where you can choose between 5 optimization options:

1.  **Standard**: Basic optimization without much regard for a high score.
2.  **Medium**: Well-balanced optimization suitable for most cases.
3.  **Strong**: Includes advanced features like automatic image lazy loading and font definition modification.
4.  **Ludicrous**: Enables all optimizations to achieve the highest possible score.
5.  **Manual**: Customize your own optimization settings. When activated, you'll see **Advanced settings** for custom configuration.
