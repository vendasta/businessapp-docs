The Advertisements section covers ad performance analysis, creative comparisons, and conversion tracking features in Advertising Intelligence.

## Why use Advertisements?

- Compare ad performance across different campaigns
- Analyze creative effectiveness side-by-side
- Track and customize LocalAds conversions
- Monitor form fills and lead generation
- Optimize campaigns based on performance data

## What's included

- **[Compare Ad Performance and Creatives](compare-and-contrast-ad-performance-and-creatives.mdx)**: Side-by-side comparison of ad performance and creative assets
- **[LocalAds Conversion Customization](localads-conversion-customization-and-form-fills.mdx)**: Customize conversion tracking and form fills for LocalAds

## Get started

1. Go to `Advertising Intelligence` > `Advertisements`.
2. Select campaigns to compare performance metrics.
3. Review creative assets and their performance.
4. Configure conversion tracking for LocalAds if applicable.

## Ad Performance Comparison

Compare campaigns to understand:
- Which creatives drive the most engagement
- Performance differences across platforms
- Cost efficiency of different ad variations
- Conversion rates by creative type

## LocalAds Conversion Tracking

Customize how conversions are tracked:
- Form fill tracking
- Custom conversion events
- Lead attribution
- Conversion value settings

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

<details>
<summary>How do I compare ad performance?</summary>

Use the Advertisements section to select multiple campaigns and view side-by-side performance metrics including clicks, impressions, conversions, and ROI.
</details>

<details>
<summary>What is LocalAds conversion customization?</summary>

LocalAds conversion customization lets you define what counts as a conversion, including form fills, phone calls, and custom events specific to your campaigns.
</details>

<details>
<summary>Can I see which creatives perform best?</summary>

Yes. The compare and contrast feature shows performance metrics for each creative, helping you identify top performers.
</details>
