Connect Microsoft Bing Ads with Advertising Intelligence to track ad spend and performance data.

## Prerequisites

Before connecting, ensure you have:

- An active Advertising Intelligence account.
- A Microsoft Bing Ads (Microsoft Advertising) account.

:::info Advanced Reporting
While connecting Microsoft Bing Ads without Advanced Reporting is possible, only account-level statistics will be reported.
:::

## Connecting Microsoft Bing Ads
![Settings > Connections navigation](img/settings-connections.jpg)

1. **Access Advertising Intelligence:** Open Advertising Intelligence and navigate to your account.

2. **Locate Connections Settings:** Navigate to **Settings** > **Connections** in your Advertising Intelligence account.
![Microsoft Advertising connection option](img/connection-option.jpg)

   ![Where to find Settings and Connections in Advertising Intelligence](./img/settings-connections.jpg)

3. **Connect to Microsoft Advertising:** Within the Connections settings, locate the **Microsoft Advertising** option and click **Connect**. Follow the prompts on Microsoft's authentication page to sign in with your Microsoft Bing Ads account.
![Connection process for Microsoft Bing Ads](img/connection-process.jpg)

   ![Microsoft Advertising connection option in Connections settings](./img/connection-option.jpg)

4. **Complete authentication:** After you grant the required permissions, the connection completes and campaign data begins syncing to your Advertising Intelligence dashboard.

   ![Connection process for Microsoft Bing Ads in Advertising Intelligence](./img/connection-process.jpg)

## Viewing Your Microsoft Bing Ads Data

Once connected, your Microsoft Bing Ads (Microsoft Advertising) data is available in Advertising Intelligence:

- **All** tab: Consolidated data for all your connected advertising platforms, including Microsoft Bing Ads, appears under the **All** tab.
- **Microsoft Advertising** tab: A dedicated tab shows your Microsoft Bing Ads campaign statistics.
- **Overview** and **Executive Report:** Your Microsoft Bing Ads data is included in the Overview section and in the Executive Report under the Advertising section.

![Microsoft Bing Ads campaign statistics view in Advertising Intelligence](./img/data-view-1.jpg)

![Microsoft Bing Ads campaign details view in Advertising Intelligence](./img/data-view-2.jpg)

## Disconnecting Microsoft Bing Ads

:::warning
Disconnecting will stop data from syncing. Reconnect later if you need to restore the integration.
:::

To disconnect Microsoft Bing Ads:

1. Navigate to **Settings** > **Connections** in Advertising Intelligence.
2. Find **Microsoft Advertising** in the list of connections.
3. Click **Disconnect** (or the equivalent control).
4. Confirm the disconnection when prompted.
![Advertising Intelligence data view 1](img/data-view-1.jpg)

![Advertising Intelligence data view 2](img/data-view-2.jpg)

## Troubleshooting

If you encounter issues with your Microsoft Bing Ads connection:

- Verify your Microsoft Bing Ads (Microsoft Advertising) account has active campaigns and ads.
- Check that you have the correct permissions in your Microsoft Advertising account.
- Try disconnecting and reconnecting the account in Advertising Intelligence.
- Clear your browser cache and cookies, then try the connection process again.
- Contact support if problems persist.

- [Microsoft Advertising Help](https://help.ads.microsoft.com/)
