---
title: Access AdVantage Merchant Center via SSO
sidebar_label: Merchant Center
description: Learn how to access AdVantage Merchant Center using single sign-on (SSO)
---

Use single sign-on to access AdVantage Merchant Center through the following steps:

1. In Advertising Intelligence, when ad campaigns are managed by a connected provider, you may see an additional side menu called `External Dashboard`.
2. Click that menu to open a list of campaigns.
3. Select individual ad campaigns to open Merchant Center.
